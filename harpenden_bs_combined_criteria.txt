# OVERVIEW

Harpenden Building Society is a specialist lender focusing on complex income and unusual property types.

**Key features:**

* Loans up to 6× income (subject to affordability)
* Interest Only lending up to £3.5m at 70% LTV (residential cases)
* No upper age limit (residential)
* Manual underwriting on all cases
* Up to four applicants permitted
* Lending available for:

  * Residential (Owner Occupied)
  * Lending In & Into Retirement
  * Second Homes
  * Holiday Lets
  * Buy to Let
  * Self-Build

---

# RESIDENTIAL MORTGAGES

## RESIDENTIAL – STANDARD

**Minimum loan:** £150,000

**Maximum loan:** £1,250,000

**Maximum LTV:** Up to 75% (Repayment / Interest Only / Part & Part)

**Maximum term:** 35 years

**Available for:**

* Purchase
* Remortgage
* Release of equity
* Owner-occupied primary residence

Up to four applicants may be considered.

---

## RESIDENTIAL – LARGE LOAN

**Minimum loan:** £1,250,001

**Maximum loan:** £3,500,000

**Maximum term:** 35 years

**Maximum LTV – smaller large-loan band:**

* Repayment: 85%
* Interest Only: 75%
* Part & Part: 75%

**Maximum LTV – larger band (up to £3.5m):**

* Repayment: 75%
* Interest Only: 75%
* Part & Part: 75%

---

## RESIDENTIAL – IN & INTO RETIREMENT

**Minimum loan:** £150,000

**Maximum loan:** £3,500,000

**Maximum term:** 35 years

**Maximum LTV:**

* Lending Into Retirement: 75%
* Lending In Retirement (already retired or >75 at start): 70%

**Applies where:**

* Any borrower is already retired; or
* Any borrower is over age 75 at the start of the mortgage

Property must be owner-occupied. Up to four applicants considered.

# BUY-TO-LET (BTL)

## BUY-TO-LET – STANDARD

**Minimum loan:** £150,000

**Maximum loan:** £2,000,000

**Maximum LTV:** Up to 75%

**Term:** 1–25 years

**Purpose:**

* Purchase
* Remortgage
* Release of equity
* Property held in personal names

**Tenancy requirements:**

* Assured Shorthold Tenancy
* Minimum 6 months
* Maximum 3 years

**Key criteria:**

* HMOs not accepted
* Minimum income £30,000 OR net assets £500,000
* No portfolio landlords (personal names)
* Max exposure: £2m or 5 properties (max 3 mortgaged)
* Limited appetite for new-build flats outside South East England

Rental cover assessed holistically.

## RESIDENTIAL – SECOND HOME

**Minimum loan:** £150,000

**Maximum LTV:** Up to 75%

**Maximum term:** 25 years

**Available for:**

---

## HOLIDAY LET

**Minimum loan:** £1,250,001

**Maximum loan:** £2,000,000

**Maximum LTV:** Up to 75% (Interest Only / Part & Part)

**Term:** 1–25 years

**Features:**

* Short-term letting
* 90 days personal usage permitted per annum
* Airbnb considered
* Properties above commercial units considered
* Top slicing permitted

---

## SELF-BUILD

**Minimum loan:** £150,000

**Maximum loan:** £2,000,000

**Maximum LTV:**

* Standard cases: Up to 75%
* Applicant retired or >75 at start: Up to 70%
* Professional project oversight may allow up to 75%

**Term:** 5–35 years

**Additional requirements:**

* Small developments only (maximum 3 properties)
* One property must be subsequent main residence
* Conversion/refurbishment projects considered
* Drawdowns against re-valuations
* Detailed specification required
* Full residential planning consent required
* Development appraisal required
* Society's solicitor to act

# INTEREST ONLY REPAYMENT STRATEGIES

## Sale of Owner-Occupied Property

* Minimum equity: £150,000
* Maximum LTV: 75%

---

## Sale of HBS Security Property (BTL / Second / Holiday)

* Maximum LTV: 75%

---

## Sale of Additional Property (Non-HBS)

* Maximum LTV: 75%
* Minimum equity on HBS security: £150,000
* Additional property LTV under 50%
* Equity exceeds £150,000

---

## Overpayments from Income & Bonuses

* Minimum equity: £150,000
* Maximum LTV: 75%

---

## Investment

* Equities: 70% of current value
* Cash ISA / Savings: 100% of current value
* Regular investments: 70% of projected value (12+ months history)

---

## Inheritance

* Probate granted
* Minimum equity: £150,000
* Maximum LTV: 75%

---

## Convert to Repayment

* Self-Build only
* Minimum equity: £150,000
* Maximum LTV: 75%

---

## Endowment

* With-profits: 75% of surrender value
* Unit-linked: 50% of surrender value
* Must be within 10 years of maturity
* Assignment required

---

## Pension Lump Sum

* Minimum age 55
* Minimum pension fund £500,000
* Maximum loan 75% of allowable lump sum

---

# INCOME

## Employed

* 3 months payslips + P60
* 2 most recent P60s for bonus/overtime

---

## Self-Employed

* 3 years tax calculations + HMRC overviews OR
* 3 years accounts OR
* Accounts certificate (within 18 months)

**Business plan required if:**

* Trading under 3 years
* Developing business
* Fluctuating results

---

## Contractor

* Usually 12 months contracting
* Minimum 3 months remaining
* Affordability based on 5 days / 46 weeks

---

## Lending Into Retirement

* Pension projections required
* Pension statements or P60s required

---

## Maintenance

* Court order OR
* 6 months bank statements + confirmation letter

---

## Rental Income

* 3 years tax calculations + HMRC overviews
* AST agreement

---

## Trust Income

* Legal document + 3 months bank statements

---

# AFFORDABILITY

Income multiples used as initial assessment only.

**Full affordability includes:**

* Net income after tax and NI
* Committed expenditure
* Essential expenditure
* Stress test rate assessment
* Future changes in income/expenditure

Up to four applicants considered.

---

# FINANCIAL STATEMENTS

## Current Bank Account

* Assets above £500k: 3 months statements
* Assets below £500k: 1 month statement

---

## Assets

Evidence required for:

* Savings
* Investments
* ISAs
* Shares
* Pension funds
* Business assets

---

# IMPAIRED CREDIT

## Level 1

* Defaults/CCJs up to £2,000
* Satisfied at least 12 months

---

## Level 2

* Defaults/CCJs up to £5,000
* Satisfied on application

---

## Arrears

* Max 3 missed payments in last 2 years
* No more than 1 loan
* Must be up to date at application

---

## Not Generally Accepted

* CCJs over £500 (past 3 years)
* Serious arrears (90+ days)
* IVA or Bankruptcy in last 3 years
* Repossession in last 6 years
* Active payday loan in last 12 months

---

# PROPERTY TYPE

**Acceptable:**

* Conventional construction (brick/tile/stone/slate)
* Flats (converted or purpose built)

**Not acceptable:**

* Concrete/steel-framed houses
* High-alumina cement properties
* Defective PRC houses
* Freehold studio/bedsit flats
* HMOs (BTL not accepted)
* Houseboats
* Any property deemed unsuitable by valuer

---

# TENURE

**Acceptable:**

* Freehold
* Leasehold (minimum 50 years remaining at end of term)
* Share of Freehold
