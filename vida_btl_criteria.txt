# VIDA HOME LOANS – BTL LENDING CRITERIA

---

**LENDER:** Vida Home Loans
**PRODUCT:** Buy to Let
**DOCUMENT TYPE:** BTL Lending Criteria
**SOURCE:** [Vida Intermediaries Lending Criteria](https://www.vidahomeloans.co.uk/intermediaries/lending-criteria)
**FOR INTERMEDIARY USE ONLY**

---

## AGE

Minimum age at application: 21.

Second applicant: 18 if direct family member, otherwise 21.

Maximum age at application: 79.

Mortgage must end before the oldest applicant’s 85th birthday.

---

## PROPERTY USE RESTRICTIONS

AirBnB and Holiday Lets: Not accepted.

Consumer Buy to Let: Not accepted.

Holiday Lets (tenancy type): Not accepted.

Regulated / Assured / Protected / Statutory tenancies: Not accepted.

---

## CREDIT – ADVERSE

Arrears, CCJs, Defaults and Unsecured Credit Arrears accepted subject to product tier and time elapsed:

Vida 36 – 36 months +
Vida 24 – 24 months +
Vida 6 – 6 months +
Packager – considered

Bankruptcy / IVA / DRO / Trust Deed:
Vida tiers – 3 years +
Packager – 1 year +

Repossessions:
Vida tiers – 6 years +
Packager – 3 years +

No secured arrears within last 6 months. Historic secured arrears must be up to date for at least 6 months.

Debt Management Plans: May be considered at underwriter discretion.

---

## LOAN PURPOSE

Remortgage allowed for legal purposes excluding:

Business finance
Speculative investments
Gambling debts
Time share purchase

Further Advances: Not offered.
Second Charges: Not offered.
Top Slicing: Not available. All BTL must be self-funding.

---

## LOAN SIZE & LTV

Minimum loan: £50,000
Maximum loan: £2,000,000

Maximum LTV: 85%

Product fees may be added provided total borrowing does not exceed 85% LTV. Adviser fees cannot be added.

---

## RENTAL CALCULATION (ICR)

ICR:

125% – SPVs or all Basic Rate taxpayers
145% – All Higher Rate taxpayers
135% – Mixed Basic and Higher Rate

Stress:

2-year fixed – initial rate + 2% or 5.5% (whichever higher)
5-year fixed – assessed at initial rate
Remortgage with no additional borrowing – initial rate

All BTL applications must be self-funding.

---

## REPAYMENT METHODS

Capital & Interest – available up to product max LTV.

Interest Only – available up to 85% LTV.

Sale of property acceptable as repayment vehicle.

Term: 5–45 years.
Leasehold minimum remaining term:
40 years (Capital & Interest)
70 years (Interest Only)

---

## DEPOSIT

Proof of deposit required unless from equity release.

Accepted sources include:

Family gifted deposits
Overseas deposits (case by case)
Builder incentives up to 5%
Director loans (SPV only, with accountant confirmation)

Government bounce back loan funds cannot be used as deposit.

---

## SPV LIMITED COMPANY LENDING

SPV must be formed solely for holding residential BTL assets.

Trading limited companies not permitted.

Acceptable SIC codes:

68100
68209
68320
68201

SPV must be registered in England, Wales or Scotland.

All directors and shareholders must provide joint and several guarantees.

SPV must not be a holding company or have subsidiaries.

---

## PORTFOLIO LANDLORDS

Defined as 4 or more mortgaged properties.

Maximum with Vida:

20 properties
£4m total borrowing

No restriction on properties with other lenders.

Average portfolio LTV ≤ 80%.
Minimum portfolio ICR 125%.

Portfolio schedule and business plan required.

---

## PROPERTY TYPES

Flats – acceptable within scheme limits.

Flats above commercial – 75% LTV, or 60% if above certain business types (restaurants, takeaways, etc).

HMOs – up to 8 bedrooms. Minimum valuation £100,000.

MUBs – up to 8 units on single freehold title.

Ex-LA flats – 80% LTV max; £200k minimum in Greater London, £80k elsewhere.

Barn conversions – subject to valuation.

Flying freehold – up to 25% of floor space.

Annex – must share services; cannot be separate HMO/MUB; single AST.

Land – up to 3 acres (case by case above).

Spray foam insulation – not acceptable.

Large Panel System (PRC) – not accepted.

---

## NEW BUILD

Accepted within scheme limits.

Must have suitable warranty (NHBC or equivalent approved list).

Incentives limited to 5% (UK Finance Disclosure Form).

Bulk purchase discount / Off plan not acceptable.

Offer validity:

Standard – 4 months
New Build – 6 months

Extensions subject to fresh checks.

---

## NATIONALITY & RESIDENCY

All applicants must provide 3 years address history, with latest 2 years continuous UK residency (not applicable to Expat range).

Applicants must be:

UK National
Settled / Pre-Settled status
Permanent right to reside

Visa holders not accepted for BTL.

---

## EXPAT RANGE

For UK nationals living abroad purchasing UK BTL.

EEA-based landlords – standard limits apply.
Non-EEA landlords – minimum property value £150,000.

---

## TENANCY TYPES

Accepted:

AST
PRT (Scotland)
Standard Occupation Contract (Wales)
Corporate lets
Student lets

DSS / Housing Benefit tenants acceptable.

Not accepted:

Holiday lets
Regulated BTL
Assured / Protected / Statutory tenants

---

## VALUATION

Mortgage valuation only.

Physical or remote valuation.

Minimum property values:

Standard BTL – £50k (≤70% LTV), £70k (>70%)
HMO – £100k
MUB – £125k (or £175k London/SE)
Ex-LA flats – £80k (£200k Greater London)

Valuer: Esurv.

Valuation appeals require 3 comparable sales within 6 months.

Valuation reports not released to customers or brokers.

---

## PRODUCT FEATURES

Non-refundable application fee: £195.

Overpayments up to 10% per annum without ERC.

Products are not portable.

Soft credit footprint at DIP stage.

---

## UNACCEPTABLE APPLICANTS

Power of Attorney
Diplomatic immunity
Criminal convictions (except minor traffic/spent convictions)
