## Overview

EquiFinance is a specialist second charge mortgage lender that collaborates with UK-wide distribution partners to offer lending products tailored to a diverse range of customer circumstances. We focus on assisting applicants who have experienced financial difficulties but are on the path to financial recovery. Additionally, we consider clients with unconventional employment situations, such as those new to their roles or recently self-employed.

### Key Features

* **No automated credit scoring** - We assess each applicant's circumstances individually, allowing for a flexible lending approach
* **Flexible underwriting** - We work closely with our panel of regulated Brokers to deliver good outcomes for our customers
* **Broker portal** - Makes it quick and easy for Intermediaries to obtain a decision in principle and to submit a full application
* **Specialist second charge mortgages** - Alternative to remortgaging that preserves the benefits of a low-rate first mortgage

---

## Product Overview

### Second Charge Mortgages

EquiFinance provides second charge mortgages that can serve as alternatives to remortgaging. These funds can be utilized for various purposes, including:

* **Debt Consolidation** - Combining multiple unsecured credit accounts, such as loans and credit cards, into a single payment
* **Home Improvements** - Financing extensions or refurbishments to enhance property value
* **Major Purchases** - Funding significant life events like weddings or purchasing a new car

### Advantages of Second Charge Mortgages

* Preserves the benefits of a low-rate first mortgage
* Avoids early redemption charges associated with remortgaging
* Potentially offers lower interest rates compared to unsecured credit
