## SECTION 2 – PRODUCT TYPE

- Product: second charge mortgages.
- Purpose: an alternative to remortgaging that preserves the benefits of a low-rate first mortgage.
- Lending available across the UK.

---

## SECTION 3 – PERMITTED USES OF FUNDS

- Debt consolidation: combining unsecured credit accounts such as loans and credit cards into a single payment.
- Home improvements: extensions or refurbishments.
- Major purchases: e.g. weddings, purchasing a new car.

---

## SECTION 4 – ADVANTAGES OF SECOND CHARGE MORTGAGES

- Preserves the benefits of a low-rate first mortgage.
- Avoids early redemption charges associated with remortgaging.
- May offer lower interest rates compared to unsecured credit.

---

## SECTION 5 – LENDING APPROACH

### Credit Assessment

- No automated credit scoring.
- Each applicant's circumstances assessed individually.
- Flexible underwriting approach.
- Customers with poor credit ratings are considered.
- Focus on applicants who have experienced financial difficulties but are on the path to financial recovery.

### Employment Considerations

- Applicants new to their roles are considered.
- Recently self-employed applicants are considered.
- Clients with unconventional employment situations are considered.
