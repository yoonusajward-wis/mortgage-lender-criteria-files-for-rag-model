## Refurb to Let

**Source:** https://www.keystonepropertyfinance.co.uk/broker/products-criteria/products?product=refurbtolet

### Overview

Refurb to Let provides short-term financing for landlords undertaking light refurbishment projects, with an option to transition to a longer-term fixed-rate product (Refurb Exit) upon completion.

### Key Criteria

**Loan Amounts:**
* **Minimum:** £200,000
* **Maximum:** £2,000,000

**Loan to Value (LTV):**
* **Rolled-up interest:** Up to 65% LTV
* **Serviced interest:** Up to 70% LTV

**Loan Term:**
* Maximum of **6 months**

**Interest Options:**
* Rolled up or serviced finance options available

**Property Types:**
* Standard properties
* Conversions from standard property to HMO - up to 6 occupants

**Refurbishment Costs:**
* Should not exceed 20% of the property value in most cases

**Fees:**
* **Procuration fee:** 1% for Refurb to Let
* **Procuration fee:** 0.55% for the Refurb Exit fixed rate
* **No exit fee or ERCs**

**Eligibility:**
* Available to experienced landlords

### Refurb Exit Product

**Loan Amounts:**
* **Minimum:** £200,000
* **Maximum:** £2,500,000

**Loan to Value (LTV):**
* **Standard properties:** Up to 80% LTV
* **Specialist properties:** Up to 75% LTV

**Fees:**
* **Arrangement Fee:** Applicable to all products; can be added to the loan or paid upfront
* **No application fee**
* **No revaluation fee**
* **Legal fees:** Payable via panel solicitor or client's solicitor

### Process

**STEP 1:** You can begin the Refurb Exit application as soon as the Refurb to Let application is fully submitted. Application processed and funds released, allowing refurb work to commence.

**STEP 2:** Submit your Refurb Exit application at least **90 days before the end of the Refurb to Let term**. Complete refurb works within 6 months.

**STEP 3:** Exit Refurb to Let product and go onto Refurb Exit fixed rate product with us or redeem the loan.

**Additional Features:**
* Title insurance used to expedite the legal process
* Can cover conversion of a standard property to an HMO - up to 6 occupants

## Product Transfer

**Source:** https://www.keystonepropertyfinance.co.uk/broker/products-criteria/products?product=producttransfer

### Overview

Product Transfer allows existing Keystone Property Finance customers to transfer to a new fixed rate product without remortgaging.

### Key Criteria

**Fees:**
* **No application fee**
* **Free valuation**
* **No legal fees**
* **Arrangement fees:** 1%, 3% & 5% arrangement fees available

**Loan to Value (LTV):**
* **Standard properties:** Up to 80% LTV
* **Specialist properties:** Up to 75% LTV
* **Ex-pat properties:** Up to 75% LTV
* **Holiday let properties:** Up to 75% LTV

**Procuration Fee:**
* Up to 0.45%

**Process:**
* If you are an existing broker, log into the MyKeystone portal where you will see any eligible applications displayed within your dashboard
* For new brokers, log into the MyKeystone portal, select the product transfer button and provide a reference number for the existing buy to let mortgage to then complete a product transfer request
