## SECTION 1 – LOAN PARAMETERS

- Minimum loan: £50,000.
- Maximum loan at up to 65% LTV: £1,500,000.
- Maximum loan at up to 75% LTV: £1,000,000.
- Maximum loan at up to 80% LTV: £500,000.
- Maximum LTV (standard): 80%.
- Maximum LTV (specialist): 80%.
- Maximum LTV (non-standard construction): 75%.
- Mortgage term: 5–25 years.
- Offer validity: 3 months.
- Maximum properties with UTB: 6 (or maximum UTB lending exposure of £2,500,000 per UTB borrower).
- Remortgage: property must have been owned for at least 3 months and registered in the borrower's name at Land Registry.
- Consumer BTL: not allowed. If the client already owns other rental property, acceptable. Contact UTB if the subject property is the client's only BTL.
- Overpayments: up to 10% of the outstanding balance can be repaid annually as a lump sum without penalty.

## SECTION 2 – APPLICANT ELIGIBILITY

- Minimum age: 21.
- At least one applicant must be under 85 at end of mortgage term.
- Maximum of 4 applicants.
- 20% of shareholders do not need to be named on the application (e.g. children).

### Income Requirements

- No minimum income requirement (except first-time buyer holiday lets — see Section 8).
- Primary applicant must evidence income via payslip, SA302, signed accounts, pension statement, employment reference, or accountant's certificate.
- Court-ordered spousal maintenance accepted.
- Applicant must be in employment for at least 3 months.

### Adverse Credit

- Active unsecured credit: all accounts must be up to date (mail order and communications accounts ignored).
- Mortgages and secured loans: no missed payments in last 12 months, nil outstanding arrears.
- CCJs and defaults: none in last 24 months if under £300 (mail order and communications accounts ignored).
- Minor adverse accepted where over 2 years old.

---

## SECTION 3 – PROOF REQUIREMENTS

### Identification

- All applicants complete biometric ID via UTB Secure Chat Hub (requires driver's licence, passport, or national ID card).
- Contact BTL team if biometric ID is unavailable.
- Non-EU nationals accepted with passport and indefinite right to reside.

### Residency

- 3-year UK address history required, usually evidenced via the Voters Roll.
- If incomplete, acceptable documents include: mortgage/loan statement, council tax bill, utility bill, bank/credit card statements, or proof of active credit.

---

## SECTION 4 – PROPERTY CRITERIA

### Houses

- Minimum value: £100,000.
- Maximum value: £5,000,000.

### Flats

- Minimum value: £125,000.
- Acceptable up to 30 floors.
- 4th floor or higher requires a lift.
- Minimum size: 30 sq.m.

### Lease Term

- Minimum 60 years remaining at completion.

### Block Exposure Limits

- Maximum of 10 units or 25% of a block (whichever is greater).
- Overall maximum: 15 units.
- Charge over freehold may be required where there is common or related ownership with the leasehold.

### Construction

- Non-standard construction accepted (maximum LTV 75%).
- Comfortable with close proximity to commercial property (e.g. above a chip shop).

### Holiday Lets

- Accepted where demand exists for short-term lettings.

### New Build

- Includes off-plan purchases and newly built properties for first occupation.
- If valuation is conducted pre-completion, a reinspection is required (fee payable).
- Must have an acceptable new-build warranty or consultant certificate, confirmed by solicitor.

**New build flat LTV limits:**
- Up to 70% LTV: acceptable.
- 70–75% LTV: underwriter review required.
- Above 75% LTV: not acceptable.

---

## SECTION 5 – MULTI-UNIT BLOCKS (MUB)

- At least one applicant must have 1 year's letting experience.
- Minimum valuation: £150,000.
- Up to 10 self-contained units accepted.
- Each unit must have separate utilities and a minimum size of 30 sq.m.

---

## SECTION 6 – HOUSES IN MULTIPLE OCCUPATION (HMO)

- At least one applicant must have 1 year's letting experience.
- Minimum valuation: £150,000.
- Up to 10 lettable rooms meeting minimum size regulations.
- HMO licence required where mandated by local authority; confirmed by solicitor.

---

## SECTION 7 – TENANCY TYPES

- Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST): 6–12 months, no subletting.
- Corporate lets: to recognised companies.
- Local authority or housing association lets: up to 5 years with break clause.
- Tenant restrictions: no vulnerable tenants or tenants under Power of Attorney.

---

## SECTION 8 – HOLIDAY LETS

- First-time landlords: must show combined income of at least £50,000 per annum.
- Agent's projections or 12 months' income history accepted.
- Maximum 6 bedrooms (standard); 7–8 bedrooms requires referral to underwriter; 9+ bedrooms not acceptable.
- Non-single dwelling properties: multi-unit or shared accommodation declared on AST basis, verified by valuer.

### Proof of Rental Income

- Remortgages: last 12 months via accounts, agency statements, or bank statements. If unavailable, agency letter accepted.
- Purchases: agency letter required.

### Agency Letter Requirements

Agency letters must be on headed paper and include:
- Corporate details.
- Expected rental income by season.
- Occupancy and void assumptions.
- Fees, commission and VAT status.

### Rental Income Calculation

- Use figures from agency letter (or weighted average).
- Convert weekly to monthly (× 4.3).
- Deduct fees and commission (including VAT).
- Apply occupancy rate: maximum 70% (equivalent to 36.4 weeks per year).
- If using last 12 months' income: divide net income by 12 and input into portal.

---

## SECTION 9 – ICR AFFORDABILITY

- Rental income confirmed by valuer or current rent (whichever is lower).
- If valuer rent is lower: accept valuer rent + 10%, capped at current rent with AST or bank statement evidence.

### Rental Cover Ratios

- Basic rate taxpayer: 125%.
- Higher rate taxpayer: 140%.
- Mixed basic/higher rate: 130%.
- Limited companies: 125%.

### Stress Rates

- 2-year fixed: higher of (initial pay rate + 2%) or 5.5%.
- 5-year fixed: initial pay rate.

### Capital Repayment Mortgages

- Capital repayment can be applied for on the same ICR basis as interest-only mortgages.
- The actual mortgage payment must be covered by assessed rental income.

---

## SECTION 10 – PORTFOLIO LANDLORDS

- Applies to any individual or limited company (SPV) applicant who owns other BTL properties.
- Borrowers with 4 or more properties must provide the latest 3 months' bank statements.
- Portfolio must be entered into the portal or BTL Hub.
- Maximum 10 properties with UTB or elsewhere.
- Background portfolio ICR: must be at least 125% at a mortgage rate of 5%.
- UTB may require additional assurance if portfolios are highly geared.
- Unlimited portfolio size for landlords overall (no restriction on properties held with other lenders).

---

## SECTION 11 – LENDING TO LIMITED COMPANIES

- Single-layer SPV companies for residential BTL only; trading companies not permitted.
- Acceptable SIC codes: 68100, 68209, 68320, 68201.
- Up to 4 directors and shareholders; must include all directors and at least 80% of shareholders.
- Gifted deposits and equity from close family members only.
- All directors and shareholders must provide joint and several personal guarantees and take independent legal advice.
- Where the property is already in an applicant's name, at least one owner must be a shareholder in the limited company.

---

## SECTION 12 – DEPOSIT CRITERIA

- Gifted deposits accepted from close relatives (parents, step-parents, children, grandparents, siblings, spouse, grandchildren).
- Overseas deposits considered by referral (FATF and EEA countries only).
- Concessionary purchase allowed if buying from a close relative; market value is used for purchase price and LTV calculation.
