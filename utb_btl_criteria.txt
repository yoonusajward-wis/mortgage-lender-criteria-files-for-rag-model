- At least one applicant must be under 85 years old at the end of the mortgage term
- 20% of shareholders do not have to be named on the application ie: children
- Lending available to 80% LTV + fees
- No minimum income requirements (Except if first time buyer holiday lets)
- Up to four applicants on an application
- Remortgage available from 3 months ownership at market value
- Rental agreements over £100,000 per year considered (subject to referral)

- Investor dominated developments subject to referral being accepted
- MUB and HMOs up to 10 units / 10 beds
- Minor adverse accepted (over 2 years old)
- New build properties/off plan purchase accepted
- Unlimited portfolio size for landlords
- Up to 6 properties can be mortgaged with UTB or max UTB lending exposure of £2.5m per UTB borrower

# Buy to Let Mortgages - Detailed Criteria Guide

## Loan Parameters

* **Maximum LTV**:

  * 80% (Standard)
  * 80% (Specialist)
  * 75% (Non-Standard)

* **Minimum Loan**: £50,000

* **Maximum Loan**:

  * £1.5m up to 65% LTV
  * £1.0m up to 75% LTV
  * £500,000 up to 80% LTV

* **Maximum with UTB**: Up to 6 properties can be mortgaged with UTB or max UTB lending exposure of £2.5m per UTB borrower (updated per latest USP sheet - September 2025).

* **Term**: 5–25 years

* **Remortgage**: Property must have been owned for at least 3 months and registered in the borrower’s name at Land Registry.

* **Offer Validity**: 3 months

* **Consumer BTL**: Not allowed. If the client is already a landlord with other rental property, it is acceptable. Contact UTB if the subject property is the client’s only BTL.

---

## Portfolio Landlords

* **Applicants**:
  Any individual applicant or Ltd Company (SPV) applicant who owns other BTL properties.

  * Borrowers with 4+ properties must provide the latest 3 months’ bank statements.

* **Portfolio Requirements**:

  * Portfolio must be entered into the portal or BTL Hub.
  * Maximum 10 properties with UTB or elsewhere.

* **Background Portfolio ICR**:

  * Must be at least 125% at a mortgage rate of 5%.
  * UTB may require additional assurance if portfolios are highly geared.

---

## Capital Repayments

* **Mortgage Repayment**: Capital repayment can be applied for on the same ICR basis as interest-only mortgages. The actual mortgage payment must be covered by assessed rental income.

* **Overpayments**: Up to 10% of the outstanding balance can be repaid annually as a lump sum without penalty.

---

## Property Criteria

* **Values**:

  * Houses: Minimum £100,000, maximum £5m.
  * Flats: Minimum £125,000.

* **Flats**:

  * Acceptable up to 30 floors.
  * 4th floor or higher requires a lift.
  * Minimum size: 30 sq.m.

* **Lease Term**: Minimum 60 years remaining at completion.

* **Exposure Limits**:

  * Max of 10 units or 25% of a block (whichever is greater).
  * Overall maximum: 15 units.
  * Charge over freehold may be required where there is common/related ownership with the leasehold.

* **Holiday Lets**: Accepted where demand exists for short-term lettings.

---

## Multi-Unit Blocks (MUB)

* **Applicants**: At least one applicant must have 1 year’s letting experience.
* **Minimum Valuation**: £150,000.
* **Units**: Up to 10 self-contained units, each with separate utilities and a minimum size of 30 sq.m.

---

## Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMO)

* **Applicants**: At least one applicant must have 1 year’s letting experience.
* **Minimum Valuation**: £150,000.
* **Number of Rooms**: Up to 10 lettable rooms meeting minimum size regulations.
* **Licence**: Required where mandated by local authority; confirmed by solicitor.

---

## New Build

* **Definition**: Includes off-plan purchases and newly built properties for first occupation.
* **Valuations**: If conducted pre-completion, reinspection is required (fee payable).
* **Warranty**: Must have an acceptable new-build warranty or consultant certificate, confirmed by solicitor.
* **Flats LTV Limits**:

  * Up to 70% LTV: acceptable.
  * 70–75% LTV: underwriter review.
  * Above 75% LTV: not acceptable.

---

## Deposit Criteria

* **Gifted Deposits**: Accepted from close relatives (parents, step-parents, children, grandparents, siblings, spouse, grandchildren).
* **Overseas Deposits**: Considered by referral (FATF and EEA only).
* **Concessionary Purchase**: Allowed if buying from a close relative; market value is used for purchase price and LTV.

---

## Tenancy Types

* **AST**: 6–12 months, no subletting.
* **Corporate Lets**: To recognised companies.
* **Local Authorities/Housing Associations**: Up to 5 years with break clause.
* **Tenant Restrictions**: No vulnerable tenants or tenants under POA.

---

## Applicant Criteria

* **Age**: Minimum 21. At least one applicant must be under 85 at end of term.

* **Number of Applicants**: Maximum of 4.

* **Adverse Credit**:

  * Active unsecured credit: all accounts must be up to date (mail order/communications ignored).
  * Mortgages/Secured loans: no missed payments in last 12 months, nil outstanding.
  * CCJs/Defaults: none in last 24 months if under £300 (mail order/communications ignored).

* **Income Requirements**: No minimum. However, primary applicant must evidence income via payslip, SA302, signed accounts, pension statement, employment reference, or accountant’s certificate. Court-ordered spousal maintenance accepted. Must be in employment for at least 3 months.

---

## Proof Requirements

* **Identification**: All applicants complete biometric ID via UTB Secure Chat Hub (requires driver’s licence, passport, or national ID card). Contact BTL team if unavailable. Non-EU nationals accepted with passport and indefinite right to reside.

* **Residency**: 3-year UK address history required, usually via Voters Roll. If incomplete, evidence with documents (mortgage/loan statement, council tax bill, utility bill, bank/credit card statements, or proof of active credit).

---

## ICR Affordability

* **Basis**: Rental income, confirmed by valuer or current rent (if lower).

* **If Valuer Rent Lower**: Accept valuer rent + 10%, capped at current rent with AST or bank statement evidence.

* **Holiday Lets**: Special rules apply (see Holiday Lets section).

* **Rental Cover Ratios**:

  * Basic Taxpayer: 125%
  * Higher Taxpayer: 140%
  * Mixed Basic/Higher: 130%
  * Ltd Companies: 125%

* **Stress Rates**:

  * 2-year fixed: Higher of initial pay rate + 2% or 5.5%
  * 5-year fixed: Initial pay rate

---

## Lending to Limited Companies

* **Definition**: Single-layer SPV companies for residential BTL. Trading companies not permitted.
* **Directors/Shareholders**: Up to 4 individuals; must include all directors and at least 80% of shareholders.
* **Acceptable SIC Codes**: 68100, 68209, 68320, 68201.
* **Deposits**: Gifted deposits/equity from close family members only.
* **Personal Guarantees**: All directors/shareholders must provide joint and several guarantees and take independent legal advice.
* **Connected Applicants**: Where property is already in an applicant’s name, at least one owner must be a shareholder in the Ltd Co.

---

## Holiday Lets

* **First-Time Landlords**: Must show combined income of at least £50,000 p.a.

* **Proof of Rental Income**:

  * **Remortgages**: Last 12 months via accounts, agency statements, or bank statements. If unavailable, agency letter.
  * **Purchases**: Agency letter required.

* **Agency Letters**: Must be on letterhead, with corporate details, expected rental income by season, occupancy/void assumptions, fees/commission/VAT status.

* **Rental Income Calculation**:

  * Use figures from agency letter (or weighted average).
  * Convert weekly to monthly (×4.3).
  * Deduct fees/commission (including VAT).
  * Apply occupancy rate (max 70% = 36.4 weeks/year).

* **If Using Last 12 Months’ Income**: Divide net income by 12 and input into portal.

* **Non-Single Dwelling Properties**: Multi-unit/shared accommodation declared on AST basis, verified by valuer.

* **Property Size**:

  * Max 6 bedrooms standard.
  * 7–8 bedrooms: referral to underwriter.
  * 9+ bedrooms: not acceptable.

For single dwelling buy-to-let properties, the valuation fees are based on property value:

* Up to £200,000: £235
* Up to £300,000: £270
* Up to £350,000: £295
* Up to £400,000: £300
* Up to £500,000: £380
* Up to £600,000: £515
* Up to £700,000: £630
* Up to £800,000: £710
* Up to £900,000: £790
* Up to £1 million: £850
* Up to £1.2 million: £1,100
* Up to £1.4 million: £1,200
* Up to £1.6 million: £1,350
* Over £1.6 million: Fees available upon request

---

### BTL HMO / MUB (Large) – up to 10 lettable rooms or units

For larger HMOs or multi-unit blocks with up to 10 lettable rooms or units, fees are available **upon application**. Intermediaries must contact UTB with property details to obtain the specific fee.

---

### BTL HMO / MUB (Large) – Re-inspection and Re-type Fees

For large HMO/MUB cases, additional charges apply depending on estimated value or purchase price:

* Up to £250,000: £120 (re-inspection), £70 (re-type)
* Up to £500,000: £175 (re-inspection), £70 (re-type)
* Up to £1,000,000: £250 (re-inspection), £70 (re-type)
* Over £1 million: Fees available only upon referral.
