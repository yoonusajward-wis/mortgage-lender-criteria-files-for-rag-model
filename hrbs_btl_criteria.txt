## SECTION 1 – PRODUCT OVERVIEW

- Buy to Let mortgages available for property investors.
- All cases reviewed manually with individual underwriting assessment.
- Portfolio landlord support available.
- Top-slicing available for non-portfolio landlords (not available on Visa BTL).
- Visa Buy to Let products available for eligible skilled worker visa holders.

---

## SECTION 2 – PORTFOLIO LANDLORD DEFINITION

An applicant is classed as a portfolio landlord where they have 4 or more qualifying buy to let mortgaged properties.

**Qualifying properties include:**
- Properties owned in the name of a limited company where the applicant holds more than 25% of the shares.
- Properties held on a consent to let basis.
- Holiday lets.

**Joint applications:**
- If the combined qualifying properties of all applicants exceed 3, the application is classed as portfolio lending.

---

## SECTION 3 – PORTFOLIO LANDLORD CRITERIA

- ICR requirement: the subject property and entire portfolio must meet a minimum 145% ICR, calculated at a reference rate of 5.5%.
- Maximum number of BTL properties acquired in the last 12 months: 3.
- Landlord experience: at least 1 applicant must have a minimum of 24 months' experience as a landlord.
- Maximum LTV: 70% (includes subject property and any unencumbered properties).
- Maximum age: applicant must not exceed age 80 at end of mortgage term.
- Top-slicing: not available for portfolio landlords.
- Each case assessed individually by the portfolio underwriting team.
- Consideration given to over-reliance on properties with EPC ratings below minimum regulatory requirements.

---

## SECTION 4 – PORTFOLIO APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

For each mortgaged and unencumbered property in the portfolio, the following must be provided:

- Account holder names.
- Property address.
- Rental income.
- Monthly mortgage payment (if any).
- Current lender and account number.
- Outstanding balance (if any).
- Mortgage repayment basis.
- Current property value.
- EPC rating.

---

## SECTION 5 – TOP-SLICING

- Available on all BTL products except Visa BTL.
- Not available for portfolio landlords.
- Can be considered on Joint Borrower Sole Proprietor (JBSP) mortgages.
- Minimum ICR for top-slicing: 100%.

**How it works:**
- Rental income must meet a minimum 100% ICR.
- Where rental income does not meet the stressed ICR, the shortfall must be coverable by the borrower's personal income.
- A combination of rental and personal income may be considered.

---

## SECTION 6 – VISA BUY TO LET

- Limited companies: not acceptable.
- Minimum income: £35,000.
- Maximum LTV: 75%.
- Property must meet minimum ICR requirements.
- Top-slicing: not permitted.
- Applicants who do not own a UK residential property must confirm residency status as part of the application.

**Accepted visas:**
- Skilled Worker.
- Health and Care.
- Global Talent.
- Ancestry.

If the visa type is not listed above, contact HRBS to discuss the application.
