## 1) Age & Income

* **Earned income (manual employment):** acceptable up to age **70**.
* **Earned income (non-manual employment):** acceptable up to age **75**.
* **Self-employed income:** acceptable up to age **75**.
* **Using pension assets:** from age **55**, pension assets can be used in the background.

## 2) Applicants

* **Standard application:** up to **2 incomes** accepted.
* **Joint Borrower Sole Proprietor (JBSP), UK applications:** up to **4 incomes** accepted from a maximum of **2 households**.
* **Expat JBSP:** maximum **2 incomes**.

## 3) Arrangements (Historic Arrangements / Adverse)

* **Debt Management / similar “arrangements”:**
* Maximum **75% LTV**.
* Must have been **satisfied for 3 years**.

## 4) Arrears

* **Historic arrears:** can be considered.
* **Requirement:** **no arrears in the last 3 years**.

## 5) Background Residential Mortgages

* **Affordability:** include **monthly payment and running costs**.

## 6) Benefits (as Income)

* **Acceptable at 100%:**
* **Child Benefit**\*, **Child Tax Credits**\*, **Working Tax Credits**\*.
* *Must be payable for the **next 5 years** minimum.*
* **Acceptable at 50%:**
* **Attendance Allowance**\*, **Constant Attendance Allowance**\*, **Disability Living Allowance (applicants only)**,
**Employment & Support Allowance**, **Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit**, **Personal Independence Payment (PIP)**,
**Universal Credit**.
* *Must be payable **for life**.*

## 7) Builder / Vendor Deposits

* **Maximum builder/vendor deposit:** **5%**.
* **Applicant must also contribute:** **5%** deposit.

## 8) CCJs

* **Ignored:** CCJs **satisfied for more than 12 months**.
* **Refer to Business Development Team (BDM):**
* **More than 1 CCJ**, or
* CCJs **over £1,000** up to **80% LTV**, and **over £500** **above 80% LTV**.

## 9) CIFAS

* Applicants **on the CIFAS register** can be considered — **refer cases**.

## 10) Close to Commercial Premises

* **Potentially acceptable** for properties **above/next to/near commercial**—**contact BDM** with details.

## 11) Concessionary Purchase (Transaction Under Value)

* Treated as **purchase under value**.
* **Deposit** required in line with product (e.g., **90% LTV** scenario still needs the balance deposit).

## 12) Contractors

* **Track record:** **12 months** in the **same line of work**.
* If **< 3 months** remaining on current contract → need **2 years’ contract history**.
* **Income calculation:** **Day rate × 5 days × 46 weeks** per annum.
* **Minimum income:** **No minimum** for contractors.
* **CIS contractors:** treated as **self-employed** → **tax returns/accounts required**.
* **Umbrella companies:** calculated **the same as basic contractors**.

## 13) Credit Agency

* **Agency used:** **TransUnion**.

## 14) Credit Score

* **No credit scoring** is used.

## 15) Criminal Convictions

* **Must be spent**, **not monetary-related** (e.g., **no fraud, money laundering**).
* Applicant must be able to obtain **home insurance on standard terms**.

## 16) Debt Management Plans

* **Must be satisfied for 3 years**.
* **Maximum LTV:** **75%**.

## 17) Debt Relief Order (DRO)

* **Up to 75% LTV**.
* Must have been **satisfied 3 years**.

## 18) Debt to Income Ratio (DTI)

* **No formal DTI**.
* **Recommend referral to BDM** where **debts outweigh income**.

## 19) Debts Being Repaid

* **Ignored** where the **debt is being repaid**.

## 20) Debts with Less than 6 Months Remaining

* **Ignored** if **< 6 months** to run.

## 21) Deductions (Treatment in Affordability)

* **Ignored (do not deduct):** additional **voluntary pension contributions**, **charitable donations**, **company pension payments**, **sharesave scheme**.
* **Deducted:** **childcare vouchers**, **maintenance payments**, **school fees**, **private healthcare**, **student loans**.

## 22) Defaults

* **Ignored:** **satisfied** defaults **> 12 months** ago.
* **Refer to helpdesk:**
* **More than 1 default**, or
* Defaults **over £1,000** up to **80% LTV**, and **over £500** **above 80% LTV**.

## 23) Deposits – Accepted Sources & Rules

* **Forces Help to Buy:** **accepted**.
* **Gifted deposit:** **accepted up to 100%**\*
* *Gift must be from **close family**: parent, grandparent, son/daughter, brother/sister.*
* *If **only 5%** is gifted, client must **add 5%** themselves **or** have **12 months’ rental history**.*
* *Gifted deposits **not acceptable** on **Expat** or **Foreign National** applications.*
* **Sale of property** or **capital raise** against a property owned in the applicant’s **limited company**: **Accepted**\*
* *Ltd Co must be an **SPV**.*
* *All applicants must be **named directors and shareholders**.*
* *Deposit must be from **drawings**, **not** a director’s loan.*
* **Loan repayable on sale of property** (divorce cases): **acceptable** — **contact BDM**.
* **Raising funds on another property:** **acceptable** (included in affordability).

## 24) Employed Income (Treatment & Age Limits)

* **Manual roles:** income taken to **age 70** or **retirement**, whichever is earlier.
* **Non-manual roles:** income taken to **age 75** or **retirement**, whichever is earlier.
* **50% accepted** for:
* Overtime, Commission, Bonus.
* **Second job** (with **6 months’ track record**).
* **Earned income (manual)** up to age rules above (if guaranteed → see note).
* **100% accepted** for **allowances**:
* Employer’s **housing allowance**, **mortgage subsidy**, **large town/London weighting**, **car allowance**.
* **Shift allowance** and **bank income** accepted at **100%** if **18-month track record**.
* **If guaranteed, 100% can be taken** (applies to the income types noted).
* **Employed by family:** acceptable; **income must be verified by a qualified accountant**.
* **Maternity/Parental leave:** acceptable if employer **confirms return date and salary in writing**.

## 25) EU / EEA / Swiss Nationals with Pre-Settled Status (No ILR)

* **Maximum LTV:** **80%**.
* **Evidence:** successful **pre-settled status**.
* **UK residence:** **12 months** minimum.
* **Work permit/visa:** **12 months remaining**.
* **Permitted sectors:** Education, Healthcare, Finance & Banking, IT, Legal, Marketing Professionals, Science R\&D, Energy Sector, **Construction Industry Professionals (not trades)** e.g., Architects.

## 26) Expats

* **Residential LTV:** up to **90%**.
* **Residential and regulated BTL:** **accepted**.
* **Acceptable currencies:** GBP, EUR, CHF, NOK, USD, CAD, SGD, HKD, AED, KWD, QAR, AUD, NZD, DKK, SEK, SAR.
* **Foreign currency income treatment:** convert to GBP and apply a **20% haircut** before affordability (then deduct tax/NI).
* **Minimum income:** **£40,000** (or equivalent).
* A **non-UK national** can be included if the case fits policy and affordability **using the UK national’s income and details**.
* **Not acceptable:** sanctioned or war-torn/in-conflict countries; **self-employed Expats**.
* **Returning Expats:** can be accepted — **refer to BDM**.

## 27) Fees

* **Arrangement fees / CHAPS fees:** **can be added** or **paid upfront**.
* **Fees may be added above LTV limits**.

## 28) Fixed-Term Contracts

* **Accepted.**
* Need **2 years’ history** in the same line of contract work **and** **≥ 3 months** remaining on current contract.
* If **< 2 years** contracting history → current contract must have **≥ 12 months** remaining.

## 29) Flats (England & Wales)

* **New build flats:** up to **90% LTV**.
* **Standard flats:** up to **95% LTV**.
* **No management company:** acceptable if **≤ 4 units** and all flats **hold a share of freehold**.
* **Lease terms:** **≥ 85 years** remaining at start; **new build flats: 125-year** lease required.
* **Ground rent:** ≤ **£250** (≤ **£1,000** if in London).
* **Ex-local authority flats:** **Right to Buy only**.
* **Storeys:** max **10**; **no lift** → max **5** storeys.
* **Absentee freeholder:** **not accepted**.
* **Basement flats:** **refer to BDM**.
* **Above commercial:** **accepted** subject to type/location — **referral recommended**.

## 30) Foreign Currency Income (General Rule)

* **Accepted** as income type; convert to GBP and apply **20% haircut**, then standard affordability.
* **Currencies permitted (residential/regulated BTL):** GBP, EUR, CHF, NOK, USD, CAD, SGD, HKD, AED, KWD, QAR, AUD, NZD, DKK, SEK, SAR.
* If applicant is an **Expat** with foreign income → **Expat product** applies.

## 31) Foreign Nationals with ILR

* **Accepted on standard criteria.**

## 32) Foreign Nationals without ILR

* **Maximum LTV:** **80%**.
* **Pre-settled status:** must evidence successful application.
* **UK residence:** **12 months** minimum.
* **Visa/work permit:** **12 months** remaining.
* **Permitted sectors:** same list as in §25.

## 33) General Property / Geography Notes

* **Lending area:** **England and Wales**.
* **Section 106:** can consider — dependent on wording and LTV; **contact BDM**.
* **Age-restricted properties:** **not accepted**.
* **Ex-local authority houses:** **accepted**.
* **Ex-local authority flats:** **not accepted** (unless **Right to Buy**).
* **Annexes:** accepted if **no separate utilities** (separate council tax allowed); up to **80% LTV**; **not** acceptable on **BTL**.
* **Annex used as short-term holiday let:** acceptable (no formal AST).
* **Leasehold houses:** **new build** minimum **250 years**; **non-new build** minimum **85 years**.
* **Wimpey No-Fines:** by referral, considered up to **80% LTV** case-by-case.

## 34) Help to Buy

* **Not accepted**.

## 35) Income Multiples / LTI

* **Standard:** **4.49×** (single or joint).
* **Enhanced to 5.49×** (single or joint) if **one applicant earns > £75,000**.
* **Enhanced to 5.49×** also where **12 months’ rent** can evidence payments **at/above** or **within 10% of** the new mortgage payment.

## 36) Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVA)

* **Up to 75% LTV**.
* Must have been **satisfied 3 years**.

## 37) Interest-Only

* **Accepted.**
* **Repayment vehicles (acceptable):** **Endowment/ISA**, **pension lump sum**, **sale of security/downsizing**\*, **sale of background property**, **other investments**.
* *Downsizing:* require **≥ £250,000 equity on day 1**, and **max 70% LTV** on the IO element (can **top up to 80%** with repayment, i.e., **part & part**).
* **Unacceptable vehicle:** **annual lump sum repayments**.
* **LTV caps:** **70%** if standard/going into retirement (may be **topped up** with **10% repayment** to **80%**).
* **Minimum income:** **no minimum** as standard.

## 38) Investment Income

* **Use 75%** of investment income.

## 39) JBSP (Joint Borrower Sole Proprietor)

* **Accepted** (UK and Expat).
* **UK:** max **4 applicants / 4 incomes**.
* **Expat:** max **2 applicants / 2 incomes**.
* **Residential LTV:** up to **90%**, with restrictions:
* **70%** max if **all applicants retired**.
* **80%** max if **term takes youngest into retirement**.
* **90%** max if **term does not** take the youngest into retirement.
* If **>70% LTV**, **max age** of any applicant at end of term is **90**.
* **Relationship:** joint borrower(s) should be **close family relative(s)**.
* **Deposit:** on UK/Expat JBSP, **any applicant** may provide the deposit (including non-proprietor).
* If **non-proprietor gifts** the deposit, they **cannot reside** at the property.
* **Fee-assisted legals:** **not available** for JBSP.

## 40) Lending Areas / Products

* **Residential UK:** accepted.
* **Residential Expat:** accepted.
* **Self Build:** accepted.
* **Shared Ownership:** **temporarily withdrawn**.

## 41) Length of Time Property Owned

* Must have been **owned for 6 months** before **remortgage/additional borrowing**.

## 42) Let-to-Buy

* **Accepted.**

## 43) Lodgers

* **Accepted** (no AST; no independent utilities; **max 1 lodger**).
* **Lodger income:** **not used** for affordability.

## 44) Maintenance (as Income)

* **100%** accepted.
* Must be **court-ordered** **or** show **12 months’ receipt history**.

## 45) Married Applicants Applying in Sole Names

* **Accepted** where partner will also reside.
* **Not available** if partner is omitted **due to adverse credit**.

## 46) Maximum Age

* **No maximum age** at application or at term end.
* **Applicants 75+ at application** must obtain **Independent Legal Advice (ILA)**.

## 47) Maximum Loan

* **Per individual product** (see product sheets).

## 48) Minimum Age / Income / Loan

* **Minimum age:** **18** at application.
* **Minimum income:** **none** on standard; **£20,000** if **already in retirement** (single or joint).
* **Minimum loan:** **£75,000**.

## 49) Missed Payments

* **No missed payments** in the **last 12 months**.

## 50) New Build

* **Flats:** up to **90% LTV** (England & Wales).
* **Houses:** up to **95% LTV**.
* **Acceptable warranties:** NHBC, Premier, Building Life Plans, Build-Zone (incl. BuildCare), Checkmate (Castle 10), ICW, LABC, and **Professional Consultants Certificate** meeting the listed professional bodies (RICS FRICS/MRICS, IStructE F/M, CIOB F/M, A/SI F/M, ABE F/M, CIAT, ARB-registered architect, ICE F/M).
* **PI insurance:** conveyancer must obtain proof; cover **≥ 6 years**.

## 51) New Employment

* If **< 6 months** in current job → must have **12 months’ continuous employment** history (else **contact BDM**).
* Can accept **new salary** if employer confirms and **≥ 1 payslip** can be provided **prior to offer**.

## 52) Non-Dependent Applicants

* **Ignored for affordability** if they have **own income to self-support**.
* **Committed debts** will still be included.
* **Personal borrowing for self-employed business** ignored if **not in client’s sole name**.

## 53) Offers

* **Valid for 6 months**.
* **Extensions** possible; may require **no-change confirmation** or updated affordability — **contact BDM**.

## 54) Other Property / Security Points

* **Splitting of title on completion:** considered.
* **EWS1:** required where applicable; **no combustible cladding/materials** on balconies without EWS1. **A1, A2, B1** acceptable (subject to valuer).
* **Max storeys:** **10**.
* **Overage clause:** can be accepted — **contact BDM**.
* **Solar panels:** accepted; if **leased**, must include **lender repossession break clause**.
* **Unadopted roads:** require **full access and maintenance** agreement.
* **Flying freehold:** max **25%**, subject to **standard-terms insurance** — **refer to BDM**.
* **Listed properties (Grade I / II / II*):*\* acceptable.
* **Maximum acreage:** **10 acres**.

## 55) Other Income Types

* **Foster income:** considered if **3 years’ fostering** and **not sole income** — **contact BDM**.
* **Seafarers:** considered — **contact BDM**.

## 56) Overpayments

* **Fee-free** overpayments up to **50% of the original loan** (subject to product availability).

## 57) Payday Loans

* **Refer to BDM** with **frequency, value, and time period**.

## 58) Pension Income

* **100%** accepted: annuity, company/private, war disablement, war widower, private, state pension.
* **Drawdown pensions:** use **100% of drawdown** (must last to end of term). Can **utilise uncrystallised funds** for income: take **80% of the pension fund ÷ mortgage term**.
* **SIPPs:** **100%** of drawn amount (must last to end of term). Uncrystallised funds treated the same (80% ÷ term).

## 59) Portability

* **Portable** as standard (subject to product availability).

## 60) Power of Attorney

* **Acceptable** on **remortgages / like-for-like**.
* **Not acceptable**: POA converting to BTL or purchasing into their own name a property they have powers over.

## 61) Probation Period

* **Can consider** if **ends before completion** and prior job was in **same line of work**.

## 62) Remortgage / Additional Borrowing – Purposes

* **Home improvements:** up to **90% LTV**.
* **Debt consolidation:** up to **90% LTV**.
* **Gifting:** accepted (≥ **£100,000** → **refer**).
* **Second home (UK only):** up to **80% LTV**.
* **Purchase of BTL or Holiday Let:** up to **90% LTV** (≤ **80%**: no simultaneous completion; **> 80%**: **simultaneous completion required**).
* **Purchase property in Ltd Co:** must be **SPV**; all applicants **named directors and shareholders**.
* **Repay Help to Buy:** up to **95% LTV** if sole reason for capital raise.
* **Shared Ownership staircasing:** accepted (subject to availability of SO products).
* **Extend a lease:** accepted.
* **Medical expenses:** **referral recommended**.
* **Purchase of land:** accepted.
* **School fees:** accepted.

## 63) Rental Income (as Income)

* **Accepted**.
* Use **tax calculation profit** (average of **latest 2 years**).
* **Note:** profit will be **net of finance costs**.

## 64) Repayment Types

* **Capital & Interest (Repayment):** accepted.
* **Part & Part:** accepted.
* **Interest-Only:** accepted.

## 65) Repossessions

* **Acceptable up to 75% LTV** if **> 3 years ago** and **any shortfall repaid in full** by the applicant.

## 66) Right to Buy

* **Accepted.**
* All applicants must be on **RTB paperwork**.
* **Capital raising:** **not accepted**.
* Can lend up to **100% of the discounted purchase price** provided **LTV on valuation ≤ 80%**.
* **Joint RTB where only one resides:** accepted; **max 80% LTV**; subject to affordability (including running costs of RTB property and any other residence).

## 67) Second Homes / Holiday Homes

* **Accepted** up to **80% LTV**.
* Acceptable for **dependant family members**.

## 68) Self Build

* **Accepted** up to **80% LTV** (**70%** for larger loans).
* **Acceptable construction:** ICF, MMC, Modular/POD, No-Fines, Off-site manufactured (MMC), Potton Homes, repaired PRC, steel frame, SIPs, thatched roof, modern timber framed (not with timber cladding), timber framed 1900–1970, post-1970 (with masonry skin), pre-1900.
* **BOPAS** accreditation required for **non-standard construction**.
* **Not habitable** properties can go through **Self Build** range.
* **Renovations:** may be Self Build or standard (depends on works) — **contact BDM**.
* **Shared access:** acceptable.
* **Multiplot:** acceptable if builder owns **< 25%** of site and there are **≤ 10** properties/sites.
* **Fee-assisted legals:** **not available** for Self Build.

## 69) Self-Employed Income (Assessment)

* **Taken to age 75** or retirement (whichever first).
* **≥ 25% shareholding** → classed as **self-employed**.
* **Ltd company owners:** use **higher of** (Net profit after tax + salary) **or** (salary + dividends).
* **Evidence:**
* **Ltd Co Directors:** **2 years** tax returns **and** accounts.
* **Sole Trader:** **2 years** tax returns.
* **LLP:** **2 years** tax returns **and** accounts (if available).
* **Projections** can be considered case-by-case — **contact BDM**.
* **Year-on-year movement rules:**
* **Increase < 20%:** use **most recent year**.
* **Increase > 20%:** use **average of last 2 years**.
* **Decline:** use **latest year** (referral recommended).

## 70) Sports Professionals

* **Assessed individually** — **contact BDM**.

## 71) Sub-Contractors

* **Accepted at 100%** — treated as **self-employed**.

## 72) Term

* **Minimum:** **6 years**.
* **Maximum:** **40 years**.

## 73) Visas

* **Family/Spousal:** can be considered — **referral recommended**.
* **Tier 1 working visas:** **not accepted**.
* **Skilled Worker Visa (formerly Tier 2):** **accepted** — must have **12 months** remaining at application.

## 74) Voters’ Roll

* **Applicants not on voter’s roll** can be accepted, but **proof of address** is required.

## 75) Zero-Hours Contracts

* Accepted if the applicants have been in the role for >18 months.
