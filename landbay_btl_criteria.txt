## SECTION 1 – APPLICANT ELIGIBILITY

### Accepted Borrower Types

- Individuals.
- Limited Companies (SPVs and trading).
- Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs).
- First-time landlords.

### All Applicants Must Meet

- Minimum age: 21.
- Maximum age: 95, provided at least one director is under 85 at end of term.
- A UK credit footprint is required.
- No adverse credit history.
- Minimum income of £25,000 per annum (no minimum income required for landlords with more than 24 months' experience).
- All income must be evidenced.

---

## SECTION 2 – FIRST-TIME LANDLORDS

- Maximum LTV: 75%.
- Must be owner-occupiers of their current main residence.

---

## SECTION 3 – PROPERTY ELIGIBILITY

### Location

- England and Wales only.
- Must not be consumer buy-to-lets, shared ownership, Help to Buy, Right to Buy or residential properties.

### Minimum Property Values

- Standard properties: £65,000.
- HMOs (in qualifying areas): £75,000.
- MUFBs: £120,000.

### Other Requirements

- Must be in an area with strong rental demand, as determined by Landbay's surveyor partners.
- Must be suitable for letting at completion.
- Rental income must meet the applicable ICR percentage — see Section 5 and the BTL calculator at: https://landbay.co.uk/intermediaries/products-and-btl-calculator/

---

## SECTION 4 – ACCEPTED PROPERTY TYPES

- Standard residential properties.
- Houses of Multiple Occupation (HMOs).
- Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs).
- Properties adjacent to or above commercial premises: considered up to a maximum LTV of 75%, provided the commercial use is in line with the local market and does not affect the property's security or long-term rental opportunities.

---

## SECTION 5 – HMOs AND MUFBs

### Size Definitions

- Small HMO/MUFB: up to 6 beds/units.
- Large HMO/MUFB: 7–12 beds/units.
- HMOs: maximum 12 bedrooms.
- MUFBs: maximum 12 units.
- MUFB definition: multiple flats on a single freehold.
- HMO minimum room dimensions must comply with statutory and local authority requirements.

### LTV Limits

- Maximum LTV for HMOs: 80%.
- Maximum LTV for MUFBs: 75%.

### Experience Requirements

- Applicants must have a minimum of 12 months' prior experience as a landlord.

---

## SECTION 6 – FIRST-TIME HMO OR MUFB LANDLORDS

Subject to product availability:

- Maximum LTV: 75%.
- Maximum loan size: £1,000,000.
- Up to 6 beds or units.
- Maximum 2 borrowers.
- Borrowers must be residential homeowners.
- Excludes trading limited companies.
- Excludes new builds.
- Investment valuations required.

---

## SECTION 7 – HOLIDAY LETS

- Maximum lending: 5 holiday let properties.
- All holiday lets require a physical valuation.
- First-time landlords excluded.
- Landlords cannot live in the property.

---

## SECTION 8 – PREMIER PRODUCTS

- Available for individuals and limited company SPVs.
- Maximum 15 BTL properties in portfolio.
- Intercompany loans are acceptable.
- Maximum exposure: £5,000,000.
- Property must be let on an AST of maximum 3 years.

---

## SECTION 9 – ICR AND AFFORDABILITY

### 2-Year Fixed Rate and Tracker Rate Products

Stressed at the greater of 5.50% or pay rate +2%.

**Individual – Basic Rate Taxpayer:**
- Standard: 125%.
- HMO/MUFB: 125%.
- First-time landlord HMO/MUFB: 135%.
- Holiday lets: 140%.

**Individual – Higher Rate Taxpayer:**
- Standard: 140%.
- HMO/MUFB: 140%.
- First-time landlord HMO/MUFB: 140%.
- Holiday lets: 140%.

**Limited Company / LLP:**
- Standard: 125%.
- HMO/MUFB: 125%.
- First-time landlord HMO/MUFB: 135%.
- Holiday lets: 125%.

### 5-Year Fixed Rate Products

Stressed at pay rate.

**Individual – Basic Rate Taxpayer:**
- Standard: 125%.
- HMO/MUFB: 125%.
- First-time landlord HMO/MUFB: 135%.
- Holiday lets: 140%.

**Individual – Higher Rate Taxpayer:**
- Standard: 140%.
- HMO/MUFB: 140%.
- First-time landlord HMO/MUFB: 140%.
- Holiday lets: 140%.

**Limited Company / LLP:**
- Standard: 125%.
- HMO/MUFB: 125%.
- First-time landlord HMO/MUFB: 135%.
- Holiday lets: 125%.

### Affordability Notes

- Where an application meets more than one of the above stress rates, the higher stress rate will apply.
- The underlying affordability of the background portfolio is assessed against a minimum underlying ICR of 125% at 5.00%.
- For tracker rate products: affordability will be recalculated if there is a change to the Bank of England base rate.

---

## SECTION 10 – APPLICATION TIMELINES

- All applications must reach offer within 50 days of full submission. If an offer is not made within this time, a new product must be selected, which could affect the loan size, rate and criteria.
- All mortgage offers are valid for 90 days.
- All property inspections must be completed within 20 days of instruction. If this timescale is not met, a new application may be requested.
