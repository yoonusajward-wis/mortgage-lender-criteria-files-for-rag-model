## Specialist Buy to Let Mortgages

### Overview

HTB offers specialist Buy to Let mortgages for a wide range of property types and borrower structures, including Limited Companies, SPVs, offshore entities, expats, foreign nationals, and more.

### Key Criteria

**Loan Amounts:**
* Maximum loan size: Up to £25 million

**Loan to Value (LTV):**
* Up to 75% LTV

**Interest Cover Ratio (ICR):**
* **Companies:** 125% minimum
* **Individuals:** 140% minimum

**Property Types Accepted:**
* Limited Companies (Trading, SPVs, New SPVs)
* Offshore entities
* Expats
* Foreign nationals
* First-time landlords
* Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs)
* Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs)
* Semi-commercial properties
* New build flats
* Short-term lets

### Product Features

* Flexible underwriting for complex cases
* Support for professional landlords
* Portfolio lending capabilities
* Specialist structures accepted

## Bridging Finance

### Overview

HTB offers residential and commercial bridging finance for a variety of property types and purposes.

### Key Criteria

**Loan Amounts:**
* **Minimum:** £100,000
* **Maximum:** £10 million
* **Developer Exit loans:** Up to £25 million

**Loan to Value (LTV):**
* Up to 75% LTV

**Loan Term:**
* 12 to 24 months

**Property Types:**
* Residential (including light refurbishment)
* Commercial
* Semi-commercial
* Development exit
* Land
* Heavy refurbishment projects

**Interest Rates:**
* Starting from 0.75% per month
* Varies based on LTV and property type

**Fees:**
* **Arrangement Fee:** 2%

**Early Repayment Charges (ERCs):**
* Minimum of 3 months' interest

### Bridging Finance Features

* Fast decision making
* Flexible terms
* Support for various property types
* Development exit finance available

## Portfolio Edge

### Overview

Portfolio Edge is designed for professional landlords with complex portfolios that don't fit standard affordability models. It combines a two-year fixed-rate mortgage with a bridging facility.

### Key Criteria

**Loan Structure:**
* Two-year fixed-rate mortgage combined with a bridging facility

**Loan Amounts:**
* **Term loan:** Minimum £3 million
* **Bridge:** Minimum £1 million

**Interest Rates:**
* **Term loan:** 4.99% with 5% arrangement fee
* **Bridge:** 0.7% per month with 2% arrangement fee

**Property Types:**
* Portfolios may include up to 20% commercial assets
* Complex portfolio structures

### Portfolio Edge Features

* Designed for professional landlords
* Combines term loan and bridging facility
* Suitable for complex portfolios
* Flexible structure for portfolios that don't fit standard models
