## SECTION 1 – SPECIALIST BUY TO LET MORTGAGES

### Loan Amounts and LTV

- Maximum loan size: £25,000,000.
- Maximum LTV: 75%.

### Interest Cover Ratio (ICR)

- Companies: minimum 125%.
- Individuals: minimum 140%.

### Accepted Borrower Types and Property Types

- Limited Companies (trading, SPVs, new SPVs).
- Offshore entities.
- Expats.
- Foreign nationals.
- First-time landlords.
- Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs).
- Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs).
- Semi-commercial properties.
- New build flats.
- Short-term lets.

### Underwriting

- Flexible underwriting for complex cases.
- Support for professional landlords.
- Portfolio lending capabilities.
- Specialist structures accepted.

## SECTION 3 – BRIDGING FINANCE

### Loan Amounts

- Minimum loan: £100,000.
- Maximum loan: £10,000,000.
- Developer Exit loans: up to £25,000,000.

### LTV and Term

- Maximum LTV: 75%.
- Loan term: 12 to 24 months.

### Interest Rates and Fees

- Interest rates starting from 0.75% per month (varies based on LTV and property type).
- Arrangement fee: 2%.
- Early repayment charge: minimum of 3 months' interest.

### Accepted Property Types

- Residential (including light refurbishment).
- Commercial.
- Semi-commercial.
- Development exit.
- Land.
- Heavy refurbishment projects.

---

## SECTION 4 – PORTFOLIO EDGE

Portfolio Edge is designed for professional landlords with complex portfolios that do not fit standard affordability models. It combines a two-year fixed-rate mortgage with a bridging facility.

### Loan Structure and Amounts

- Structure: two-year fixed-rate mortgage combined with a bridging facility.
- Term loan minimum: £3,000,000.
- Bridge minimum: £1,000,000.

### Interest Rates and Fees

- Term loan: 4.99% with 5% arrangement fee.
- Bridge: 0.70% per month with 2% arrangement fee.

### Property Criteria

- Portfolios may include up to 20% commercial assets.
- Complex portfolio structures accepted.

---

## SECTION 5 – DEVELOPMENT FINANCE

### LTV and Term

- Maximum LTV: 65% of Gross Development Value (GDV).
- Loan term: 12 to 36 months.

### Loan Amounts

- Tailored based on project needs.

### Accepted Development Types

- Residential developments.
- Student accommodation.
- Mixed-use schemes.

### Eligibility

- Experienced property developers only.
