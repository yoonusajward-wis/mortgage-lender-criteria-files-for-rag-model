1. LOAN & BORROWER CRITERIA
================================================================================

LOAN SIZE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum: £50,000
- Maximum: £1,500,000

PROPERTY VALUE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum: £100,000
- Maximum: £10,000,000

LOAN TERM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum: 5 years
- Maximum: 40 years

BORROWER AGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum age at application: 21
- Maximum age at application: 88

NUMBER OF BORROWERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Maximum 2 applicants

RESIDENCY REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- UK resident for at least 3 years
- Must have indefinite leave to remain

REPAYMENT TYPES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Capital & Interest (C&I)
- Interest Only (I/O)

MAXIMUM LTV
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- C&I: up to 75%
- Interest Only: up to 75%

DEBT CONSOLIDATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Allowed up to a maximum 75% LTV

PORTFOLIO LIMITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Maximum 4 mortgaged properties in total at completion (includes main residence)

LIMITED COMPANIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Not accepted – personal applications only

MINIMUM PERSONAL INCOME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- No minimum income requirement
- Assessment based solely on rental performance

================================================================================
2. AFFORDABILITY & RENTAL ASSESSMENT
================================================================================

AFFORDABILITY METHOD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Based on rental income only
- Rental income must cover 140% of stressed mortgage payments
- Stress based on Interest Only payments at:
  - Higher of: product rate + 2% or 5.5%

RENTAL INCOME CALCULATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Use 30 weeks estimated letting per year
- Based on average of:
  - High-season weekly rent
  - Mid-season weekly rent
  - Low-season weekly rent

PERSONAL OCCUPANCY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Up to 90 days per year personal use allowed

================================================================================
3. LOAN PURPOSE
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE PURPOSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Purchase of a Holiday Let
- Remortgage of existing Holiday Let
- Remortgage of existing residential property to convert to Holiday Let

================================================================================
4. PROPERTY CRITERIA
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE LOCATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- England (including Isle of Wight)
- Wales
- Scotland, including islands connected by road bridge

UNACCEPTABLE LOCATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Northern Ireland
- Isle of Man
- Channel Islands
- Any island not accessible by road bridge
  - Exception: Isle of Wight
- Holiday parks / holiday complexes
- London (Holiday Lets not accepted due to the 90-day letting cap)

================================================================================
5. UNACCEPTABLE PROPERTY TYPES
================================================================================

- Retirement or age-restricted properties
- Sheltered accommodation
- Park homes
- Mobile homes
- Caravans
- Houseboats

================================================================================
6. PROPERTY CONDITION & MINIMUMS
================================================================================

CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Must be in readily lettable condition
- Allowance made for furnishing, decorating, and minor updating

MINIMUM PROPERTY VALUES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Standard: £100,000
- Above or adjacent to commercial premises: £150,000
- Ex-public-sector:
  - Houses: £150,000
  - Flats & maisonettes: £250,000

BEDROOMS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Maximum 6 bedrooms

KITCHENS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Up to 2 kitchens acceptable

ANNEXES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- One fully self-contained annexe acceptable if let short-term only

================================================================================
7. NEW BUILD CRITERIA
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- New build houses
- New build flats
- Conversions completed within last 12 months
- Developer must be registered with New Homes Ombudsman Service (NHOS)

CONSTRUCTION REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Must have suitable structural warranty or Professional Consultant's Certificate (PCC)

MULTI-UNIT DEVELOPMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- If part of a development with more than 3 units, the development must be registered with the New Homes Quality Board (NHQB)

NEW BUILD INCENTIVES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Maximum 5% incentives allowed, including:
  - Cashback
  - Stamp duty paid
  - Deposit contributions
  - Legal fee incentives
- Incentives do not need to be deducted from valuation or purchase price
- Incentive value must be matched by applicant's own funds

================================================================================
8. EX-PUBLIC-SECTOR PROPERTIES
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE (WITH MINIMUM VALUES)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Houses: £150,000
- Flats / maisonettes: £250,000

ADDITIONAL CONDITIONS FOR FLATS/MAISONETTES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- No more than 4 storeys
- No communal balconies
- No open-access walkways

UNACCEPTABLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Ex-public-sector bought under Right to Buy still within pre-emption period

================================================================================
9. NON-STANDARD CONSTRUCTION
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE (WITH CONDITIONS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Single skin / half-brick walls allowed only in single-storey non-habitable rooms
- Modern timber-framed properties built after:
  - 1950 in Scotland
  - 1970 in England & Wales
- Modern methods of construction (MMC) considered case-by-case

UNACCEPTABLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Timber-frame properties where cavity was retrospectively filled with insulation

================================================================================
10. ABOVE OR ADJACENT TO COMMERCIAL PREMISES
================================================================================

CONSIDERED (CASE-BY-CASE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Restaurants
- Takeaways
- Cafes
- Pubs and bars

MINIMUM PROPERTY VALUES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- £150,000 outside London
- £250,000 in Greater London

================================================================================
11. FLATS & MAISONETTES
================================================================================

GENERAL ACCEPTABILITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Maximum 6 storeys as standard

EXCEPTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Private flats over 6 storeys may be accepted if:
  - Developer has signed the Developer Remediation Contract (England)
  - Or Welsh Government equivalent (Wales)

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Flats above the 3rd floor must have a lift (except Scottish tenements)
- Non-standard construction flats limited to 4 storeys

SERVICE CHARGE & GROUND RENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Service charge ≤ 1% of property value
- Ground rent ≤ 0.1% of property value
- Ground rent escalation must not double more frequently than every 20 years
- Must not be linked to property value

================================================================================
12. NEW BUILD FLATS
================================================================================

- Acceptable once fully completed
- All communal areas must be finished
- Appropriate warranty/certification required

================================================================================
13. LEASEHOLD REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

- Minimum 85 years lease remaining at completion
- Freehold flats/maisonettes are unacceptable

================================================================================
14. SCOTTISH TENEMENT FLATS
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE IF:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Value ≥ £100,000 (or £120,000 if above commercial)
- Built before 1915
- Masonry construction with pitched roof
- Maximum 5 storeys
- No communal balconies or open-access walkways

LICENSING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- A Holiday Let licence may be required
- Must be referred for review

================================================================================
15. COVENANTS
================================================================================

- Properties with restrictive covenants affecting usage are likely to be unacceptable
- Refer if unsure
