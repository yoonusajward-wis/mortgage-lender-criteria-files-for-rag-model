## Overview

The Stafford Building Society offers Buy to Let (BTL) mortgages for personal applicants (individuals, not limited companies). The Society provides BTL products for standard buy to let properties, holiday lets, and expat buy to let applications.

### Key Features

* **Maximum 70% LTV** - For all BTL products
* **Portfolio landlords accepted** - Up to 10 BTL mortgaged properties
* **Holiday let products available**
* **Expat BTL products available**
* **Interest-only, repayment, or part-and-part** - Available repayment options
* **No HMOs** - Houses of Multiple Occupancy not accepted
* **No top slicing** - Not available
* **No capital raising for debt consolidation** - Not permitted

## Rental Income & Affordability

### Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR)

**Standard BTL (Non-Portfolio):**
* **LTV up to 50%:** 130% at 5.50% or 2% above the product rate, whichever is higher
* **LTV between 50% and 70%:** 140% at 5.50% or 2% above the product rate, whichever is higher

**Portfolio Landlords:**
* **Entire portfolio:** 150% at 5.50% or 2% above the product rate, whichever is higher
* **Mortgage security (Society's loan):** Standard ICR stress criteria apply (130% for LTV up to 50%, 140% for LTV 50-70%)

### Rental Assessment

* Rental income must meet ICR requirements
* Rental assessment required from appropriate sources
* Subject to valuation and market rental assessment

### Top Slicing

* **Not available** - Top slicing is not permitted for BTL applications

---

## Portfolio Landlords

### Definition

* **Portfolio landlords:** Applicants with four or more BTL mortgaged properties
* **Maximum portfolio size:** Up to 10 BTL mortgaged properties in total

### ICR Stress Testing for Portfolio Landlords

* **Entire portfolio:** 150% at 5.50% or 2% above the product rate, whichever is higher
* **Mortgage security (Society's loan):** Standard ICR stress criteria apply:
  * LTV up to 50%: 130% at 5.50% or 2% above the product rate, whichever is higher
  * LTV between 50% and 70%: 140% at 5.50% or 2% above the product rate, whichever is higher

## Holiday Let Specific Criteria

### Holiday Let Properties

* **Holiday let properties** - Accepted (to be read in conjunction with BTL General criteria)
* **The Society will lend on residential properties to individuals on a BTL basis for renting out as a holiday let property**
* **Maximum LTV:** 70%
* **Holiday parks are not acceptable**
* **Occupancy restrictions are considered** subject to valuer's comments
* **Location** - England (including Isle of Wight) and Wales
* **Minimum property value:** £100,000

### Holiday Let Rental Assessment

* **The Society requests the applicant(s) to provide independent confirmation from a local agent or comparable online specialist** of the predicted holiday rental over 12 months, taking into account high, medium and low seasons
* **An average taken over a 30-week occupancy assumption** applied on the basis of the example below

**Example Calculation:**
* If the Low Season is £1,650, the Medium Season £2,300 and the High Season £3,100, an average per week of the 3 seasons (£2,350) will be used
* Then we will apply a 30-week occupancy assumption
* In this example gross rental income per annum would be £70,500
* The mortgage payment is then stressed at an ICR of 140% @ 5.5% or 2% above the product, whichever is higher, of the average monthly holiday let income

## Expat BTL Specific Criteria

### Expat Buy to Let

* **Expat BTL products available** - Specific products for expatriate applicants (to be read in conjunction with BTL General criteria)
* **The Society will lend to UK citizens residing out of the country** (subject to being on the acceptable countries list) for BTL and HBTL purchases and re-mortgages
* **Homeownership requirement:** Applicants do not need to own a UK property
* **Residency:** Expat applicants accepted
* **EPC requirement:** ExPat BTL requires valid EPC (exemptions not considered)
* **Location:** England (including Isle of Wight) and Wales
* **Minimum property value:** £100,000
* **Maximum LTV:** 70%

### Expat Applicant Requirements

* **Acceptable applicants must be employed by a nationally recognised company**
* **The applicant's company must not be involved in high-risk industries** as listed here: https://www.fca.org.uk/firms/financial-crime/money-laundering-regulations
* **The Society will require 3 months' salary slips** and may request an employment reference provided in English
* **All mortgage payments must be made from a UK bank account** in the applicant(s) own name
* **The Ex-Pat BTL property must be managed through a UK letting agent**
* **For Ex-Pat applicants who already own mortgaged UK property(s):** They must provide the latest mortgage statement(s) for each mortgaged property(s)

### Acceptable & Unacceptable Countries

* **For the full list of accepted & unaccepted countries, please refer to the Expat Country List document**
* Contact the Society for the current Expat Country List

### Expat Proof of Deposit

* **Deposits which originated outside of the UK are accepted** subject to the funds being in the applicant(s) UK bank account prior to application
* **An explanation of how the funds have been acquired** as part of the application process must be provided

### Expat Capital Raising

* **If an applicant wants to raise funds on their UK property, to fund a deposit/purchase in the Country they currently live in, this is acceptable**, subject to the funds being paid to the solicitor acting on the borrowers'/Society's behalf

### Expat BTL Products

* **BTL Expat and Standard 2 Year Discount of 4.25% (70% LTV)** - Product Code: BL09
* **BTL And Holiday Expat 2 Year Discount of 1.55% (70% LTV)** - Product Code: BL08
* **Expat And Standard Holiday BTL 2 Year Discount of 3.95% (70% LTV)** - Product Code: HB08
* **Retention BTL/HL/Expat 2 Year Discount of 2.35%** - Product Code: RT14 (for existing customers)

### Expat Documentation

* **Expat ID Requirements** - Specific identification requirements apply
* **3 months' salary slips** - Required
* **Employment reference** - May be requested (provided in English)
* **Latest mortgage statement(s)** - Required for each mortgaged UK property (if applicable)
* Standard income and property documentation required
