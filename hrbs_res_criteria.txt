## SECTION 2 – JOINT BORROWER SOLE PROPRIETOR (JBSP)

- Allows family and friends to be included on the mortgage.
- All parties share responsibility for the mortgage.
- Only the main purchaser is on the property deeds.
- Applicant not party to the mortgage deed must receive Independent Legal Advice prior to completion.
- Up to 4 borrowers.
- Non-family members accepted.

### Income Multiple

- 4.49x for the 2 highest earners.
- 1x for additional applicants.
- Tailored Term option available.

### Maximum LTV by Relationship

- Parent: 95% (when products available).
- Spouse: 95% (when products available).
- Sister: 95% (when products available).
- Brother: 95% (when products available).
- Non-family members / other joint applicants: 80% — must be referred to the MRC and must be residential homeowners.

### Tailored Term

- Allows different applicants to share the mortgage over different timescales.
- Younger applicant affordability calculated over maximum term.
- Older applicant contribution can be calculated over a shorter term.

## SECTION 3 – INCOME FLEX

- Available up to 95% LTV.
- Flexible income assessment allowing inclusion of additional income sources.

**Income types considered include:**
- Investment income.
- Commission.
- Bonuses.
- Overtime.
- Rental income.
- Stocks and shares income.

### Agency Workers

- Maximum LTV: 80%.

### Bank Work / Locum Work

- 6 months' evidence required.

### Benefits and Tax Credits

- Maximum 100%.

### Bonus, Commission and Overtime

- Maximum 100%.
- 1 year's evidence required.
- Cannot exceed 100% of basic income.

### Bursary

- Maximum 100%.
- Another earned income required.

### CIS Contractors

- Treated as PAYE if taxed at source.
- 3 months' payslips required.

### Day Rate Contractors

- Day rate × 48 weeks.
- No minimum income.
- Minimum remaining contract term: 3 months.

### Fixed-Term Contractors

- 12-month contract in place.
- No renewal required.

### Investment Income

- Maximum 100%.
- 2 years' SA302s required.

### Lodger Income

- Up to 100% up to the current tax-free allowance (£7,500 per annum).
- Primary income must exceed £7,500 per annum.

### Net Profit and Salary (Limited Company)

- Accepted.

### Probation Period

- Considered if new and previous roles are similar or represent a promotion.

### Retained Profits

- Up to 80% LTV.
- Applicant must be main shareholder.
- Company must have been trading for a minimum of 2 years.
- Minimum 2 years' accounts required.
- Cannot exceed applicant's shareholding percentage.
- Can be used if salary and net profit are insufficient.

### Second Income

- Maximum 100%.
- Maximum 55 hours per week.

### Self-Employed Income

- 1 year's accounts/SA302s accepted.
- Declining profits acceptable.
- Projections permitted from an accountant.

### Short-Term Contracts

- Considered with 6 months' contract history and 1 renewal.

### Sports Professionals

- Accepted.

### Stipend Income

- Up to 100%.
- 12 months' evidence required.
- If primary income: maximum 80% LTV.

### Tronc Payments (Tips)

- Up to 100%.
- Minimum 12 months' evidence required.

### Units of Dental Activity (UDA)

- Value of current annual contract accepted.

### Umbrella Companies

- Accepted.

### Zero Hours Contracts

- 12 months' evidence required.
- Maximum 80% LTV if sole or highest income source.

## SECTION 4 – CREDIT FLEX

- Available up to 80% LTV.
- Standard income multiple: 4.49x.
- Maximum loan size: £500,000 (LTV dependent).

### CCJs

- None in last 3 months.
- Maximum 2 between 3–36 months.
- Total value up to £500.

### Communications

- Any number of late payments (including settled default).

### Credit Card / Mail Order / Current Account / Utilities

- Up to 5 months late within last 24 months.
- Over 24 months: any number considered.

### Debt Management Plans

- None registered in last 12 months.
- Must be satisfied before completion.

### Defaults (Utilities, Credit Cards, Telecoms)

- None in last 3 months.
- Maximum £2,500 total in last 36 months.
- Above £2,500: refer to sales team.

### Hire Purchase / Unsecured Loan

- Up to 2 months late in last 24 months.
- Up to 3 months late between 24–36 months.

### IVA / Debt Relief Order / Bankruptcy

- None in last 3 years.
- Historical cases must be satisfied.

### Mortgage or Secured Loan

- Up to 2 months late in last 24 months.
- Up to 3 months late between 24–36 months.

### Payday Loans

- None in last 6 months.
- Maximum total £500 in last 36 months.
- No arrears permitted.

### Important Credit Conditions

- No secured arrears in last 3 months.
- Latest unsecured loan payment must be up to date.
- All CCJs, defaults and DMPs must be satisfied before completion.
- Debt consolidation not permissible.
- IVA must be satisfied for at least 3 years before application.
- Explanations for imperfect credit may be requested.

## SECTION 5 – SKILLED WORKER VISAS (RESIDENTIAL)

- No minimum UK residency required.
- No minimum income requirement.
- No minimum time remaining on visa required; if less than 6 months remaining, confirmation of extension required.
- 1-year employment track record required.
- Joint applications accepted where the partner holds a spousal visa.
- Self-employed accepted.

### LTV Limits

- Residential lending: up to 95% LTV (restricted to 90% LTV if UK residency is less than 12 months).
- Buy to Let lending: up to 75% LTV.

### Accepted Visas

- Skilled Worker.
- Health and Care.
- Global Talent.
- Ancestry.

If the visa type is not listed, contact HRBS to discuss the application.

### New Build Purchases

- Builder deposits up to 5% accepted.
- No adjustment of purchase price for LTV.
- Up to 90% LTV gross to visa holders regardless of residency period.

### Visa Buy to Let

- Limited companies: not acceptable.
- Must meet minimum ICR requirements.
- Top-slicing: not permitted.
- If no UK residential property is owned, residency confirmation required.
- Minimum income: £35,000.

### Visa Self-Employed

- Up to 90% LTV.
- Minimum 1 year's accounts required.
- Minimum 3 months remaining on contract.
- Must be sponsored by a licensed UK employer.

**Eligible professions:**
- IT and technology contractors.
- Education professionals.
- Healthcare professionals (excluding doctors and dentists).
- Legal professionals.
- Architects.
- Surveyors.
- Engineers.

### Additional Requirements

- Repayment basis only.
- Contractors accepted.
- Proof of UK address required.
- Deposit must be held in a UK bank account.

## SECTION 6 – FLEX PLUS

**Available for:**
- First-time buyers.
- New home purchases.
- Remortgages.

**Not available for:**
- Existing customers renewing.
- Borrowing more.

**Scenarios include:**
- Second home purchase up to 90% LTV.
- Unique or non-standard property.
- Concessionary purchase up to 90%.
- Family loan towards deposit.
- 100% benefit income.
- 100% commission income.
- Capital raise up to 90% of property value.
- Debt consolidation up to 30% of property value.
- Income multiples up to 4.49x for all 4 applicants.
- Multiple jobs exceeding 55 hours per week.
- Combination of Income Flex and Credit Flex (maximum 80% LTV).

## SECTION 7 – GREEN MORTGAGES

- Available to borrowers pledging to use at least 50% of additional funds for energy efficiency improvements.
- Retrogreen partnership available for energy efficiency assessment, EPC improvement planning and end-to-end project management.

### Qualifying Improvements – Energy Efficiency

- Insulation: walls, roofs, lofts, basements and floors.
- External wall insulation.
- Internal wall insulation.
- Loft insulation.
- Double glazing.
- Draught proofing.
- Energy-efficient doors.
- Insulated render.
- Low-energy lights.
- Tank and pipe insulation.
- Cavity wall insulation.

### Qualifying Improvements – Heating

- HVAC installation/replacement.
- Ground source heat pumps.
- Air source heat pumps.
- Heating controls.
- Efficient circulating pumps.
- Micro-CHP.

### Qualifying Improvements – Renewables

- Solar hot water.
- Solar photovoltaic.
- Rainfall capture.

### Qualifying Improvements – Resilience

- Flood-resistant doors and windows (BS 851188).
- Demountable barriers.
- Resilient insulation.
- Resilient wall and floor finishes.
- Heatwave resistance measures (external shutters, external insulation).
