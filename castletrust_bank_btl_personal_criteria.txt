## Overview

Castle Trust Bank offers Buy to Let (BTL) mortgages for personal applicants (individuals, sole traders, partnerships, and LLPs). The Bank provides flexible BTL products suitable for various investment strategies, including standard residential portfolios, Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs), Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs), and holiday lets.

### Key Features

* **Flexible lending criteria** - Suitable for first-time landlords, portfolio landlords, complex structures, ex-pats, and foreign nationals
* **TermTen Product** - 10-year term with 5-year fixed rate
* **Interest-only payments** - Available
* **Portfolio landlords accepted** - No maximum portfolio size restrictions
* **Various property types** - Standard BTL, HMOs, MUFBs, holiday lets
* **Competitive ICR requirements** - Tiered ICR based on loan term

## General Lending Criteria

### Where We Lend

* **Location:** England and Wales only

### Loan-to-Value (LTV)

* **Maximum LTV:** Up to 75% (net)
* **TermTen Product:** Up to 77% gross LTV (including net loan and arrangement fee)

### Loan Amounts

* **Minimum loan amount:** £150,000 (may vary by product)
* **TermTen Product:** Minimum £200,000
* **Maximum loan amount:** £2 million (may vary by product)
* **TermTen Product:** Maximum £10,000,000 (gross)

### Loan Terms

* **Term:** 10 years (TermTen Product)
* **Fixed rate period:** 5 years (TermTen Product)
* **Repayment:** Interest-only available

## Applicant Requirements

### Applicant Types

* **Individuals** - Personal applicants
* **Sole traders** - Self-employed individuals
* **Partnerships** - Business partnerships
* **LLPs** - Limited Liability Partnerships
* **First-time landlords** - Accepted
* **Portfolio landlords** - Accepted (no maximum portfolio size restrictions)
* **Ex-pats** - Acceptable providing they own a property in the UK
* **Foreign nationals** - Acceptable where they have proven UK mortgage conduct
* **First-time landlords** - For UK residents, first-time landlords are acceptable and can also be first-time buyers. For foreign nationals, first-time landlords are acceptable, but not if they are also a first-time buyer
* **Complex structures** - Considered on a case-by-case basis
* **Power of Attorney applications** - Not acceptable

### Income Requirements

* **No specified minimum income** - Applicants must have demonstrable income and a suitable repayment strategy
* **Rental income** - Must meet Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR) requirements
* **Joint applications** - Where one applicant has demonstrable income to cover the repayment, joint applicants who are students or unemployed will be considered

### Age Requirements

* **Minimum applicant age:** 21 years
* **Maximum applicant age at end of term:** 85 years

## Property Criteria

### Property Location

* **England and Wales only**

### Property Value

* **Minimum security value:** £75,000 (TermTen & Bridge products)
* **Minimum property value:** £135,000 (may vary by product)

### Property Types

**Acceptable Properties:**
* **Standard buy to let properties** - Residential properties let on Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST)
* **Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs)** - Small and large HMOs accepted (TermTen Product)
* **Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs)** - Accepted (TermTen Product)
* **Holiday lets** - Accepted (affordability based on holiday let income, not AST)
* **Serviced Apartments** - Acceptable
* **Airbnb** - Acceptable
* **Portfolio properties** - Multiple properties considered
* **Aggregated value on blocks of flats** - Up to 20 units (TermTen Product)
* Subject to valuation and standard property criteria

**Commercial Property:**
* **Commercial property is not acceptable** for any applications unless residential planning is in place

**Property Requirements:**
* **Must be let on an Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST) or equivalent**
* **AST period:** Between 6 to 12 months required (longer terms by negotiation)
* **Limited Company lets** - Acceptable where the tenant is a named individual
* **Not occupied by the applicant or their immediate family**
* **Subject to valuation and standard property criteria**

### Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)

* **Properties with no valid EPC rating or rating below E** - Not acceptable (unless exempt)
* **EPC exemptions:** May be considered on a case-by-case basis

### Property Construction

**Acceptable Construction:**
* **Standard construction** - Means:
  * Masonry walls of solid or cavity construction having a minimum thickness of 200mm
  * Modern timber (post 1975) or steel framed (post 1998) property with a masonry outer cladding originally built with the benefit of a suitable new home warranty
  * Standard slate or tiled roof construction
  * Flat roof warranty must be in place
  * Thatch (considered on an individual basis)
  * Modern methods of construction

**Unacceptable Construction:**
* All non-traditional construction types
* Steel framed, with non-masonry cladding
* Solid masonry having a thickness less than 200mm
* Wimpey No Fines
* Laing Easi Form post 1945
* Croswall
* Agricultural properties
* Concrete (No Fines, Cheshire Brine, Mundic, Precast Reinforced Concrete (PRC))
* Cladded blocks without a satisfactory (A1, A2, B1) EWS1 form
* Cross wall construction pre 1970
* Holiday Parks
* Hostels
* House boat
* Laing Easi Form pre 1945
* Log cabin
* Mobile home
* Single Skin
* Timber Frame (period properties considered on an individual basis)

**Note:** Castle Trust Bank will not normally lend on a security of non-standard construction.

### Ex-Local Authority Properties

* **An ex-local authority property must be located in an area or block where 50% or more of the properties are privately owned**

### Minimum Floor Space

* **Minimum floor space:** 35m² per unit
* **MUFB properties:** Can consider units between 30 and 35m², so long as the average of the block is 35m² or more

### Minimum Unexpired Lease

* **Minimum acceptable unexpired lease:** 60 years at maturity

### Tenancy Agreement

* **Must be let on an Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST) agreement**
* **AST period:** Between 6 to 12 months required (longer terms by negotiation)
* **Limited Company lets** - Acceptable where the tenant is a named individual
* **Holiday Lets, Serviced Apartments and Airbnb** - Acceptable
* **Holiday lets** - Affordability based on holiday let income, not AST

## Rental Income & Affordability

### Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR) - BTL Rental Coverage Test

**Calculation of Rental Cover for Castle Trust Bank loan:**

**Borrower Type - 5 Year Fixed:**
* **Basic rate taxpayer & limited companies:** Pay Rate @ 125%
* **High rate taxpayer & HMOs:** Pay Rate @ 145%

**Stress Rate:**
* **5.5%** - Standard stress rate applied to all ICR calculations

### Rental Assessment

* Rental income must meet ICR requirements
* Rental assessment required from appropriate sources
* Subject to valuation and market rental assessment
* **Holiday lets** - Affordability based on holiday let income, not AST

### Portfolio Landlords

* **No maximum portfolio size restrictions**
* Portfolio landlords accepted
* ICR stress testing applies to individual properties
* Portfolio-wide assessment may be considered

## Product Types

### TermTen Product

**TermTen - 10 Year Term with 5 Year Fixed Rate:**
* **Term:** 10 years
* **Fixed rate period:** 5 years
* **Interest rate:** Starting from 6.39% (fixed for first 5 years)

* **Property types:** Standard BTLs, MUFBs, small and large HMOs, Holiday Lets
* **Aggregated value on blocks of flats:** Up to 20 units

### Other BTL Products

* Various BTL products available
* Loan sizes: £150,000 to £2 million
* Maximum LTV: Up to 75% (net)
* Terms and rates vary by product

## Credit History

### Adverse Credit

**Bankruptcy Orders or Sequestration:**
* **Unacceptable** where registered in the last 36 months, whether discharged or not
* **Unacceptable** where registered prior to 36 months ago and not discharged

**County Court Judgements (or Decrees) and Defaults:**
* **Maximum total value of £5,000 satisfied**, over no more than 3 instances within 36 months prior to application
* **No instances** have occurred within 12 months prior to application

**Individual Voluntary Arrangements or Trust Deeds:**
* **Unacceptable** where registered in the last 36 months, whether satisfied or not
* **Unacceptable** where registered prior to 36 months ago and not satisfied

**Missed Mortgage Payments:**
* **No missed payment** within last 12 months
* **Maximum of two missed payments** in the last 36 months, excluding the first 12 months

**Repossessions:**
* **Not acceptable** in the last three years

**Unsecured Credit:**
* **Maximum 2 in 12 months**, maximum status 1
* **No more than 4 missed payments** in 36 months, maximum status 1

### Supporting Documentation

Standard BTL mortgage application documentation required, including:

* **Rental assessment** - Required (from appropriate sources)
