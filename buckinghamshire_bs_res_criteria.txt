## SECTION 1 – ADVERSE CREDIT

### Bankruptcy / Sequestration

* Everyday Residential: Discharged for at least 5 years.
* Credit Revive: Consider if discharged for at least 3 years.
* Credit Restore: Consider once discharged.
* No further adverse credit since bankruptcy.
* Deposit must be from applicant's own funds.
* Gifted deposit: refer to Key Account Manager.

### County Court Judgements (CCJs)

* Assessed on total value.
* Everyday Residential: Up to £100 outstanding in last 3 years (none in last 2 years).
* Credit Revive: Up to £500 outstanding in last 3 years (none in last 2 years).
* Credit Restore: More than £500 outstanding in last 3 years considered.

### CCJs – Parking Fines

* Everyday Residential: Up to £500.
* Credit Revive: Up to £500.
* Credit Restore: More than £500 in last 3 years.

### Debt Management Plan (DMP)

* Everyday Residential: None in last 3 years.
* Credit Revive: None in last 3 years.
* Credit Restore: Underwriter discretion.

### Debt Relief Order (DRO)

* Everyday Residential: Discharged for at least 5 years.
* Credit Revive: Discharged for at least 3 years.
* Credit Restore: Consider once discharged.
* No further adverse since DRO.
* Deposit from own funds.
* Gifted deposit: refer.

### Defaults – HP, Credit Cards & Unsecured Loans

* Everyday Residential: None in last 2 years.
* Credit Revive: None in last 2 years.
* Credit Restore: Underwriter discretion.

### Defaults – Utilities & Communications

* Assessed on total value.
* Everyday Residential: Up to £250 in last 2 years.
* Credit Revive & Credit Restore: Underwriter discretion.

### IVA

* Everyday Residential: Satisfied for 5 years (no adverse since).
* Credit Revive: Satisfied for 3 years (no adverse since).
* Credit Restore: Must be satisfied on completion.
* Capital raising to repay IVA may be considered.
* Deposit from own funds.
* Gifted deposit: refer.

### Missed Payments – Mail Order

* Everyday Residential & Credit Revive: Status 2 in last 2 years.
* Credit Restore: Status 3 or more in last 2 years.

### Missed Payments – Utilities & Communications

* Considered if not defaulted.
* If defaulted, refer to default criteria.

### Mortgage Arrears

* Everyday Residential: None in last 2 years.
* Credit Revive: Up to Status 2 in last 2 years (none in last year).
* Credit Restore: Status 3 or more in last 2 years.

### Rent Arrears

* Considered with justifiable reasons.

### Repossession

* Not acceptable.

## SECTION 2 – APPLICANT ELIGIBILITY

### Age at Application

* Minimum 18.
* No maximum, subject to affordability.

### Age at End of Term

* No maximum, subject to affordability.
* Working income accepted to age 75.
* Beyond 75: retirement income required.

### Retired at Application

* Accepted.
* If reliant on pension income, stress on sole affordability for joint cases.

### Couple Application in Sole Name

* Not acceptable for purchase.
* Remortgage may be considered (refer).

### Criminal Record

* Not acceptable unless spent.
* Not acceptable for financial crime or fraud.

### Limited Companies

* Not accepted on residential range.

### Maximum Applicants

* Maximum 4.

### Power of Attorney

* Accepted.

### Residency

* British Citizens: no minimum residency.
* Returning ExPats: no minimum period.
* LTV restriction may apply for recent return.
* Settled Status treated as indefinite leave.
* Pre-Settled, Spousal, Work, Skilled Visa:

  * 2–3 years UK living and working.
  * Home Office email certificate required.
  * Must be standard credit.
  * Deposit fully saved (no gifted or undeclared income).
  * Visa must permit UK work.

### Trusts & Trustees

* Not accepted.

## SECTION 4 – PROPERTY ELIGIBILITY

### Agricultural Ties

* Considered up to 50% LTV.

### Auction Purchases

* Accepted.
* Day one remortgages considered.

### Composite Cladding

* EWS1 required.
* A1 & A2 acceptable.
* B1 case-by-case.
* B3 unacceptable.

### Ex-Local Authority

* Accepted.
* Flats maximum 4 storeys.

### Flats Above Commercial

* Prime locations acceptable.
* Secondary locations unlikely acceptable.
* Block minimum 3 storeys including commercial.
* Valuer must confirm demand.

### Maximum Floors

* Maximum 6 storeys.
* Ex-local authority maximum 4.

### Flat Roofs

* Case-by-case.
* If >10% of roof area, refer.

### Freehold Flats

* Not accepted.

### Garden/Basement Flats

* Considered with adequate light, ventilation and demand.

### Ground Rent

* Not acceptable if escalation exceeds RPI.
* Not acceptable if initial ground rent exceeds £1,000 London / £250 outside London.

### Japanese Knotweed

* Category 3 & 4 require report and PCA/INNSA contractor.
* Retention until treatment commenced.

### Leasehold Term

* Minimum 85 years at purchase.
* Minimum 50 years remaining after redemption.
* Shorter may be considered if extended simultaneously.

### Non-Standard Construction

* Valuer to advise.
* Refer before submission.

### Purchase Adjoining Property

* Considered.
* Refer before submission.

### Restrictive Covenant

* May consider.
* Refer.

### Solar Panels

* Considered.
* Refer.

### Studio Apartments

* Minimum 35 SQM.

## SECTION 11 – JBSP

### Overview

* Immediate family member joint borrower.
* Not on title deeds.
* Cannot reside in property.
* All borrowers jointly and individually liable.

### Age

* Minimum 18.

### Maximum LTV

* Product dependent.
* Maximum 90% including fees.

### Maximum Applicants

* 4.

### Reverse JBSP

* Adult children support parents.
* Term based on child's age.

### Term

* Based on buyer's age if affordability achievable within 5 years.
