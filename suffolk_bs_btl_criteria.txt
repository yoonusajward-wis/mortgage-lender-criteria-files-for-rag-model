## Actual Rent vs. Valuer’s Rent

* We will **not** use the current rent received from the current tenant for affordability assessment **when it exceeds the valuer’s estimate**.

## Applicant

* **Minimum age:** 18 at application.
* **No maximum age** at the end of the mortgage term.
* **Maximum applicants:** 2.

## Applications with Sitting Tenants

* We can potentially accept cases of this nature. We can help where there is a sitting tenant as long as their tenancy agreement was a standard ASTA and they are not a tenant on any kind of Protected Tenancy basis.
* **Please speak to our Business Development Team.**

## Arrangement Fees

* Can be **added to the loan**, **above the maximum LTV** as long as the **overall loan fits within the ICR calculation**.

## Arrangements

* **Up to 75% LTV.**
* **Must have been satisfied 3 years.**

## Arrears

* We can consider **historic arrears** – **no arrears in the last 3 years**.

## Back to Back Transactions

* Property **must have been owned for 6 months** before we can accept a **remortgage** application.

## Bankruptcy

* Must have been **discharged for more than 3 years**.
* **Maximum 75% LTV.**
* **No further court orders** must have been registered.

## Basement Flats

* We can potentially lend on a **basement flat**. **Please refer** the property to our **Business Development Team**.
* **Maximum of 10 storeys**, including the basement flat.

## BTL ICR Calculation

* **Non-UK taxpayers:** **125%** of the stressed mortgage payment (either **pay rate + 2%** (Min **5.5%**) **or** the **5-year rate**).
* **Basic rate taxpayers:** **125%** of the stressed mortgage payment (either **pay rate + 2%** (Min **5.5%**) **or** the **5-year rate**).
* **Higher or additional rate taxpayers:** **145%** of the stressed mortgage payment (either **pay rate + 2%** (Min **5.5%**) **or** the **5-year rate**).
* **Joint application:** we use the **worst case scenario**.

## CCJs

* We can **ignore CCJs** that have been **satisfied for more than 12 months**.
* **Refer to our Business Development Team** on cases with: **more than 1 CCJ** or **CCJs over £1,000 up to 80% LTV**.

## Changes to Application Post Offer

* We can potentially allow **post-offer changes**; subject to **another underwrite** and potentially an **additional admin fee** (case by case).
* **Please contact our Business Development Team.**

## CIFAS

* We can consider customers **registered on the CIFAS register**. **Please refer** these cases.

## Coach House

* We can consider **coach houses** but will need to determine if the property is **freehold or leasehold**, and if there is an element of **flying freehold**.

## Concessionary Purchase

* Treated as a **transaction under value**.
* Applicants must provide a **deposit in line with our products** (e.g., **80% LTV**).

## Consumer BTL

* We can accept applications classed as **Consumer Buy to Let (CBTL)**.
* **They are treated the same as standard BTL applications.**

## Credit Reference Agency Used

* **TransUnion.**

## Criminal Convictions

* The conviction must be **spent** and **cannot be monetary related** (fraud, money laundering etc.).
* The applicant must be able to obtain **home insurance on standard terms**.

## Debt Management Plans

* Must have been **satisfied for 3 years**.
* **Maximum 75% LTV.**

## Debt Relief Order (DRO)

* **Up to 75% LTV.**
* Must have been **satisfied 3 years**.

## Defaults

* We can **ignore defaults** that have been **satisfied for more than 12 months**.
* **Refer to our helpdesk** on cases with: **more than 1 default** or **defaults over £1,000 up to 80% LTV** and **over £500 above 80% LTV**.

## Deposits

* **Gifted deposit** accepted for **UK applicants** (must be from **immediate family members**).
* **Gifted deposits are not accepted for Expat applications.**
* **Source of funds outside of the UK:** we can consider **expat applications** where the source of the deposit **does not originate from the UK**. It must originate from the **country that the applicant is domiciled in** and the **funds must be in the UK prior to completion**.
* **Vendor-paid deposits:** in some circumstances, vendors may pay the deposit for the applicant, **maximum 5%**. Acceptable where it is a **developer** paying the deposit as part of its **marketing activity** and where the **valuer confirms the full valuation** of the property. For a **small independent builder** we require **written confirmation** that paying the deposit is part of its marketing activity. The **applicant must also provide a 15% deposit**.
* **Capital raising on another property:** we can consider, but the **funds cannot have originated from us**.

## DWP / Housing Benefit Tenants

* **We can consider.** Please contact the **Business Development Team** to discuss further.

## EU Nationals with Pre-settled Status

* We can potentially lend to **EU, EEA (including Iceland, Norway and Liechtenstein)** and **Swiss** citizens if clients **evidence successful pre-settled status**.
* Must have been in the **UK for 12 months**.
* Must have **valid UK work permit/visa**, with **12 months remaining**.
* Must work in one of the following sectors: **Education, Healthcare, Finance & Banking, IT, Legal, Marketing Professionals, Science Research & Development, Energy Sector and Construction Industry Professionals (Not Trades)** e.g., **Architects**.

## EWS1 Requirements

* **EWS1 forms are required.**
* We will **not** lend on properties with **combustible cladding** or **materials on balconies** **without** an EWS1 form.
* **A1, A2 and B1** are acceptable, subject to **valuer’s comments**.
* **Maximum 10 storeys.**
* **EWS1 forms issued by Tri Fire Ltd will not be accepted.**

## Ex-Local Authority

* **Houses/bungalows:** **accepted**.
* **Flats/maisonettes:** **not accepted**.

## Expat

* **Expat BTL & Holiday Let:** accepted **up to 80% LTV**.
* Where an applicant is an Expat, the **UK taxpayer status** will be used to determine the **ICR required**.
* **Self-Employed** applicants are **not accepted** for Expat applications.
* **Most countries and currencies** are acceptable for Expat BTL & Expat Holiday Let (**excluding any UN sanctioned or war-torn areas**).
* **Regulated BTL:** due to full affordability assessment, applicants must be paid in one of the following currencies: **Sterling, Euro, Swiss Franc, Norwegian Krone, US Dollar, Canadian Dollar, Singapore Dollar, Hong Kong Dollar, UAE Dirham, Kuwaiti Dinar, Qatari Riyal, New Zealand Dollar, Danish Krone, Swedish Krona, Saudi Riyal**.
* **Minimum income:** **£25,000 UK£ equivalent**.
* **UK bank account:** Expat applicants **must hold** a UK bank account.
* **Address history:** **3 years** (UK or abroad).

## First-Time Buyer BTL

* We accept BTL applications from **First Time Buyers** for both **Expat and UK-based** applicants.
* **Standard BTL:** rent must cover the loan using our **ICR assessments**; we also conduct an **affordability check** (precautionary) alongside the ICR assessment.
* **Expat BTL:** rent must cover the loan using our ICR assessment and **underwriter approval** will be required. An **affordability assessment outside of this would not apply**.

## Flats & Apartments

* **Maximum 80% LTV for New Build Flats.**
* **80% LTV for standard flats.**
* **Maximum 10 storeys.**
* **Lift required if over 5 storeys.**
* **Ex-local authority flats not accepted.**
* **Minimum lease 85 years** upon application.
* **Flats above commercial** are accepted (subject to **suitable commercial nearby**) – **please contact the Business Development Team**.

## Foreign Nationals with Indefinite Leave to Remain

* **Accepted on standard criteria.**

## Foreign Nationals with Settled Status

* We can potentially lend to **EU Nationals** with a **settled status** under the **EU Settlement Scheme**.

## Foreign Nationals Without Indefinite Leave to Remain

* **Maximum 80% LTV.**
* We can potentially lend to **EU, EEA (including Iceland, Norway and Liechtenstein)** and **Swiss** citizens if clients **evidence successful pre-settled status**.
* Must have been in the **UK for 12 months**.
* Must have **valid UK work permit/visa**, with **12 months** remaining.
* Must work in one of the following sectors: **Education, Healthcare, Finance & Banking, IT, Legal, Marketing Professionals, Science Research & Development, Energy Sector and Construction Industry Professionals (Not Trades)** e.g., **Architects**.

## Grade 1 & 2 Listed Buildings

* We can potentially lend on properties that are **Grade 1 or 2 Listed**; **subject to valuer’s comments**.

## Holiday Let

* **Accepted** – **maximum 80% LTV (product dependent)**.
* **Minimum income:** **£25,000**.
* Must be an **owner occupier** – *(Owner occupier not required for Expat applications).*
* **Affordability:** based on **average of low, medium & high seasons × 30 weeks** (can be **35 weeks** if let for the last **2 years** on a holiday-let basis by the applicants). Divide by **12** to obtain **average monthly rental**, then apply **standard ICR calculations**.
* **Airbnb / other letting options:** accepted; **lettings agent letter** required to provide **known or anticipated letting value** (not Airbnb).
* **Letting agent letters** required to provide the **known or anticipated rental income**.
* **Personal use:** up to **60 days** permitted.
### Holiday Let Calculation
* **Average of High, Mid and Low weekly seasonal Rent**, **multiplied by 30 weeks**, **divided by 12** to get a **monthly rental** figure, then through the **buy to let calculator**.

## Income Made up Primarily of Benefits

* We can potentially accept applicants whose income is **primarily benefits**.
* Note: some income is **only taken at 50% or not at all**, and the applicant must meet **minimum £25,000 per annum**.

## Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVA)

* **Up to 75% LTV.**
* Must have been **satisfied 3 years**.

## Joint Borrower Sole Proprietor (JBSP)

* We can accept **JBSP on BTL** for **UK and Expat** applicants.
* **UK:** maximum **4 applicants**.
* **Expat:** maximum **2 applicants**.
* **Relationship:** joint borrower(s) should be **close family relative(s)**.
* **Deposit:** on JBSP UK and Expat cases, we will allow **any of the applicants** to provide the deposit (including the **non-proprietor**).
* If the **non proprietor is gifting the deposit** they **cannot reside** in the property.
* *We’re unable to offer **fee assisted legals** on joint borrower sole proprietor applications.*

## Let to Buy

* **Accepted.**

## Let to Rent

* We can consider where an applicant is looking to **let out their existing residential home** and **move into rented accommodation** – please **contact our Business Development Team**.

## Maximum Loan

* **£1 million.**
* For larger loans, **please contact our Business Development Team**.

## Maximum LTV

* **Maximum 80% LTV** for **BTL and Holiday Let.**
* (**Repeated statement confirmed**: Maximum 80% LTV for **BTL & Holiday Let** applications.)

## Maximum Term

* **30 years.**

## Minimum Income

* **Minimum £25,000** for **UK and Expat** applicants (if joint, **one applicant must earn a minimum £25,000**).

## Minimum Property Value

* **£100,000.**

## Minimum Term

* **6 years.**

## Missed Payments

* There **cannot** be any **missed payments in the last 12 months**.

## Mortgage Offers

* **Standard offer validity – 6 months.**
* **New Build offer validity – 6 months.**
* Mortgage offers **can be extended** – **please contact us** if an extension is required.

## Moving in with Family

* We can potentially consider a **BTL remortgage** where the property is **currently the applicant’s main residence** but **will now be let** and the applicant will be **moving in with family**.

## New Build Warranties

* **Acceptable New Build Warranties:**
**NHBS Guarantee; Premier Guarantee Scheme; Building Life Plans; Build-Zone (includes BuildCare); Checkmate (Castle 10); ICW; LABC; Professional Consultants Certificate (see full list below).**
* **Acceptable Professional Consultants Certificate:**
**Architects certificate accepted.** Where supervised by a **qualified person**, the Conveyancer must ensure the Supervisor is a member of one of:
* **Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (FRICS or MRICS)**
* **Institute of Structural Engineers (F.I.Struct.E or M.I.Struct.E)**
* **Chartered Institute of Building (FCIOB or MCIOB)**
* **Architectural and Surveying Institute (FASI or MASI)**
* **Association of Building Engineers (FB.Eng or MB.Eng)**
* **Chartered Institute of Architectural Technologists**
* **Architect registered with the Architects Registration Board (ARB)** (Must be registered with ARB even if also a member of another institution, e.g., **RIBA**)
* **Institute of Civil Engineers (FICE or MICE)**
**Note:** The Conveyancer must obtain a copy of their **Professional Indemnity insurance** and must ensure that the policy provides **cover for a period of not less than six years**.

## Next to Commercial

* We can potentially accept. **Please contact our Business Development Team** to discuss.

## Non Owner Occupier BTL

* We can potentially accept BTL applications from **Non Owner Occupiers**. **Please contact our Business Development Team**.
* **Holiday Let** applications would need to be **owner occupiers** for us to be able to lend.

## Overpayment Facility

* **Fee-free overpayments up to 50%** of the **original loan** (subject to product availability).

## Payday Loans

* **Please refer** details to **Business Development Team**, including **frequency, value and time period** of payday loans being used.

## Portability

* The majority of our mortgages are **portable as standard** (please check **product criteria**).

## Portfolio Landlords

* We’re **unable to accept** applications from **portfolio landlords**.
* Applicants can have a **maximum of 3 properties** (including properties held in a **limited company**).
* This includes **unencumbered** as well as **mortgaged** properties.
* *(Repeated confirmation of the same rule applies.)*
* **Applicants over 75** will need to seek **Independent Legal Advice** to ensure they understand the implications of the mortgage contract and potential issues of lending into later life on benefits and in the event of needing long-term care.

## Private Local Authority Searches

* We can potentially accept a **private local authority search**, subject to:
* A **suitably qualified search agent** carries out the personal search and has **indemnity insurance** that adequately protects us **or** the **search insurance policy** adequately protects us.
* The **Solicitor** must confirm that the **title is good and marketable**.
* Personal searches are subject to the search firm **subscribing to the Search Code** monitored by the **Property Code Compliance Board**.

## Properties Needing Refurbishment

* We can consider a **BTL application** where the property **requires refurbishment** before it is let.
* **LTV** will be based on **current property value**.
* **Notes:**
* **Detailed costings/quotes** requested at application.
* Property must be in a **liveable/mortgageable condition** on application.
* Only **non-structural works** permitted.
* **Savings** must be evidenced to cover the **first 6 months of mortgage payments**.
* **Please contact our Business Development Team** to discuss further.

## Property Let Via Airbnb (Holiday Let)

* We can accept **holiday let** applications where the property will be let via **Airbnb or other letting options**.
* Properties marketed via Airbnb will be subject to our **existing rental coverage assessment**, requiring a **lettings agent** (**not Airbnb**) to provide the **known or anticipated letting value**, exactly the same as a standard holiday let.

## Property Location

* We will lend throughout **England & Wales**.

## Regulated BTL

* **Acceptable** for **UK and Expat** applicants.
* We will carry out a **full affordability assessment** instead of a rental calculation.
* See **Expat section** for acceptable currencies for **Regulated BTL**.

## Remortgage or Additional Borrowing Purpose

* **Home improvements – accepted.**
* **Purchase of BTL or Holiday Let – accepted** (≤ **80%**: no simultaneous completion; **> 80%**: simultaneous completion required).
* **Purchase property in Limited company** – must be an **SPV** and all applicants must be **named directors and shareholders**.
* **Repay help to buy and let the property – accepted.**
* **Buy out another party – accepted.**
* **Debt consolidation – not accepted.**
* **Extending a lease – accepted.**
* **Raising money to gift – accepted** (**£100,000 or over** need to be **referred**).
* **Paying off residential mortgage – accepted.**
* **HMO conversion back to standard property – potentially can accept** (contact BDM).
* **Medical expenses – we can consider** (contact BDM).
* **School Fees – we can consider** (contact BDM).

## Restrictions

* We may be able to accept properties with **agricultural restrictions** – **maximum 50% LTV**. **Please contact our Business Development Team.**

## Returning to UK – Employed

* We can potentially consider applications from applicants **returning to the UK** and going straight into **employment with the same company**.
* **Please contact the Business Development Team** (case by case).

## Solar Panels

* **Accepted** – if **leased**, must have the **mortgage lender repossession break clause** in the contract.

## Tenancy

* **Assured Shorthold Tenancy.**
* **Maximum 36 months AST.**
* **Minimum 6 months AST.**
* **Maximum 4 individuals** accepted on a tenancy.
* The property must still be let via a **standard ASTA agreement** and **not** be classed as an **HMO**.
* We can accept up to **4 individuals**, **providing they are a family unit**. If they are **unrelated professionals** then **maximum 3 individuals** on the tenancy.

## Topslicing

* **Not accepted currently.**

## Visas

* We can potentially consider applicants that hold a **Family Visa** – **please discuss** with our **Business Development Team**.
* We can potentially consider applicants that hold a **Skilled worker Visa (previously Tier 2 Visa)**. Because of the potential short-term nature of residency, the applicant(s) should be of a **professional standing** and working in one of the following sectors: **Education, Healthcare, Finance & Banking, IT, Legal, Marketing Professionals, Science Research & Development, Energy Sector and Construction Industry Professionals (Not Trades)** e.g., **Architects**.
