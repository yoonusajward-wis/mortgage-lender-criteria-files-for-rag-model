# SECTION 1 – APPLICANT ELIGIBILITY

---

## Rule: Age of Applicants

Minimum age at application: 18 years
Maximum age at end of repayment term: 75 years

---

## Rule: Number of Applicants

Maximum applicants per application: 2

---

## Rule: Type of Applicants

Allowed:

* Individual applicants
* Limited companies

---

## Rule: Residency Requirements

Applications accepted from applicants residing in:

* Bahrain
* Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA)
* Kuwait
* Qatar
* United Arab Emirates
* Oman

---

# SECTION 2 – CREDIT HISTORY

---

## Rule: Bankruptcy, IVA, Possession

Not acceptable:

* Bankruptcy
* IVA
* Voluntary enforced possession

---

## Rule: CCJs

Not acceptable:

* Any CCJ within the last 3 years

Previous CCJs:

* Must be satisfied before application

---

## Rule: Mortgage Payment Conduct

Not acceptable:

* Any missed mortgage or property finance payments in the last 12 months

---

## Rule: Unsecured Missed Payments

Maximum allowed:

* 2 missed or late payments in last 24 months
* Must be up to date for last 6 months

Includes:

* Mobile phone / communications arrears

---

## Rule: Defaults

Not acceptable:

* Any defaults in the 12 months prior to application

Acceptable:

* Maximum 1 default
* Up to £300
* Between months 13–36
* Must be satisfied before submission

---

## Rule: Credit Report

Requirement:

* Up-to-date UK credit report required for all applicants with an active UK footprint

---

# SECTION 3 – FINANCE PURPOSE

---

## Rule: Acceptable Finance Purpose

Allowed:

* Purchase
* Refinance
* Capital-raising
* Debt consolidation

---

# SECTION 4 – DEPOSIT

---

## Rule: Gifted Deposit

Gifted deposits are accepted.

Condition:

* Must be from a family member

No further donor restrictions are specified in this document.

---

# SECTION 5 – INCOME

---

## Rule: Income Multiples

Maximum Facility-to-Income (FTI) ratio: 4.5

This is applied to total combined income of all applicants.

---

## Rule: Sources of Income Considered

### Employed Income

Accepted:

* Salary
* Bonus (50% of average of last 3 years)
* Commission (50% of average of last 3 years)
* Overtime (50% of average of last 3 years)

### Self-Employed Income

Accepted:

* Personal income (salary)
* Dividends (average of last 3 years)

### Other Income

Accepted:

* Rental income (100% of rental income after deduction of finance payments and 25% allowance for letting costs and void periods)
* Investment income (average of last 3 years)
* Government allowances (Kuwait only): man-power, marriage, housing, car allowance, town allowance
* Pension retirement income

### Foreign Currency Income

Income not in GBP:

* Converted using XE Calculator ([www.xe.com](http://www.xe.com))
* No additional haircut applied
* Must be received from acceptable country list

# SECTION 6 – PROPERTY CRITERIA

---

## Rule: Property Location

Allowed:

* England
* Wales

---

## Rule: Minimum Property Value

Outside London:

* Minimum £150,000

Inside London (within M25):

* Minimum £350,000

---

## Rule: Property Type – Acceptable

Allowed:

* Standard residential property
* New-build (must have NHBC or equivalent warranty)
* Leasehold (minimum 50 years remaining at end of term)
* Properties above commercial premises (subject to assessment of business type)
* EPC rating A, B, C, D or E

---

## Rule: Property Type – Not Acceptable

Not acceptable:

* Studio flats
* Freehold flats/maisonettes
* Mobile homes
* Houseboats
* Grade 1 listed buildings

---

# SECTION 7 – AFFORDABILITY ASSESSMENT

---

## Rule: Affordability Methodology

Affordability is assessed by deducting:

1. Basic essential expenditure
2. Basic quality of living costs
3. Committed expenditure

From total allowable income to calculate disposable income.

---

## Rule: Basic Essential Expenditure

Includes:

* Food and housekeeping
* Gas, electricity, heating
* Water
* Telephone
* Council tax
* Buildings insurance
* Ground rent and service charge (leasehold)
* Essential travel

---

## Rule: Basic Quality of Living Costs

Includes:

* Clothing
* Basic recreation
* Personal goods
* School/childcare fees
* Maintenance payments

---

## Rule: Committed Expenditure

Includes:

* Mortgage payments (all obligors’ properties)
* Personal finance
* Credit cards

These commitments must continue after finance is provided.

---

## Rule: Bank Statement Evidence

Bank statements submitted as evidence must include statements from the applicant’s country of residence and, where applicable, from the United Kingdom (UK).

---

# SECTION 8 – STRESS TEST

---

## Rule: Stress Testing – Residential

Fixed term less than 5 years:

* Profit rate + 2%

Fixed term 5 years or more:

* Profit rate
