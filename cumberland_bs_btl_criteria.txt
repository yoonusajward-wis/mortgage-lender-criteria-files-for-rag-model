# Cumberland Building Society – Buy to Let Criteria

**LENDER:** Cumberland Building Society

**DOCUMENT TYPE:** Lending Criteria
**LAST UPDATED:** 2025
**FOR INTERMEDIARY USE ONLY**

**Source:** [Buy to Let – intermediaries](https://www.cumberland.co.uk/intermediaries/buy-to-let)

---

## SECTION 1 – APPLICANT ELIGIBILITY

### Residency

* Individual borrowers must be resident in the UK.
* Limited Company borrowers must be incorporated in the UK and beneficial owners must be resident in the UK.

### Income Requirements

**Amateur Landlords (Individuals & Partnerships):**
* Annual income of above £25,000 (or £50,000 with 100% rental coverage where top slicing is required).

**Amateur Landlords (Limited Companies):**
* Annual income of above £25,000 (or £50,000 with 100% rental coverage where top slicing is required).

**Portfolio Landlords (Individuals, Partnerships & Limited Companies):**
* Annual income of above £25,000 (or £50,000 with 100% rental coverage where top slicing is required).
* Background portfolio cannot exceed 20 properties.
* Maximum 8 individual Society BTL loans per borrower.

---

## SECTION 2 – PROPERTY ELIGIBILITY

### Tenure

* Properties can be freehold or leasehold.
* Minimum of 85 years remaining on the lease at the start of the mortgage.
* Minimum of 50 years remaining on the lease at maturity.

### Construction

* Unable to mortgage property which is a temporary or movable structure.

### Tenancy Agreement

* Tenancy agreement should be on an Assured Shorthold Tenancy basis, or in Scotland on a Private Residential Tenancy basis.
* Maximum term of 6 months.
* Any existing tenancy agreement must have commenced after 1 March 1997.

### Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)

* All properties must have an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) with a rating of E or higher, or a suitable exemption, for properties in England and Wales.

---

## SECTION 3 – FEES

* Arrangement fees will apply.
* Free valuations up to £750,000 loan amount, on selected products only.
* Legal fees are met by the borrower.

---

## SECTION 4 – RENTAL INCOME AND AFFORDABILITY

### 4.1 Amateur Landlords (Individuals or Partnerships)

**Products with a term less than 5 years:**
* Annual rental income must be a minimum of 145% of annual mortgage interest at a stressed rate of 5.5%, or product rate +2% if higher.

**Products fixed for 5 years or more:**
* Annual rental income must be a minimum of 145% of annual mortgage interest calculated using the product rate.

**Remortgages with no additional borrowing:**
* Annual rental income must be a minimum of 145% of annual mortgage interest calculated using the product rate.

**Capital & Interest mortgage:**
* DCR must be evidenced at 125% coverage, with a further check that ICR is met at 145%. Both DCR and ICR must be met.
