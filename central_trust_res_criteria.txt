## SECTION 1 – OVERVIEW & KEY CRITERIA

### Overview

* Residential mortgage range designed to help the underserved. No strict automation; each application is assessed by our team. We consider self-employed, probation period, and historical or recent adverse credit.
* Full UK coverage: England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

### Employment

* We accept employed, self-employed, contractors, agency workers and zero hour contracts.

### Impaired Credit

* We consider all credit profiles including CCJs, defaults, missed payments and mortgage arrears.

### Non-Standard Construction

* No LTV restrictions, as long as the property is mortgageable and suitable to the area.

### Income

* We accept benefit income (e.g. PIP and Universal Credit), pension income and child maintenance.

### Property Value

* Minimum property value: £75,000.

## SECTION 2 – FIRST CHARGE RESIDENTIAL

### Product Characteristics

* First charge on main residence.
* Loan sizes: £10,000 to £250,000.
* Loan to value: up to 80%.
* Mortgage terms: 3 to 30 years.
* Fixed and variable rates available.
* Capital repayment mortgage only (no interest only or part and part).
* Unlimited overpayments allowed.
* Product fees can be paid up front or added to the mortgage.
* Full eligibility criteria: request from your Business Development Manager or access via intermediary portal.

### Target Market

* Homeowners resident in England, Wales, Scotland or Northern Ireland with equity in their residential property, who may have an imperfect credit history and who have a borrowing need.
* Not designed for: non-homeowners; those with no or little equity; severely credit impaired (even if able to pay); those who do not meet lending or property criteria; those requiring interest only or part and part.

---

## SECTION 3 – SECOND CHARGE RESIDENTIAL

### Product Characteristics

* Second charge secured on main residence.
* Loan sizes: £10,000 to £250,000.
* Loan to value: up to 90%.
* Mortgage terms: 3 to 30 years.
* Fixed and variable rates available.
* Capital repayment mortgage only (no interest only or part and part).
* Unlimited overpayments allowed.
* Product fees can be paid up front or added to the mortgage.
* Full eligibility criteria: request from your Business Development Manager or access via intermediary portal.

### Target Market

* Same target group as First Charge: homeowners with equity, imperfect credit history, borrowing need. Same exclusions (non-homeowners, no/little equity, severely credit impaired, interest only/part and part not available).

## SECTION 4 – DISTRIBUTION & APPLICATIONS

### Distribution Strategy

* Available through Direct and Intermediary channels.
* Intermediary distribution via: Directly Authorised Brokers/Intermediaries; Networks; Mortgage Clubs.
* All Intermediaries must be accredited with Central Trust Limited.
* All applications on a fully advised basis only.

### Customer Needs & Objectives (Target Market)

* Access to finance not based on credit score.
* Looking to repair credit file to enable future borrowing from high street lenders.
* Consolidation of secured and unsecured credit into a more manageable monthly payment.
* Loan size or term requirements that may not be available via unsecured products.

---

## SECTION 5 – VULNERABLE CUSTOMERS

* Product may include customers with characteristics of vulnerability or those who may experience vulnerability during the lifetime of the mortgage.
* CTL is mindful of the FCA's four key drivers: Health; Life Events; Resilience; Capability.
* Framework includes: staff education and training; dedicated collections specialists; customer service and communications; affordability assessment at application; monitoring. Customers with certain disabilities may have reasonable adjustments but will not receive a different outcome to others within the target market.
* Intermediaries must comply with obligations to treat customers in vulnerable circumstances fairly.
