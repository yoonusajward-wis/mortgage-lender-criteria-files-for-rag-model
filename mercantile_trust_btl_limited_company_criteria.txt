## SECTION 1 – PRODUCT OVERVIEW

- Geographic coverage: England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.
- Loan range: £10,000 to £500,000.
- Security: 1st and 2nd charges up to 75% LTV.
- Maximum 5 loans per applicant; maximum total £1,000,000.
- Portfolio landlords: no background property stress testing; unlimited BTL properties in background accepted.
- Available on all rental properties including Holiday Lets, HMOs, MUFBs and Student Lets.
- DSS tenants accepted.
- Property cross-charging available for the same product.
- No heavy refurbishment or development.
- Consumer Buy to Lets: not accepted.

## SECTION 5 – APPLICANT AND LIMITED COMPANY CRITERIA

- Maximum 4 directors per limited company application.
- No SIC code requirement.
- No rate loading for limited company applications.
- Applicant age: 18–85 at end of term (applies to directors and guarantors).
- ID verification: Nivo ID via the Mercantile Trust app; full 3-year proof of UK residency required for directors and guarantors.
- Mortgage history: minimum 6 months required.
- Income: no minimum or maximum income requirement (unless first-time buyer or first-time landlord).
- First-time buyer or landlord: minimum income £22,500 and maximum LTV 65%.
- Portfolio landlords: unlimited BTL properties in background accepted; no portfolio stress testing.
- Tenant restrictions: tenants must not be blood relatives of the applicant (parents, grandparents, siblings or children).

---

## SECTION 6 – AFFORDABILITY

- DSCR: 125% — affordability calculator includes 1% stress rate.
- Rental income evidence: valid ASTs for BTL properties plus latest bank statement showing rental income.
- Rent calculation: the lowest of AST rent, valuation or AVM is used (refer if market evidence is available) — except HMOs, MUFBs and Student Lets.
- Income sources: BTL surplus and external income used, including self-employed income and benefit/pension income.
- Untenanted properties: proof that 3 months' rental payments can be covered is required for untenanted property purchases or first charge light refurbishment.

## SECTION 7 – PROPERTY TYPES

### Standard Buy to Let

- Standard BTL criteria as outlined above.

### HMO (House in Multiple Occupation)

- HMO licence or application to be provided if required.
- ASTs required for all tenants (individual agreements or a joint AST covering all tenants).
- Valuation figure must be based on the residential value.
- Rent from ASTs and bank statement used.
- No EPC required.

### Holiday Lets

- Directors/guarantors must own their residence.
- 100% of current yearly holiday income accepted from an SA302 or accounts certificate; if owned less than 1 year, 50% of projected income from a letting agent is accepted.
- Surplus income or personal income may be used to support the application.
- No EPC required.

### Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs)

- 1st and 2nd charge available.
- Over 4 units: please refer.
- ASTs required for all tenants; rent from ASTs and bank statement used.

### Student Lets

- ASTs required for all tenants (individual agreements or a joint AST).
- If AST term is for a university year, rent is averaged over 12 months.
- Rent from ASTs and bank statement used.

### Homeowner Business Loans (Secured on Main Residence)

- 2nd charge only.
- Majority of the loan must be for business purposes — evidence required.
- Minimum loan: £25,000.
- No EPC required.

## SECTION 8 – ADVERSE CREDIT

Adverse credit assessment applies to directors and guarantors of the limited company. Status units are applied as follows:

### Mortgage or Secured Loan (Maximum 1 on application)

- 1 status unit per arrear in the last 12 months.
- Ignored: part or late payments made in the same calendar month; outstanding arrears prior to the last 12 months (units calculated according to the monthly instalment).

### Defaults

- 1 status unit per default (no maximum amount).
- Ignored: sums under £300; satisfied sums under £3,000; all defaults over 12 months old.

### CCJs

- 1 status unit per CCJ (no maximum amount).
- Ignored: sums under £300; satisfied sums under £3,000; all CCJs over 12 months old.

### Credit Cards, Unsecured Loans, Utilities (3 or More Payments Down)

- 1 unit per credit card, unsecured loan or utility.
- Ignored: sums under £300; accounts currently 2 or fewer payments unpaid regardless of previous conduct.

### Other

- Ignored: mail order and communications (mobile phone) missed payments; discharged bankruptcies or settled IVAs over 3 years old.

## SECTION 9 – PROPERTY AND VALUATION CRITERIA

### Property Requirements

- Minimum property size: 30 sq.m. (except individual rooms in HMOs, MUFBs and Student Lets).
- Minimum property value: £75,000.
- LTV calculation: lower of market value or purchase price.
- EPC required: minimum rating E (not required for HMOs, Holiday Lets or Homeowner Business Loans).
- Current buildings insurance policy required.
- Most construction types accepted; no holiday villages or parks.
- High-rise flats in a block with over 6 floors: please refer.

### LTV Restrictions by Property Type

- Ex-council flats and maisonettes: maximum 75% LTV at £150,000 or above valuation; maximum 65% LTV below £150,000 valuation.
- High-value properties: maximum 75% LTV up to £1,000,000; maximum 70% LTV £1,000,001–£1,500,000; maximum 65% LTV above £1,500,000.
- Freehold flats: maximum 65% LTV.

### Valuation Requirements

**Full valuation always required for:**
- Purchases.
- Self-builds.
- Holiday lets.
- Ex-council flats.
- Properties over £500,000 above 65% LTV (outside the M25).
- Properties over £750,000 above 65% LTV (inside the M25).

### Valuation Types by LTV

- Up to 65% LTV, net loan up to £200,000: Hometrack AVM (minimum confidence level 4.0) or drive-by/full valuation by approved valuer.
- Above 65% up to 70% LTV, net loan up to £150,000: Hometrack AVM (minimum confidence level 5.0) or full valuation by approved valuer.
- Above 70% up to 75% LTV, net loan up to £100,000: Hometrack AVM (minimum confidence level 6.0) or full valuation by approved valuer.

- Hometrack AVM to be carried out by the broker.
- Drive-by or full valuations to be instructed via Pure Panel Management, DM Hall or Gateway.
