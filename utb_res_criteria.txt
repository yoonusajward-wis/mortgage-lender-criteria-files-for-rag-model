**Last Updated:** September 2025

## Overview

We understand that each person is unique and that many cases are too complex for the 'High Street'. Where customers have circumstances that require a bit more flexibility, you need a lender who'll take a different view.

---

## Customers We Love to Help

- Owners of unusual properties or construction types / unencumbered properties
- Professionals and those with complex income
- Self-employed and contractors
- Borrowers with impaired credit
- Buy to let landlords, individuals or Ltd Co SPVs with HMOs, MUBs or holiday lets
- Second charge residential borrowers
- Mortgage Prisoners
- Product Transfer options are available for all products (eligible existing customers are offered these options within 90 days of expiry of their current fixed rate)

## Flexible Terms and Approach to Adverse Credit

- Normal LTV applied for non standard properties
- Lend for any legal purpose (including some business purposes)
- On consolidation applications all credit card, mail order and store card funds sent to the client to pay rather than UTB sending cheques or electronic transfer
- Mail order and communications arrears and defaults ignored
- CCJs and defaults under £300 ignored
- Missed mortgage payments made within 30 days of due date ignored
- 1 year discharged for IVAs
- Product range available which allows 1 missed mortgage payment within 12 months
- Prime Plus & Prime product ranges can be used if a customer has unsecured arrears which have been brought up to date and evidenced

# Mortgages and Second Charge Loans – Detailed Criteria Guide

## Property Requirements

**Mortgage History**

* A minimum of 12 months’ mortgage history is required.

**Location**

* Properties must be located in England, Wales, or Mainland Scotland.

**Main Property Exclusions**

* Properties valued under £90,000 with < 85% LTV.
* Properties under £125,000 with > 85% LTV (first charge).
* Properties under £150,000 with > 85% LTV (second charge).
* Commercial properties.
* Right to Buy within 4 years of purchase.
* Agricultural properties.

**Buildings Insurance**

* No evidence required.

**Borrower Legal Representation**

* For remortgage and unencumbered secured loans: not required.
* For purchases: minimum of two solicitors in practice.

**Purchase Deposits**

* Equity from an existing property.
* Personal savings (must show build-up over time).
* ISA in customer’s name.
* Gifted deposit from blood relatives or step-relatives.
* Gifted equity with SDL tax paid at full OMV.

---

## Valuation Requirements

**First Charge Products**

* **Purchase**: Full valuation required.
* **Remortgage**:

  * Hometrack AVM up to 75% LTV, £250k gross loan, confidence score ≥ 5.0, max property value £1m.
  * Drive-by valuation up to 75% LTV, £500k gross loan.
  * Internal mortgage valuation required for all loans > 85% LTV.
  * Specialist reports required if property not purchased/remortgaged in the last 5 years.
* **Unencumbered**:

  * Hometrack AVM up to 30% LTV, £50k gross loan, confidence score ≥ 5.0, max property value £1m.
  * Drive-by valuation up to 75% LTV, £100k gross loan.
  * Internal mortgage valuation up to maximum LTV and gross loan.

**Solicitor Details**

* Customer may choose own solicitor for purchase, but firm must have ≥ 2 partners.
* Indemnity insurance is required for gifted equity.

**Second Charge Products**

* **Hometrack**

  * Max £150k gross loan:

    * Up to 85% LTV (min confidence score 6.5)
    * Up to 80% LTV (score 6)
    * Up to 75% LTV (score 5)
    * Up to 60% LTV (score 4)
    * Up to 40% LTV (score 3)
  * Max £250k gross loan: up to 75% LTV, score ≥ 5.
  * Max property value: £1m.
  * Properties > £1m: up to 75% LTV, max £150k loan, min score 6.
* **Drive-By Valuation**:

  * Up to 75% LTV, £50k gross loan.
  * Up to 65% LTV, £100k gross loan.
* **Internal Valuation**:

  * Allowed up to maximum LTV and gross loan.
* Specialist reports not required if purchased/remortgaged in last 5 years.

## ID and Residence

* Documentary proof of ID required for all applications (usually through UTB Chat Hub).
* If digital ID cannot be provided, applicants must take Independent Legal Advice from a pre-approved firm with at least 2 partners.

---

## Loan Purpose

* Purchase or remortgage only (not available to first-time buyers).
* Capital raise allowed for most legal purposes, excluding bankruptcy avoidance.
* Debt consolidation limited to £25,000 and only for home improvements completed in the last 6 months.

---

## Applicant Profile

* Age: 25–85 (at end of term).
* Minimum income: £50,000 (application).
* Minimum security property value: £100,000.
* Affordability assessed on the interest-only monthly repayment.
* An Interest-Only Declaration Form must be signed, confirming repayment vehicle(s).
* London postcodes available in packaging guide.

---

## Loan-to-Value (LTV)

* Maximum 70% LTV for downsize.
* Maximum 75% LTV for all other repayment vehicles.

---

## Repayment Vehicles

Multiple repayment vehicles are allowed, including:

* Downsize: min equity £250,000 (London) or £150,000 (nationally).
* Sale of investment property (85% of net equity).
* Cash savings (90% of current value).
* UK ISA (90% of current value).
* Pension (100% of tax-free PCLS amount).
* Endowment (75% of projected lump sum).
* Unit Trusts, OEICs, or investment bonds (75% of current portfolio value).

---

## Income and Affordability

**Minimum Income**

* **First charge**:

  * £15k if LTV ≤ 85%.
  * £40k if LTV > 85%.
* **Second charge**: £15k for all applications.

**Acceptable Sources of Income**

* Employed: 6 months in current role, or 3 months if 12 months continuous employment and not in probation.
* 100% of guaranteed income (basic pay, car allowance, shift allowance).
* 75% of regular income (bonus, overtime, commission) with last 2 payslips + latest P60.
* Bonuses:

  * If ≥ £10k: 75% of average over last 2 years.
  * If < £10k: 50% of average over last 2 years.
* Self-employed (sole traders/partnerships): 100% of net profit.
* Limited companies: proportionate % of latest year’s net profit (based on shareholding) + salary. Retained profits and director loans not accepted.
* Pension: 100% of regular income.
* Family/child benefits: 100%, provided total income ≥ £25k.
* Unacceptable: any other benefits or income not in GBP.

---

## Proof of Income

* **Employed**: last 2 payslips (computerised) or 2 manual payslips + P60/bank statement.
* **Self-employed (sole trader/partnership)**: last 2 years’ Tax Calculations + corresponding Tax Year Overviews + latest business bank statement (TYO not required if 100% submitted).
* **Limited company**: accountant’s certificate OR last 2 years’ accounts, latest Tax Calculation + TYO, + latest business bank statement.
* **Pension**: last pension statement + latest bank statement.
* **Maintenance, benefits, tax credits**: Court order/CSA documentation or entitlement letter + bank statement.
* **Trust income**: Trust Deed + latest bank statement.

---

## Affordability Assessment

* Must be assessed on UTB portal.
* Assessment must be sustainable and evidenced for the entire loan term.
* Debt consolidation (status 1 and 2) must be ≤ 1.5x total gross income.

---

## Age

* Minimum: 18 years old (25 for interest-only).
* Maximum: 85 years at end of term.

# Status Definitions – First & Second Charge Products

For **Prime Plus and Prime (less than 85% LTV):**

* **Unsecured:** Must be currently up to date.
* **Secured Loans and Mortgages:** No arrears in the last 3 months, none in the last 12 months, and no outstanding arrears.
* **CCJs:** None in the last 24 months. CCJs under £300 may be ignored.
* **Defaults:** None in the last 24 months. Defaults under £300 may be ignored.

For **Prime Plus and Prime (greater than 85% LTV):**

* **Unsecured:** Must be currently up to date.
* **Secured Loans and Mortgages:** No arrears in the last 3 months, none in the last 12 months, and no outstanding arrears.
* **CCJs:** None in the last 36 months. CCJs under £300 may be ignored.
* **Defaults:** None in the last 36 months. Defaults under £300 may be ignored.

For **Near Prime (less than 85% LTV):**

* **Unsecured:** Can be ignored if debts are being consolidated.
* **Secured Loans and Mortgages:** No arrears in the last 3 months, a maximum of 1 arrear in the last 12 months, and no more than 1 outstanding arrear.
* **CCJs:** A maximum of 1 in the last 24 months. CCJs under £300 may be ignored.
* **Defaults:** A maximum of 1 in the last 24 months. Defaults under £300 may be ignored.

**Additional Notes (applies to all plans):**

* Mail order and communications debts are ignored.
* Previous conduct will still affect the applicant’s credit score.
