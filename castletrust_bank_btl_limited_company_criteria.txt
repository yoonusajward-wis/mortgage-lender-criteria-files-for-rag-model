## SECTION 1 – OVERVIEW & LENDING PARAMETERS

### Overview

* Castle Trust Bank offers Buy to Let (BTL) mortgages for limited companies, including Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) and trading companies.
* Flexible BTL products for various investment strategies: standard residential portfolios, Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs), Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs), and holiday lets.
* Limited company lending: SPVs and trading companies accepted. TermTen product: 10-year term with 5-year fixed rate. Interest-only payments available. Portfolio landlords accepted with no maximum portfolio size restrictions. Personal guarantees required from all directors and shareholders (see Applicant Requirements).

### Where We Lend

* **Location:** England and Wales only.

### Loan-to-Value (LTV)

* **Maximum LTV:** Up to 75% (net).
* **TermTen Product:** Up to 77% gross LTV (including net loan and arrangement fee).

### Loan Amounts

* **Minimum loan amount:** £150,000 (may vary by product).
* **TermTen Product:** Minimum £200,000.
* **Maximum loan amount:** £2,000,000 (may vary by product).
* **TermTen Product:** Maximum £10,000,000 (gross).

### Loan Terms

* **Term:** 10 years (TermTen Product).
* **Fixed rate period:** 5 years (TermTen Product).
* **Repayment:** Interest-only available.

## SECTION 2 – APPLICANT REQUIREMENTS

### Company Types

* **Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs)** – Non-trading companies established solely for holding and letting residential investment properties.
* **Trading companies** – Companies engaged in trading activities.
* **Limited companies** – Standard limited companies.
* **First-time landlords** – Accepted.
* **Portfolio landlords** – Accepted (no maximum portfolio size restrictions).
* **Complex structures** – Considered on a case-by-case basis.

### Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) Codes

* **Acceptable SIC Codes for SPVs:** 68100 (Buying and selling of own real estate); 68209 (Other letting and operating of own or leased real estate); 68320 (Management of real estate on a fee or contract basis).
* SPVs must be non-trading and established solely for holding and letting residential investment properties.

### Personal Guarantees

* Personal guarantees required from all shareholders holding more than 25% of shares.
* Any shareholders holding less than 25% share of the company do not have to appear on the application.
* Corporate borrowers must provide personal guarantees from all shareholders holding more than 25% of shares.
* If company has fewer than 4 directors or shareholders, all must be included in the mortgage application.

### Income Requirements

* No specified minimum income; applicants must have demonstrable income and a suitable repayment strategy.
* Rental income must meet Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR) requirements.
* Company income may be considered for trading companies.

### Age Requirements

* **Minimum applicant age:** 21 years.
* **Maximum applicant age at end of term:** 85 years.

## SECTION 3 – PROPERTY CRITERIA

### Property Location and Value

* **Location:** England and Wales only.
* **Minimum security value:** £75,000 (TermTen & Bridge products).
* **Minimum property value:** £135,000 (may vary by product).

### Property Types

* **Standard buy to let properties** – Residential properties let on Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST).
* **Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs)** – Small and large HMOs accepted (TermTen Product).
* **Multi-Unit Freehold Blocks (MUFBs)** – Accepted (TermTen Product).
* **Holiday lets** – Accepted (affordability based on holiday let income, not AST).
* **Serviced Apartments** – Acceptable.
* **Airbnb** – Acceptable.
* **Portfolio properties** – Multiple properties considered.
* **Aggregated value on blocks of flats** – Up to 20 units (TermTen Product).
* All subject to valuation and standard property criteria.

### Commercial Property

* Commercial property is not acceptable for any applications unless residential planning is in place.

### Property Requirements

* Must be let on an Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST) or equivalent.
* AST period: between 6 and 12 months required (longer terms by negotiation).
* Limited Company lets acceptable where the tenant is a named individual.
* Not occupied by the applicant or their immediate family.
* Subject to valuation and standard property criteria.

### Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)

* Properties with no valid EPC rating or rating below E are not acceptable (unless exempt).
* EPC exemptions may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

### Property Construction

* **Acceptable:** Standard construction (masonry walls solid or cavity minimum 200mm thickness; modern timber post 1975 or steel framed post 1998 with masonry outer cladding and suitable new home warranty; standard slate or tiled roof; flat roof warranty in place; thatch considered individually; modern methods of construction).
* **Unacceptable:** All non-traditional construction types; steel framed with non-masonry cladding; solid masonry less than 200mm thickness; Wimpey No Fines; Laing Easi Form post 1945; Croswall; agricultural properties; concrete (No Fines, Cheshire Brine, Mundic, Precast Reinforced Concrete (PRC)); cladded blocks without satisfactory (A1, A2, B1) EWS1 form; cross wall construction pre 1970; Holiday Parks; Hostels; House boat; Laing Easi Form pre 1945; Log cabin; Mobile home; Single Skin; Timber Frame (period properties considered on an individual basis).
* The Bank will not normally lend on non-standard construction.

### Ex-Local Authority Properties

* An ex-local authority property must be located in an area or block where 50% or more of the properties are privately owned.

### Minimum Floor Space

* **Minimum floor space:** 35m² per unit.
* **MUFB properties:** Units between 30 and 35m² can be considered so long as the average of the block is 35m² or more.

### Minimum Unexpired Lease

* **Minimum acceptable unexpired lease:** 60 years at maturity.

### Tenancy Agreement

* Must be let on an Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST) agreement.
* AST period: between 6 and 12 months required (longer terms by negotiation).
* Limited Company lets acceptable where the tenant is a named individual.
* Holiday Lets, Serviced Apartments and Airbnb acceptable.
* Holiday lets: affordability based on holiday let income, not AST.

---

## SECTION 4 – RENTAL INCOME & AFFORDABILITY

### Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR) – BTL Rental Coverage Test

* **Borrower type – 5 year fixed:** Basic rate taxpayer & limited companies: Pay Rate @ 125%; High rate taxpayer & HMOs: Pay Rate @ 145%.
* **Stress rate:** 5.5% – Standard stress rate applied to all ICR calculations.

### Rental Assessment

* Rental income must meet ICR requirements.
* Rental assessment required from appropriate sources.
* Subject to valuation and market rental assessment.
* Holiday lets: affordability based on holiday let income, not AST.

### Portfolio Landlords

* No maximum portfolio size restrictions.
* Portfolio landlords accepted.
* ICR stress testing applies to individual properties.
* Portfolio-wide assessment may be considered.

## SECTION 5 – PRODUCT TYPES

### TermTen Product

* **Term:** 10 years.
* **Fixed rate period:** 5 years.
* **Interest rate:** Starting from 6.39% (fixed for first 5 years).
* **Maximum LTV:** 77% gross (including net loan and arrangement fee).
* **Minimum loan:** £200,000.
* **Maximum loan:** £10,000,000 (gross).
* **Repayment:** Interest-only.
* **Arrangement fee:** 4% (can be added to loan).
* **Early Repayment Charge (ERC):** 5% during fixed period.
* **Exit fee:** 1%.
* **Property types:** Standard BTLs, MUFBs, small and large HMOs, Holiday Lets.
* **Aggregated value on blocks of flats:** Up to 20 units.
* **Applicable to:** Complex ownership structures, portfolio landlords, first-time landlords, limited companies, individuals, ex-pats, foreign nationals.

### Other BTL Products

* Various BTL products available.
* Loan sizes: £150,000 to £2,000,000.
* Maximum LTV: Up to 75% (net).
* Terms and rates vary by product; refer to current rate cards for specific product details.

## SECTION 7 – CREDIT HISTORY

### Bankruptcy Orders or Sequestration

* Unacceptable where registered in the last 36 months, whether discharged or not.
* Unacceptable where registered prior to 36 months ago and not discharged.

### County Court Judgements (or Decrees) and Defaults

* Maximum total value of £5,000 satisfied, over no more than 3 instances within 36 months prior to application.
* No instances have occurred within 12 months prior to application.

### Individual Voluntary Arrangements or Trust Deeds

* Unacceptable where registered in the last 36 months, whether satisfied or not.
* Unacceptable where registered prior to 36 months ago and not satisfied.

### Limited Company Insolvencies / Liquidations

* No insolvencies or liquidations in last 3 years.

### Missed Mortgage Payments

* No missed payment within last 12 months.
* Maximum of 2 missed payments in the last 36 months, excluding the first 12 months.

### Repossessions

* Not acceptable in the last 3 years.

### Unsecured Credit

* Maximum 2 in 12 months, maximum status 1.
* No more than 4 missed payments in 36 months, maximum status 1.
