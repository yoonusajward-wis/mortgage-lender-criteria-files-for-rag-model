1. LOAN & BORROWER CRITERIA
================================================================================

NUMBER OF BORROWERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Maximum 2 applicants

MINIMUM / MAXIMUM LOAN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Hodge Residential: Minimum £50,000
- Hodge Resi Retire (50+) / RIO: Minimum £20,000
- Maximum Loan: £2,000,000

MAXIMUM LOAN BY LTV BAND
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

| Loan Size | Max LTV |
|-----------|---------|
| Up to £600,000 | 95% LTV |
| Up to £850,000 | 90% LTV |
| Up to £1,000,000 | 85% LTV |
| Up to £2,000,000 | 75% LTV |

LOAN TERM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum 5 years
- Maximum 40 years

AGE AT APPLICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum age: 21
- Maximum age:
  - 75 – Hodge Resi
  - 88 – Resi Retire (50+) & RIO

REPAYMENT TYPES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Repayment (C&I)
- Interest Only

LTV LIMITS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 95% – Repayment Purchase (Resi & Resi Retire)
- 90% – Repayment Remortgage (Resi & Resi Retire)
- 75% – Interest Only (all products)

DEBT CONSOLIDATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Allowed
- Max LTV capped at 85% (Repayment only)

================================================================================
2. LOAN PURPOSES
================================================================================

PERMITTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Purchase of main residence or second home
- Remortgage with capital raising
- Debt consolidation
- Transfer of equity
- Business injection
- Payment of taxes

NOT PERMITTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Clearing gambling debts
- Purchase of timeshare
- Right-to-Buy
- Shared Equity
- Shared Ownership

================================================================================
3. INCOME MULTIPLES
================================================================================

HODGE RESI
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 90% LTV → 5× income
- 80%–90% → 5.5× income
- ≤80% → 6× income
- Interest Only: 5× income

HODGE RESI RETIRE (50+) & RIO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 90% LTV → 5× income
- ≤90% → 5.5× income
- Interest Only: 5× income

SPECIAL RULE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- £ for £ remortgages = 6× income regardless of LTV

4. INCOME & AFFORDABILITY CRITERIA
================================================================================

EMPLOYED INCOME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 100% of PAYE income used up to expected retirement date
- Can be used up to age 80
- If used past age 70, plausibility of continued employment assessed

OVERTIME / COMMISSION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Average of last 12 months
- If stable → up to 100%
- If not stable → 50% or 2-year average

BONUS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Annual: up to 75% of average of last 2 payments
- Monthly: treated as overtime/commission

SELF-EMPLOYED – SOLE TRADERS & PARTNERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum 12 months trading
- Latest year's figures used if sustainable (projections may be requested)

Evidence Requirements:

- If trading ≥2 years:
  - 2 years SA302s & tax overviews
- If trading <2 years:
  - Minimum 1 year's accounts
  - Accountant's projection + latest 3 months business bank statements

New Partners:
- Replacing existing partner → 2 years partnership accounts + actual drawings
- Newly created partner → expected % drawings
- If insufficient evidence → accountant's certificate acceptable

LIMITED COMPANY DIRECTORS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum 12 months trading
- Affordability based on salary + dividends, plus retained profit in certain cases

Retained Profit Allowed If:
- Borrower(s) own 100% of shares
- Business trading ≥2 years
- Retained profit stable/increasing
- Accountant confirms funds can be drawn safely

Evidence mirrors Sole Trader requirements.

CONTRACTORS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Day Rate Contractors:
- Min 12 months contracting
- Min 3 months remaining OR evidence of renewal
- Affordability: 48 weeks × day rate
- Max 3 months gap between contracts in last year
- Day-1 contractors: considered with relevant experience + £50k min income
- Multiple concurrent contracts may be accepted case-by-case

Fixed-Term Contractors:
- Employer must pay Tax/NI
- Min 12 months experience in similar role
- Min 3 months remaining OR evidence of renewal
- No minimum income

================================================================================
5. RETIREMENT INCOME
================================================================================

STATE PENSION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Amounts in payment OR expected entitlement

PENSIONS IN PAYMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 100% accepted (annuities, personal, company)

UFPLS / DRAWDOWN / COLLECTIVE INVESTMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Used based on projected fund value × % drawdown rate

Sliding scale:

| Age | Income % allowed |
|-----|------------------|
| 50–59 | 3.3–3.9% |
| 60–69 | 4.2–5.6% |
| 70–76 | 5.7–7.6% |
| 77+ | 7% |

Additional rules:
- Use 100% of projected pot less declared tax-free withdrawal
- Investments: use 75% of projected pot

SURVIVING PARTNER STRESS TEST
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- If term extends beyond 87th birthday of either applicant:
  - Affordability tested for each borrower individually
  - Life insurance can be used if it repays the loan

GUARANTORS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Not permitted

================================================================================
6. CREDIT CRITERIA
================================================================================

UNSECURED ARREARS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Max 2 months arrears in last 2 years
- Must be up to date at application
- Comms/utilities ignored

SECURED / MORTGAGE ARREARS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- No arrears in last 6 months
- Max 1 month arrears in last year
- Max 2 months in last 2 years

DEFAULTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Satisfied: up to £500 in last 3 years
- Unsatisfied: allowed if > 3 years old and total <£250
- Comms/utilities ignored

CCJs
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Satisfied: max £500 in last 3 years OR >3 years regardless of amount
- Unsatisfied: <£250 AND >3 years old
- Comms/utilities allowed at underwriter discretion

DEBT MANAGEMENT PLAN / IVA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Acceptable if discharged ≥3 years

REPOSSESSION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Acceptable >6 years ago

BANKRUPTCY / SEQUESTRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Acceptable if discharged ≥6 years

CREDIT SCORE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Used to assess plausibility

================================================================================
7. RESIDENCY CRITERIA
================================================================================

FOREIGN NATIONALS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- With ILR: treated as UK nationals
- Without ILR: acceptable if:
  - Global Talent Visa (≥18 months evidence)
  - Skilled Worker Visa (permanent employment)
  - Health & Care Visa
  - Spousal Visa (joint applications only)
  - Max LTV 90%
  - Minimum 2 years UK residency
  - Minimum 2 years UK credit history

EU SETTLEMENT SCHEME / IRISH NATIONALS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Min 2 years UK residency
- Must be UK taxpayer
- Must have 2 years credit history

RETURNING EXPATS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Accepted from day 1 if:
- Returning to permanent employment OR retired
- Residing in UK at application
- UK credit conduct identifiable
- Max time abroad: 2 years

If abroad >2 years OR self-employed OR no credit file:
- Min 6 months UK residency

================================================================================
8. INTEREST ONLY
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE REPAYMENT STRATEGIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Sale of mortgaged property (subject to regional equity minimums)
- Sale of other property (equity must fully cover loan)
- Realisation of investments
- Existing endowment (mid-growth projection)
- Pension lump sum: 25% of midpoint projected maturity value

MINIMUM EQUITY FOR SALE OF MORTGAGED PROPERTY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

| Region | Minimum Equity |
|--------|----------------|
| Scotland (ex. Edinburgh & Dundee), North East | £100k |
| North West, Yorkshire & Humber, Wales | £120k |
| Rest of UK | £150k |
| London | £250k |

MAX LTV
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 75%, including debt consolidation

MINIMUM INCOME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- No minimum income requirement for Interest Only

================================================================================
9. PROPERTY CRITERIA
================================================================================

MINIMUM / MAXIMUM VALUE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum £100,000
- Maximum £10 million
- 95% LTV: min property value £300,000

LOCATIONS ACCEPTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- England (including Isle of Wight)
- Wales
- Scotland (incl. road-bridge islands)

LOCATIONS NOT ACCEPTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Northern Ireland
- Isle of Man
- Channel Islands
- Any island without road bridge (except IoW)
- Holiday parks / complexes
- London Holiday Lets (due to >90-day letting ban)

UNACCEPTABLE PROPERTY TYPES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Retirement, sheltered or age-restricted
- Park homes
- Mobile homes
- Caravans
- Houseboats

================================================================================
10. NEW BUILD
================================================================================

ACCEPTABLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Houses & flats built/converted within last 12 months
- Developer must be NHOS registered
- Must have warranty or PCC

DEVELOPMENTS >3 UNITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Must be registered with the New Homes Quality Board (NHQB)

RESTRICTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- No 95% LTV on new builds
- Allowed incentives up to 5%, e.g.:
  - Cashback
  - SDLT contribution
  - Deposit gift
  - Legal fees
- Incentives do NOT reduce valuation
- Incentives must be matched by borrower

================================================================================
11. EX-PUBLIC-SECTOR PROPERTIES
================================================================================

MINIMUM VALUES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Houses: £150,000
- Flats/maisonettes: £250,000

FLATS / MAISONETTES ONLY ACCEPTED IF:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- ≤4 storeys
- No communal balconies
- No open walkways

NOT ALLOWED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Ex-public-sector at 95% LTV
- Properties still within Right to Buy pre-emption period

================================================================================
12. PROPERTY FEATURES
================================================================================

BEDROOMS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Up to 6 (more by referral)

KITCHENS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Up to 2 acceptable

ANNEXES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- 1 self-contained annexe acceptable if short-term let only

NON-STANDARD CONSTRUCTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Single-skin accepted only in single-storey, non-habitable rooms
- Modern timber frame accepted (post-1950 Scotland / post-1970 England & Wales)
- Timber frames with retro-filled cavities not accepted
- MMC considered case-by-case

ABOVE / ADJACENT TO COMMERCIAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Restaurants, takeaways, cafes, pubs may be considered
- Minimum values:
  - £150,000 outside London
  - £250,000 in London

================================================================================
13. FLATS & MAISONETTES
================================================================================

STOREYS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Up to 6 as standard
- 6 storeys allowed if developer signed Developer Remediation Contract (England/Wales)

LIFT REQUIREMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- If >3rd floor → building must have a lift
- Except Scottish tenements

NON-STANDARD FLATS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Max 4 storeys

SERVICE CHARGE / GROUND RENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Service charge ≤ 1% of property value
- Ground rent ≤ 0.1%
- Ground rent must not double more often than 20 years
- Must not be linked to property value

NEW BUILD FLATS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Acceptable when fully complete with warranty
- Not allowed at 95% LTV

LEASEHOLD REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum 85 years remaining at completion
- Freehold flats / maisonettes NOT accepted

SCOTTISH TENEMENT FLATS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Acceptable if:
- Min value £100,000 (or £120,000 above commercial)
- Pre-1915 masonry build
- ≤5 storeys
- No communal balconies or access

================================================================================
14. RIO (RETIREMENT INTEREST ONLY)
================================================================================

AGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Minimum 50
- Maximum 88

REPAYMENT TYPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Interest Only
- No repayment vehicle required
- Loan repaid on death or move into care

TERM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Lifetime mortgage – no end date

LTV
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Max 75%

MINIMUM EQUITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- No specific minimum, but property must be £100k+

AFFORDABILITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Must demonstrate ability to maintain payments for life
- Joint cases: affordability assessed on each applicant individually if one dies
- Life insurance proceeds may be considered
