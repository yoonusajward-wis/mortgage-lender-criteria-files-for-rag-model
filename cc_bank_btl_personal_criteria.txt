## Overview

Cambridge & Counties Bank offers Buy to Let (BTL) mortgages for personal applicants (individuals). The Bank provides flexible BTL products suitable for experienced property investors, with products designed for both individual name(s) and limited company lending.

### Key Features

* **Experienced frontline team** - Assist in structuring the right deal for your client
* **Support through application process** - And beyond
* **Manual underwriting process** - Combined with wide market knowledge
* **Documentation prepared by us** - Commission paid at the same time as the loan is drawn down
* **Outstanding customer satisfaction scores**

---

## General Lending Criteria

### Where We Lend

* **Location:** England, Scotland, and Wales

### Loan-to-Value (LTV)

* **Maximum LTV:** Varies by product type (see product-specific sections below)

### Loan Amounts

* **Minimum loan:** £250,000 in England and Wales
* **Minimum loan:** £500,000 in Scotland
* **Maximum borrowings:** Up to £15m per customer with no restriction on property numbers (varies by product)

### Loan Terms

* **Term:** From 12 months to 30 years (varies by product and rate type)
* **Rate types:** Variable and fixed rates available
* **Fixed rate terms:** 3 or 5 years (depending on the product)
* **Variable rate terms:** From 12 months to 30 years

### Repayment Methods

* **Interest-only:** Available (varies by product)
* **Capital and Interest:** Available

## Applicant Requirements

### Applicant Types

* **Individuals** - Personal applicants (UK residents)
* **Limited companies** - UK registered limited companies
* **LLPs** - Limited Liability Partnerships
* **Experienced property investors** - Only available to experienced property investors

### Residency Requirements

* **For borrowing in personal name(s):** Individual must be a UK resident
* **Corporate borrowers:** Must be registered in the UK or Crown dependencies

### Experience Requirements

* **Only available to experienced property investors** - All products require experienced property investors
* **HMO products:** Only available to experienced HMO investors in their local area

### Personal Guarantees

* **Personal guarantees requested** - From business owners/directors but are not always mandatory

## Property Criteria

### Property Location

* **Location:** England, Scotland, and Wales
* **Security:** Secured by way of a first legal charge on a property located in England, Scotland or Wales

### Property Types

**Acceptable Properties:**
* **Standard buy to let properties** - Single unit residential properties
* **Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs)** - Single or multiple tenancy agreements
* **Holiday lets** - Properties with C1 or C3 planning
* **Large portfolios** - No limit to the number of properties you can own
* Subject to product-specific criteria

### 2. Expat Buy-to-Let

Living abroad shouldn't be a barrier to purchasing and managing property in the UK. Our expat buy-to-let finance gives you the ability to continue expanding your property portfolio by purchasing new property, refinancing or releasing equity and all whilst based abroad.

**Key Features:**
* **All identification and paperwork** can be signed and witnessed by a legal firm on the Global 500 list so you don't need to travel to the UK
* **Borrowing must be through limited company or LLP entities** - Not available for individual name(s)
* **No limit to the number of properties** you can own
* **No valuation fee payable** until loan is approved
* **Equity release** for purchase and refinance
* **Gifted deposits permitted** (between family members)
* **Interest-only options available**
* **Lending available in England, Scotland & Wales**
* **Funding available for large portfolios and borrowings**

**Interest Rate Options:**
* **Variable rate:** Terms from 12 months to 30 years
* **Fixed rate:** Terms of 3 or 5 years (depending on the product)

**Lending Criteria:**
* **Maximum LTV:** Up to 60% LTV of open market value
* **Only available to experienced property investors**
* **Minimum loan:** £250k in England and Wales
* **Minimum loan:** £500k in Scotland
* **Maximum borrowings:** Up to £15m per customer with no restriction on property numbers
* **Terms:** From 12 months to 30 years
* **Personal guarantees:** Requested from business owners/directors but are not always mandatory
* **Corporate borrowers:** Must be registered in the UK or Crown dependencies
* **Security:** Secured by way of a first legal charge on a property located in England, Scotland or Wales

### 3. Holiday Lets

With staycations on the rise, the future potential of the UK's expanding holiday market is becoming popular. Our holiday let mortgages are designed for properties that will be let out on a short-term basis. Built on an understanding of the market, we offer finance aimed at investors looking to buy and develop UK holiday lettings properties.

**Key Features:**
* **Gifted deposits permitted** (between family members)
* **Equity release** for purchase and refinance
* **No valuation fee payable** until loan is approved
* **Interest-only options available**
* **Available in England, Scotland & Wales**

**Commercial Loan Interest Rate Options (C1 planning):**
* **Variable rate:** Terms from 12 months to 25 years
* **Fixed rate:** 3 & 5 year fixed rates available (depending on the product)

**Residential Loan Interest Rate Options (C3 planning):**
* **Variable rate:** Terms from 12 months to 30 years
* **Fixed rate:** 3 & 5 year fixed rates available (depending on the product)

**Lending Criteria:**
* **Maximum LTV:** Up to 75% LTV of an independent professional valuation
* **Only available to experienced property investors**
* **No minimum earned income required** - However, resilience to loan repayments must be demonstrated
* **Minimum loan:** £250k in England and Wales
* **Minimum loan:** £500k in Scotland
* **Maximum borrowings:** Up to £15m per customer with no restriction on property numbers
* **Personal guarantees:** Requested from business owners/directors but are not always mandatory
* **Corporate borrowers:** Must be registered in the UK or Crown dependencies
* **For borrowing in personal name(s):** Individual must be a UK resident
* **Security:** Secured by way of a first legal charge on a property located in England, Scotland or Wales

**Commercial (C1 planning):**
* **Terms:** 12 months to 25 years dependent upon the repayment type
* **Available for properties such as:** Holiday villages, holiday parks, complexes and owner occupier with C1 planning permissions

**Residential (C3 planning):**
* **Terms:** 12 months to 30 years dependent upon the repayment type
* **Available for:** Single self-contained properties only, with no restrictive covenants regarding occupancy in place
* **The property must be capable of being let on an AST basis** and have an acceptable level of rental demand

---

### 4. House in Multiple Occupation (HMO)

**Key Features:**
* **Gifted deposits permitted** (between family members)
* **No limit on the number of investment properties** per client
* **Equity release** for purchase and refinance
* **Investment based valuations apply** to properties with appropriate planning
* **No valuation fee payable** until loan is approved
* **Limited company lending or individual name(s)** - Available
* **Interest only options available**
* **HMO with single or multiple tenancy agreements** - Available
* **Lending available in England, Scotland & Wales**

**Interest Rate Options:**
* **Variable rate:** Terms from 12 months to 30 years
* **Fixed rate:** 3 & 5 year fixed rates available

**Lending Criteria:**
* **Maximum LTV:** Up to 70% LTV of open market value or 80% of vacant possession value
* **Only available to experienced HMO investors in their local area**
* **Minimum loan:** £250k in England and Wales
* **Minimum loan:** £500k in Scotland
* **Maximum borrowings:** Up to £15m per customer
* **Terms:** From 12 months to 30 years
* **Personal guarantees:** Requested from business owners/directors but are not always mandatory
* **Corporate borrowers:** Must be registered in the UK or Crown dependencies
* **For borrowing in personal name(s):** Individual must be a UK resident
* **Security:** Secured by way of a first legal charge on a property in England, Scotland or Wales
* **Planning consent:** All properties must hold planning consent for C4 HMO or Sui Generis to qualify for an investment based valuation, otherwise a single dwelling house valuation will apply

* Additional forms as required
