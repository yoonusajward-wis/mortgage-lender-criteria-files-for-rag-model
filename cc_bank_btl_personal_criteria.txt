## SECTION 1 – GENERAL LENDING CRITERIA

* Lending in England, Scotland and Wales.
* Security: first legal charge on a property in England, Scotland or Wales.
* Only available to experienced property investors.
* Individual borrowers must be UK residents.
* No limit to the number of properties a borrower can own.
* Maximum borrowings: up to £15,000,000 per customer (varies by product).
* Minimum loan: £250,000 in England and Wales; £500,000 in Scotland.
* Loan terms: from 12 months to 30 years (varies by product and rate type).
* Rate types: variable and fixed (3 or 5 year fixed terms available).
* Repayment methods: interest only or capital and interest.
* No valuation fee payable until the loan is approved.
* Gifted deposits permitted between family members.
* Commission paid at the same time as the loan is drawn down.
* Manual underwriting process.

## SECTION 2 – BUY TO LET

* Available to experienced property investors only.
* Available to individual names and limited companies.
* Individual borrowers must be UK residents.
* Maximum LTV: 75% of open market value.
* Minimum loan: £250,000 in England and Wales; £500,000 in Scotland.
* Maximum borrowings: up to £15,000,000 per customer with no restriction on property numbers.
* Variable rate terms: 12 months to 30 years.
* Fixed rate terms: 3 or 5 years.
* Interest only available.
* Equity release for purchase and refinance.
* Gifted deposits permitted between family members.
* Security: first legal charge on a property in England, Scotland or Wales.

## SECTION 3 – EXPAT BUY TO LET

* Available to experienced property investors only.
* Borrowing must be through a limited company or LLP — not available in individual names.
* Maximum LTV: 60% of open market value.
* Minimum loan: £250,000 in England and Wales; £500,000 in Scotland.
* Maximum borrowings: up to £15,000,000 per customer with no restriction on property numbers.
* Variable rate terms: 12 months to 30 years.
* Fixed rate terms: 3 or 5 years.
* Interest only available.
* Equity release for purchase and refinance.
* Gifted deposits permitted between family members.
* All identification and paperwork can be signed and witnessed by a legal firm on the Global 500 list — travel to the UK is not required.
* Security: first legal charge on a property in England, Scotland or Wales.

---

## SECTION 4 – HOLIDAY LETS

* Available to experienced property investors only.
* Available to individual names and limited companies.
* Individual borrowers must be UK residents.
* No minimum earned income required; however, resilience to loan repayments must be demonstrated.
* Maximum LTV: 75% of an independent professional valuation.
* Minimum loan: £250,000 in England and Wales; £500,000 in Scotland.
* Maximum borrowings: up to £15,000,000 per customer with no restriction on property numbers.
* Interest only available.
* Equity release for purchase and refinance.
* Gifted deposits permitted between family members.
* Security: first legal charge on a property in England, Scotland or Wales.

### Commercial Holiday Let (C1 Planning)

* Available for: holiday villages, holiday parks, complexes and owner-occupied properties with C1 planning permission.
* Variable rate terms: 12 months to 25 years.
* Fixed rate terms: 3 or 5 years.

### Residential Holiday Let (C3 Planning)

* Available for single self-contained properties only with no restrictive covenants regarding occupancy.
* Property must be capable of being let on an AST basis and have an acceptable level of rental demand.
* Variable rate terms: 12 months to 30 years.
* Fixed rate terms: 3 or 5 years.

---

## SECTION 5 – HOUSES IN MULTIPLE OCCUPATION (HMO)

* Available to experienced HMO investors in their local area only.
* Available to individual names and limited companies.
* Individual borrowers must be UK residents.
* Maximum LTV: 70% of open market value or 80% of vacant possession value.
* Planning consent: all properties must hold C4 HMO or Sui Generis planning consent to qualify for an investment-based valuation; otherwise a single dwelling house valuation applies.
* HMO with single or multiple tenancy agreements accepted.
* Investment-based valuations apply to properties with appropriate planning.
* Minimum loan: £250,000 in England and Wales; £500,000 in Scotland.
* Maximum borrowings: up to £15,000,000 per customer.
* Variable rate terms: 12 months to 30 years.
* Fixed rate terms: 3 or 5 years.
* Interest only available.
* Equity release for purchase and refinance.
* Gifted deposits permitted between family members.
* Security: first legal charge on a property in England, Scotland or Wales.
