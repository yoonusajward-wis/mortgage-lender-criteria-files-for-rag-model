# VIDA HOME LOANS – RESIDENTIAL LENDING CRITERIA

# ACCEPTABLE VISA TYPES

Foreign nationals are acceptable subject to evidence of right to reside in the UK. All applicants must provide 3 years’ address history, with the most recent year showing continuous UK residency.

Applicants with permanent right to reside (including EU/EEA/Swiss nationals with Settled or Pre-Settled status or Indefinite Leave to Remain) can access the Standard Residential range up to 97% LTV.

Applicants holding one of the following visas must use the Foreign National product range:

Skilled Worker Visa, British National (Overseas) Visa, Health and Care Worker Visa, Family Visas (Spouse/Partner, Parent, UK Ancestry, Skilled Worker Dependent), Innovator Founder Visa, Investor Visa, Global Talent Visa, Minister of Religion Visa and Sportsperson Visa.

There is no minimum time remaining required on a visa.

The Foreign National range is available up to 90% LTV where either:

• One applicant earns at least £50,000; or
• Joint applicants earn at least £70,000 combined.

If these income thresholds are not met, the maximum LTV is restricted to 75%.

A minimum of 5% of the deposit must come from the applicant’s own funds.

---

# ACCOUNTANT QUALIFICATIONS

Recognised qualifications include ACA, FCA, ACCA, FCCA, AAPA, FAPA, ACMA, FCMA, MAAT, FMAAT, CTA and AIA.

If the accountant does not hold a recognised qualification, SA302s and Tax Year Overviews are required for Sole Traders and Partnerships. For Limited Companies, the accountant’s certificate must be signed by a recognised qualified accountant.

---

# ADOPTION ALLOWANCE

Adoption allowance is not accepted.

---

# AFFORDABILITY

The following are considered within affordability:

Childcare, school or university fees, ground rent, service charges, maintenance, CCJs, defaults, DMP payments, council tax, student loans, salary sacrifice schemes, payslip deductions and travel costs.

---

# AGE

Minimum age at application is 21. Maximum age to apply is 64 (minimum term 5 years). The mortgage term must not exceed the oldest applicant’s 70th birthday unless lending into retirement criteria are met.

Lending up to age 79 (term finishing by 80th birthday) is possible where the applicant:

Is under 50 at application;
Is contributing regularly to a pension;
Is more than 10 years from anticipated retirement;
Is borrowing on Capital & Interest (Interest Only not allowed beyond age 70).

If an applicant does not meet these criteria, they may remain on the application but their income cannot be used for affordability. The term must still end by age 80.

---

# FOREIGN CURRENCY INCOME

Applicants paid in foreign currency are not acceptable.

---

# ARCHITECT’S CERTIFICATE

An architect’s certificate is acceptable where the consultant holds a recognised qualification and suitable professional indemnity insurance.

---

# ARREARS

Satisfied and unsatisfied unsecured missed payments of £250 or more are acceptable depending on product range:

Vida 36 – acceptable if older than 36 months
Vida 24 – acceptable if older than 24 months
Vida 6 – acceptable if older than 6 months
Packager – considered with no maximum

---

# BANKRUPTCY / IVA / DRO / TRUST DEED

Acceptable subject to product range:

Vida 36 – 3 years from discharge
Vida 24 – 3 years from discharge
Vida 6 – 3 years from discharge
Packager – 1 year from discharge

---

# CCJs

Satisfied and unsatisfied CCJs of £250 or more are acceptable depending on product range:

Vida 36 – older than 36 months
Vida 24 – older than 24 months
Vida 6 – older than 6 months
Packager – considered if less than 6 months

---

# DEFAULTS

Satisfied and unsatisfied defaults of £250 or more follow the same timeframes as CCJs across the Vida 36, 24, 6 and Packager ranges.

---

# DEBT CONSOLIDATION

Debt consolidation is available up to 85% LTV.

Debt Management Plans and Debt Arrangement Schemes may be considered at underwriter discretion subject to satisfactory affordability and conduct.

---

# DEPOSIT

Proof of deposit is required unless funded by equity from sale of current property.

Acceptable sources include own funds, equity, remortgage of owned property, inheritance, directors loan (with accountant confirmation), gifted deposit from close relatives (up to 100%), overseas deposits (case-by-case), and builder/vendor incentives up to 5%.

Cryptocurrency deposits are not acceptable.

Deposits from a Limited Company are acceptable with written confirmation from the company accountant that the loan will not adversely affect the business and all shareholders agree.

---

# CONCESSIONARY / DISCOUNT PURCHASE

Permitted where the vendor is either a family member or a landlord (minimum 5% deposit and 12-month tenancy).

Maximum advance is 100% of the discounted purchase price or 75% of open market value (whichever is lower). For long-term tenants purchasing from a landlord, up to 95% of the discounted purchase price or 75% of market value (whichever is lower).

The family vendor must not remain in the property and must relinquish rights. Proof of satisfactory mortgage conduct is required if the property is mortgaged. Gifted equity is not acceptable.

---

# CONTRACTORS

Vida considers sole traders, partnerships, umbrella contractors, limited companies, fixed-term contractors and Day 1 contractors.

Day 1 contractors must have at least 1 year’s track record in the same line of work and 3 months remaining on their contract. Self-employed contractors’ income is calculated using weekly rate × 48 weeks.

Umbrella and PAYE contractors require latest 3 months’ payslips and P60 (if applicable).

CIS contractors are assessed using latest SA302 and Tax Year Overviews plus either 3 months’ payslips/invoices or 12 months’ CIS payslips/invoices.

---

# EMPLOYMENT & INCOME

No minimum time in current job is required. Day 1 employment is acceptable with at least 3 months’ employment history.

Employed income requires latest 3 months’ payslips. For bonuses, overtime or commission, 3 months’ payslips plus P60 are required.

Income accepted:

100% – regular bonuses, pensions, trust income, second job (12 months history), allowances, foster care income, child benefit, child tax credit, armed forces compensation scheme payments.

75% – commission, overtime, profit-related pay.

50% – DLA, Carer’s Allowance, IIDB, PIP, ESA, Incapacity Benefit, Armed Forces Independence Payment, Universal Credit, Working Family Tax Credits.

Commission and profit-related pay accepted at 75% where evidenced.

---

# BENEFIT INCOME

Benefit income can be considered provided at least one applicant earns £15,000 per annum from employment or self-employment.

Child Benefit and Child Tax Credit are accepted at 100%, subject to conditions:

All applicants must earn under £60,000 each.
Only children aged 11 or younger at application can be included.

Other benefits such as ESA, DLA, PIP, IIDB, Incapacity Benefit and Universal Credit are accepted at 50%.

Carer’s Allowance is accepted at 50%.

---

# CREDIT CARD BALANCES

3% of the outstanding balance is annualised and deducted unless evidence shows the card has been fully repaid each month for the last 3 months.

---

# PROPERTY – FLATS

Flats are acceptable up to scheme LTV limits.

Flats above or adjacent to commercial premises are acceptable up to 75% LTV. Where located above or next to restaurants, takeaways, dry cleaners, hairdressers, laundrettes, tattoo/piercing/nail parlours, public houses or petrol stations, the maximum LTV is 60%.

High-rise flats are acceptable subject to valuer comment. There is no maximum storey limit.

Studio flats must have a minimum internal floor area of 30m² and 35m² external.

Ex-Local Authority flats are acceptable up to 80% LTV where predominantly still council owned. Minimum valuation is £80,000 (£200,000 in Greater London).

Freehold flats are acceptable only in Scotland or as Coach House flats in England & Wales.

Flying freehold is acceptable up to 25% of total floor space subject to valuer confirmation.

Flat roofs are acceptable up to 100% coverage subject to valuation.

---

# FREEHOLD HOUSES WITH SELF-CONTAINED FLATS

Properties with a single annex may be considered where services are shared. The annex must be occupied by family members on a long-term basis or used on a short-term/holiday let basis.

---

# FURTHER ADVANCE

Further advances are not currently offered.

---

# GUARANTEED INCOME PAYMENT / FORCES

Accepted at 100%.

---

# GUARANTORS

Guarantors are not accepted.

Vida offers a Joint Borrower / Sole Proprietor product called **Helping Hand**, allowing family members (including parents, stepparents, children, step/adopted children, siblings, grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, nieces and nephews) to support an applicant by joining the mortgage without being named on the title deeds. Income from the supporting applicant can be used for affordability.

---

# GUARDIAN’S ALLOWANCE

Accepted at 100%.

---

# HOUSING ALLOWANCE / MORTGAGE SUBSIDY

Accepted at 100% where provided by an employer.

---

# HOUSING BENEFIT

Not accepted as income.

---

# TRUST INCOME

Accepted at 100% where supported by SA302 and corresponding Tax Year Overview.

---

# INCOME MULTIPLE

Maximum income multiple is 6×, subject to affordability.

---

# INHERITANCE AS INTEREST ONLY STRATEGY

Not acceptable. Repayment strategies must be in place on or before completion.

---

# INHERITED PROPERTIES

Acceptable once probate has been completed. Applications are treated as a remortgage.

---

# INTEREST ONLY

Available up to 75% LTV with a maximum loan of £1m.

Interest Only is not permitted where the applicant is already retired or where the term extends into retirement.

---

# INTEREST ONLY – REPAYMENT STRATEGY

A credible strategy must be in place to fully repay the mortgage at term end.

Acceptable strategies include savings (Cash ISA or other), stocks and shares, endowment policies, pension plans, sale of another mortgaged property and downsizing.

For downsizing, minimum equity at the start of the mortgage must be:

£250,000 for properties in London and South East England.
£175,000 for properties in the rest of the UK.

Other strategies, including sale of other assets or occasional income payments, may be considered.

An Interest Only Repayment Strategy Declaration Form is required.

---

# INVESTMENT INCOME

Accepted at 100% where supported by SA302 and Tax Year Overview.

---

# JAPANESE KNOTWEED

Not acceptable if Category A or B within the property boundary or neighbouring properties.

Category C or D may be considered, or where previously Category A/B but now subject to an assignable, up-to-date treatment plan. All cases subject to satisfactory valuation.

---

# JOB SEEKER’S ALLOWANCE

Not accepted.

---

# JOINT BORROWER / SOLE PROPRIETOR

The Helping Hand product allows family members to join the mortgage and support affordability without being named on the title deeds.

---

# LEASEHOLD – OWNED FREEHOLD INTEREST

Where the applicant owns part or all of the freehold, Vida will register its charge against both the leasehold and freehold titles (where solely owned).

---

# LEASEHOLD REMAINING TERM

Capital & Interest requires a minimum of 40 years remaining at the end of term.

Interest Only requires 70 years remaining at the end of term.

---

# LENDING AREA

Lending available in England, Wales and selected Scottish postcodes.

Accepted Scottish postcode areas include AB (with exclusions), DD, DG, EH (with exclusions), FK (with exclusions), G (with exclusions), IV (with exclusions), KA (with exclusions), KW (with exclusions), KY (with exclusions), ML, PA (with exclusions), PH (with exclusions) and TD.

(Full postcode exclusions apply as per Vida criteria.)

---

# LENDING INTO RETIREMENT

Maximum retirement age is 70.

Lending into retirement may be considered where the applicant is under 50 and more than 10 years from retirement.

Requirements include:

Later Life Lending Declaration.
Evidence of pension provision.
Plausibility of working to loan maturity.

Restricted to Capital & Interest only.

---

# RETIRED AT APPLICATION

A retired applicant may only apply jointly with another employed or self-employed applicant.

The retired applicant’s income cannot be used for affordability.

Term must end by the oldest applicant’s 80th birthday.

---

# LENDING TO TRUSTS

Not offered.

---

# LIGHT REFURBISHMENT

Acceptable subject to valuer comments.

---

# LIVE / WORK PROPERTIES

Considered where:

The property is used exclusively by the owner for their own business.
Business use does not exceed 40% of floor area.
The property can be sold as a standard residence without major modification.

---

# LOAN ASSESSMENT

General upper limit of 6× income, subject to affordability.

Outgoings assessed include childcare, fees, ground rent, maintenance, credit commitments, salary sacrifice, travel costs and Interest Only repayment strategy commitments.

Remaining credit commitments are included in the assessment. For remortgages with debt consolidation, remaining balances after consolidation are included.

Credit card commitments are assessed at 3% of outstanding balance.

---

# LOAN PURPOSE

Most legal purposes accepted, including capital raising.

Not accepted:

Injection into a business
Speculative investments
Gambling debts
Time share purchase
Consumer Buy to Let
Second charges
Further advances

---

# LOAN SIZE

Minimum loan: £50,000
Maximum loan: £2m
Maximum Interest Only loan: £1m

---

# LODGER INCOME

For remortgages, 100% of lodger income accepted, evidenced by last 3 months’ bank statements.

---

# LONDON WEIGHTING

Accepted at 100%.

---

# LTV

Maximum LTV is 97%.

Limits by loan size:

Up to £750,000 – 97% LTV
Up to £1.5m – 80%, 85% or 90% LTV
Up to £2m – 75% LTV

Interest Only limited to 75% LTV.

Product fees may be added above maximum LTV without affecting LTV calculations (not above 97%). Affordability is calculated on the gross loan including fees.

For LTVs above 90%, credit file must show no current arrears on major unsecured items.

For Vida 24 and Vida 6 products, LTV above 85% requires no current arrears on major unsecured items.

---

# MARITAL STATUS

Applications should be submitted jointly where applicants are married, in civil partnership, cohabiting or financially dependent.

Single applications in these cases require full justification and are subject to underwriter discretion.

---

# MATERNITY / PATERNITY LEAVE

Considered using return-to-work income, supported by employer confirmation of return date and salary.

---

# MINIMUM FLOOR AREA

Studio flats require:

Minimum internal area of 30m².
Minimum external area of 35m².

---

# MINIMUM INCOME

Main applicant must earn at least £15,000 per annum.

---

# NATIONALITY, RESIDENCY & VISAS

Foreign nationals acceptable subject to right to reside and 3 years’ address history with most recent year in the UK.

Permanent right to reside applicants can access Standard Residential range up to 97% LTV.

Visa holders listed under Foreign National criteria can access up to 90% LTV (income thresholds apply). If income thresholds are not met, maximum LTV is 75%.

Minimum 5% deposit must be from applicant’s own funds.

---

# NEW BUILD

New build houses and flats accepted up to 90% LTV.

Property must:

Never have been occupied.
Have acceptable warranty (e.g. NHBC, LABC, Premier Guarantee, ICW, Protek and other recognised providers).
Have a UK Finance Disclosure of Incentives Form completed.

Maximum builder incentive is 5% of purchase price.

Offers valid for 6 months and extendable for a further 6 months subject to reassessment and £120 revaluation.

Bulk purchase discounts and certain off-plan arrangements are not acceptable.

---

# NEW BUILD – BUILDER SALES INCENTIVES

Accepted provided total incentives do not exceed 5% of the purchase price. Confirmation must be provided by the vendor/builder via the CML/UK Finance Disclosure Form.

The 5% maximum can be split, for example 2.5% gifted deposit and 2.5% cash or other incentives.

---

# NON-BORROWING OCCUPIERS

Acceptable. All occupiers over age 17 must sign a witnessed Declaration following independent legal advice.

---

# NON-SIMULTANEOUS / STAGGERED COMPLETION

Acceptable provided income can support both mortgage commitments.

---

# NON-UK TAX PAYER / NON-DOMICILE

Not acceptable. Applicants must reside in the UK and be paid in sterling.

---

# NUMBER OF APPLICANTS

Maximum of 4 applicants.

---

# OTHER INCOME

Income accepted as follows:

100% accepted — regular bonuses (supported by payslips and P60), trust income, occupational pensions, second job (minimum 3-month record), additional job (12-month record), car allowance, shift allowance, London weighting, housing allowance, mortgage subsidy, armed forces compensation scheme payments, lodger income (remortgage only), foster care income, child benefit, child tax credit, guardian’s allowance.

75% accepted — commission, overtime commission, profit-related pay.

50% accepted — DLA, Carer’s Allowance, IIDB, PIP, ESA, Incapacity Benefit, Armed Forces Independence Payment, Universal Credit, Working Family Tax Credits.

---

# PART REPAYMENT / PART INTEREST ONLY

Not offered.

---

# PAYDAY LOANS

All payday loans must be repaid prior to completion. Underwriter must be satisfied there is no ongoing reliance.

---

# PENSION CONTRIBUTIONS

Generally ignored for affordability unless considered excessive.

Where lending into retirement and pension income is relied upon, evidence of pension contributions is required.

---

# PERIOD PROPERTIES / NON-STANDARD CONSTRUCTION

Timber framed properties constructed pre-1950 acceptable only if considered a “period property” and subject to valuer comments.

---

# PORTING

Not offered.

---

# PRIVATE SALES

Private sales (no estate agent) acceptable.

---

# PROBATIONARY PERIOD

May be considered subject to underwriter discretion.

---

# PRODUCT FEATURES

All products include a non-refundable £195 application fee (Fee Saver products have £0 application fee).

Overpayments up to 10% per annum permitted without ERC.

ERCs apply depending on product.

Broker fees cannot be added to the loan.

Porting not available.

---

# PRODUCT TRANSFER

Available via Vida’s Product Switch Hub.

---

# PROFESSIONAL LANDLORDS

Accepted and treated as self-employed.

---

# PROFESSIONAL SPORTS PEOPLE

Considered on individual merit.

---

# PROOF OF ADDRESS

Minimum 2-year residency history required.

If not on electoral roll, acceptable documents include driving licence, recent utility bill, recent bank/credit card statement, council tax bill, HMRC correspondence or mortgage/rent statement.

---

# PROOF OF ID

Electronic ID checks are undertaken. If unsuccessful, documentary ID and address verification required as per acceptable document list.

---

# PROPERTIES ABOVE / ADJACENT TO COMMERCIAL

Accepted up to 75% LTV.

Properties above or next to restaurants, takeaways, dry cleaners, hairdressers, laundrettes, tattoo/piercing/nail parlours, public houses or petrol stations limited to 60% LTV, subject to valuer comments.

---

# PROPERTY INVESTMENT CLUBS

Not accepted.

---

# PROPERTY – MINING / MINESHAFT

May be considered subject to valuation and legal checks.

---

# PROPERTY – SUBSIDENCE

Not acceptable.

---

# PROPERTY CONDITION

Property must be habitable at application. Subject to valuer comments.

---

# PROPERTY INFORMATION

Remortgages within 6 months may be considered subject to discretion.

Accepted property types include ex-local authority houses (subject to conditions), flying freeholds (max 25%), barn conversions, Grade I and II listed buildings, properties with one annex for family use, residential land up to 3 acres (more case-by-case), solar panel roof leases and unencumbered properties (treated as remortgage).

Agricultural restrictions not acceptable.

Maximum one kitchen (except granny annex with no tenancy).

Minimum 1 bedroom. No maximum bedrooms or property value.

Various non-standard constructions acceptable subject to BOPAS/BBA certification and design life requirements.

Estate rent charges acceptable where specific legal protections apply and annual charge does not exceed £500.

---

# PROPERTY OWNED LESS THAN 6 MONTHS

May be considered subject to underwriter discretion.

---

# PROPERTY WITH LAND / ACREAGE

Up to 3 acres acceptable. Must be wholly residential. Private paddocks and stables acceptable. Farms and smallholdings not acceptable.

---

# PROPERTY WITH OUTBUILDINGS

Permitted under same criteria as land — must remain residential in nature.

---

# PROPERTY WITH SHARED ACCESS / SERVICES

Shared access may be acceptable subject to valuer and solicitor confirmation.

Shared services not acceptable except for septic tanks.

---

# SPRAY FOAM INSULATION

Not acceptable.

---

# PURCHASE

Vendor ownership under 6 months may be considered subject to discretion.

---

# PURCHASE OF ADJOINING PROPERTY

Acceptable where both are single dwelling units with no shared services other than cesspit.

---

# REMORTGAGE

Accepted where applicant has owned property at least 6 months.

Remortgages within 6 months may be considered subject to discretion.

Bridging finance remortgage permitted within 6 months.

Capital raising allowed where value improvements evidenced.

Private bridge repayment not permitted.

Transfer of Equity allowed.

Second charges must be repaid unless securing business overdraft (Deed of Postponement required).

Not permitted for speculative investments, gambling, time share or failing business support.

---

# RENTAL INCOME

As additional income: accepted at 100%.

As sole income: acceptable under self-employed criteria.

---

# REPAYMENT METHODS

Capital & Interest available to maximum product LTV.

Interest Only available to 75% LTV with acceptable repayment vehicle, including savings, investments, endowment, pension (75% of projected value), sale of property or downsizing (minimum equity thresholds apply).

UK bank Direct Debit required.

---

# REPOSSESSIONS

Acceptable subject to tier:

Vida 36, 24, 6 – 6 years since repossession.
Packager – 3 years since repossession.

---

# ADVERSE TIERS

Three Residential tiers plus a Packager tier available.

---

# RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS

Not acceptable.

---

# RETIREMENT INTEREST ONLY

Not offered.

---

# RIGHT TO BUY / RIGHT TO ACQUIRE

Available on purchase only.

Maximum loan is lower of 100% discounted purchase price or 75% open market value.

Section 125/RTA3 required and last 12 months rental history.

Permanent right to reside required.

---

# SECOND HOME

Not accepted.

---

# SECOND JOB

Up to 100% accepted with minimum 3-month track record.

---

# SECURED CREDIT ARREARS

No secured arrears allowed within last 6 months.

Historic secured arrears must be up to date for at least 6 months.

Timeframes apply depending on product tier (Vida 36, 24, 6, Packager).

---

# SELF BUILD

Only considered once completed with suitable 10-year warranty.

---

# SELF-EMPLOYED

Sole trader/LLP/partnership – 2 years accounts required (1 year plus projection if trading under 2 years).

Limited company treated as self-employed if 25%+ shareholding.

If 100% shareholder, net profit may be used. Otherwise salary + dividends.

Retained profit can be used as deposit, not income.

---

# SHARED OWNERSHIP

Not offered.

---

# SHELTERED / RETIREMENT HOUSING

Not offered.

---

# SOLAR PANELS

Roof space lease consent acceptable.

---

# SOLICITORS

Must be on Vida panel.

---

# STEEL FRAME

Acceptable if post-2000 construction.

---

# TERM

5 to 45 years.

Leasehold minimum remaining term: 40 years (C&I) and 70 years (Interest Only).

---

# TIMBER FRAME

Acceptable with brick or block/render outer skin.

---

# TRONC (TIPS) INCOME

Accepted at 75% with sufficient evidence and track record.

---

# UNACCEPTABLE APPLICANTS

Not accepted:

Power of Attorney
Diplomatic immunity
Criminal records (except minor/spent convictions under Rehabilitation of Offenders Act)

---

# UNDERPINNED PROPERTIES

Accepted if:

10-year guarantee in place,
No sign of new movement,
Insurer willing to provide normal cover,
Solicitor confirms compliance.

---

# UNIVERSAL CREDIT

Accepted at 50%, not as main income source.

---

# UNSECURED CREDIT ARREARS

Acceptable subject to product tier limits and timeframes.

---

# VALUATION

Mortgage valuation only. Fee-free option with Fee Saver products up to £1m property value.

HomeBuyer and Building Surveys not offered.

Valuer: Esurv.

---

# OFFER VALIDITY

Standard offer valid 4 months.

New Build/Off-plan valid 6 months.

Extensions may require fresh credit and affordability checks.

---

# VALUATION APPEALS

Minimum 3 comparable properties within 1 mile sold in last 6 months required.

---

# ZERO HOURS CONTRACTS

Acceptable with 12-month track record of sustainable earnings.
