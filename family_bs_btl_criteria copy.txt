# FAMILY BUILDING SOCIETY

## BUY TO LET CRITERIA (A–Z)

**Lender:** Family Building Society
**Product:** Buy to Let
**Source:** [https://intermediaries.familybuildingsociety.co.uk/criteria/buy-to-let/a-z/](https://intermediaries.familybuildingsociety.co.uk/criteria/buy-to-let/a-z/)
**Last Verified:** Website content at time of file creation

**RAG convention:** Each criterion uses explicit labels where applicable: **Maximum:** **Minimum:** **Allowed:** **Not Acceptable:** **Condition:** so that rules are self-contained without implied context.

---

# CRITERIA 1 – ADDRESS HISTORY (MIN UK ADDRESS HISTORY NEEDED)

**Standard Acceptability:** 36 months

**Minimum:**
* 36 months (3 years) UK address history required.

**Allowed (outside policy):**
* UK expatriates abroad without a 3-year UK address history may be considered.
* Condition: Applicant must reside in an acceptable country from the approved expatriate list (refer to acceptable list of countries expatriates can be resident in on the Society’s website).

---

# CRITERIA 2 – ADVISOR OWN APPLICATION

**Standard Acceptability:** Acceptable

**Allowed:**
* Buy to Let applications from an advisor applying for their own mortgage.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society can accept such applications.

---

# CRITERIA 3 – AGE LIMITS: MAXIMUM AGE AT END OF MORTGAGE TERM

**Standard Acceptability:** 94

**Maximum:**
* 94 years at end of mortgage term (absolute maximum age we will lend to).
* 89 years at point of application (maximum age at application; at that age maximum term is 5 years).

**Condition:**
* For other ages, actual maximum term depends on age at application; refer to Maximum Mortgage Term Guidelines.
---

# CRITERIA 4 – AGE LIMITS: MAXIMUM AGE FOR SELF-EMPLOYED BORROWERS

**Standard Acceptability:** 89

**Minimum:**
* 5 years (minimum mortgage term on a BTL application).

**Maximum:**
* 90 years at point of application (effective maximum age for self-employed borrowers; standard acceptability 89 refers to this policy).

**Condition:**
* Applies to self-employed borrowers only; minimum term of 5 years determines the effective maximum age at application.

---

# CRITERIA 5 – AGE LIMITS: MINIMUM AGE AT APPLICATION

**Standard Acceptability:** 18

**Minimum:**
* 18 years at point of application (each applicant).

**Not Acceptable:**
* Applicants under 18 at application.

---

# CRITERIA 6 – APPLICANT NOT ON ELECTORAL REGISTER

**Standard Acceptability:** Acceptable

**Allowed:**
* Applicants who are not on the electoral register.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society can consider such applicants for a Buy to Let application.

---

# CRITERIA 7 – ARRANGEMENT FEES EXCEEDING LENDING LTV

**Standard Acceptability:** Not Allowed

**Not Acceptable:**
* Adding arrangement fee to the mortgage advance where this would cause the maximum lending policy LTV to be exceeded.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society does not allow fees to be added where it means the lending loan to value limits will be exceeded.

---

# CRITERIA 8 – ARRANGEMENT FEES EXCEEDING PRODUCT LTV

**Standard Acceptability:** Not Allowed

**Not Acceptable:**
* Adding arrangement fee to the mortgage advance where this would cause the product LTV to be exceeded.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society does not allow fees to be added where it means the product loan to value limits will be exceeded.

---

# CRITERIA 9 – ARRANGEMENT TO PAY

**Standard Acceptability:** Not Acceptable

**Not Acceptable:**
* Applicants that have an arrangement to pay registered on their credit file.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society will not consider such applications.

---

# CRITERIA 10 – ARREARS (SECURED / MORTGAGE)

**Standard Acceptability:** Acceptable

**Allowed:**
* Applications from applicants with history of mortgage or secured loan arrears may be considered.

**Maximum:**
* 1 month’s arrears within the last 12 months.

**Condition:**
* Any existing mortgage or secured loan must be up to date at the time of application.
* Account must be currently up to date.
* Explanation of any arrears required.

---

# CRITERIA 11 – ARREARS (UNSECURED)

**Standard Acceptability:** Acceptable

**Allowed:**
* Applications where applicant(s) have history of unsecured loan arrears may be considered.

**Maximum:**
* 1 month’s arrears within the last 12 months.

**Condition:**
* Any existing mortgage/rent/loan must be up to date at the time of application.
* Account must be currently up to date.
* Explanation of any arrears required.

---

# CRITERIA 12 – BACK TO BACK (REMORTGAGE – BRIDGING)

**Standard Acceptability:** Not Acceptable

**Not Acceptable:**
* Applications where the customer funded the purchase from a bridging loan and now wishes to mortgage the property (ownership less than 6 months).

**Condition:**
* Property must have been owned at least 6 months for such cases to be considered.

---

# CRITERIA 13 – BACK TO BACK (REMORTGAGE – CUSTOMER FUNDED)

**Standard Acceptability:** Not Acceptable

**Not Acceptable:**
* Applications where the customer funded the purchase from own funds and now wishes to mortgage the property within 6 months of ownership.

**Condition:**
* Property must have been owned at least 6 months in this situation for consideration.

---

# CRITERIA 14 – BACK TO BACK (REMORTGAGE – INHERITED)

**Standard Acceptability:** Acceptable

**Allowed:**
* Remortgages where an inherited property has been owned for less than 6 months.

**Condition:**
* Customer has inherited the property and wishes to mortgage it on a Buy to Let basis.

---

# CRITERIA 14A – REMORTGAGE PURPOSE: BALANCE SWAP (MAX LTV)

**Standard Acceptability:** 75%

**Maximum:**
* 75% LTV where remortgage purpose is a simple balance swap (no additional monies being raised).

**Condition:**
* The Society will lend up to 75% LTV; a reduced rental calculation is used for £ for £ (pound for pound) remortgages.

(Source: [BTL A–Z page 1](https://intermediaries.familybuildingsociety.co.uk/criteria/buy-to-let/a-z/1).)

---

# CRITERIA 15 – CAN CONSIDER EARNED INCOME (TOP SLICING)

**Standard Acceptability:** No

**Not Acceptable:**
* Using applicant’s earned income in addition to rental income when calculating maximum mortgage that can be borrowed.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society will not consider earned income in affordability assessment where the UK property is to be a Buy to Let.

---

# CRITERIA 16 – CAN EXTEND MORTGAGE OFFER

**Standard Acceptability:** Yes

**Allowed:**
* Mortgage offer may be extended beyond the standard expiry.

**Condition:**
* Documentation and the valuation may need to be updated if these items exceed six months old.

---

# CRITERIA 17 – USE ACTUAL RENT IF HIGHER THAN VALUER ESTIMATE

**Standard Acceptability:** No

**Not Acceptable:**
* Using the actual rent received from an existing tenant for affordability when it is higher than the valuer’s estimate.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society will not use the current rent received from the current tenant for affordability assessment when it exceeds the valuer’s estimate.

---

# CRITERIA 18 – USE VALUER ESTIMATE IF HIGHER THAN ACTUAL RENT

**Standard Acceptability:** No

**Not Acceptable:**
* Using the valuer’s rental estimate for affordability when the actual rent being received is less than the estimate.

**Condition:**
* Family Building Society will use actual rent if lower than the valuer’s estimate (e.g. on remortgage where existing tenant pays below market).

---

# CRITERIA 19–23 – CCJs

**Last 3 Months:**
* **Not Acceptable.** Family Building Society will not consider applicants that have had CCJs registered in the past 3 months on Buy to Let applications.

**3–12 Months:**
* **Not Acceptable.** Family Building Society will not consider applicants that have had CCJs registered more than 3 months ago and less than 12 months ago on Buy to Let applications.

**12–24 Months:**
* **Not Acceptable.** Family Building Society will not consider applicants that have had CCJs registered more than 12 months ago and less than 24 months ago on Buy to Let applications.

**24–36 Months:**
* **Not Acceptable.** Family Building Society will not consider applicants that have had CCJs registered more than 24 months ago and less than 36 months ago on Buy to Let applications.

**Over 36 Months:**
* **Not Ignored.** Family Building Society will not ignore any CCJs that were registered over 36 months ago on Buy to Let applications.

---

# CRITERIA 24 – COMPANY BTL AFFORDABILITY

**Standard Acceptability:** ICR based

**Definition:**
Relates to the potential rental calculation applied for company BTL.

**Condition:**
* Normal BTL ICR (interest cover ratio) requirements apply.

---

# CRITERIA 25 – CONSUMER BUY TO LET (CBTL)

**Standard Acceptability:** Acceptable

**Definition:**
Consumer Buy to Let (CBTL) is often referred to as an accidental landlord; indicates if we can lend when we consider the transaction to be a CBTL.

**Allowed:**
* Family Building Society can potentially accept applicants classed as a Consumer Buy to Let (CBTL).

---

# CRITERIA 26 – CONTRACT REASSIGNMENT

**Standard Acceptability:** Not Acceptable

**Definition:**
Purchaser buying a new build property from a third party (not the builder/developer) who has secured the property off plan and exchanged but not completed, then sells the contract to the new purchaser (sometimes for a premium, sometimes not).

**Not Acceptable:**
* Contract reassignment is not acceptable. These are not acceptable.

# CRITERIA 32–33 – DEBT MANAGEMENT PLAN

**Current / Unsatisfied:** Not Acceptable
* We do not accept applications where there is an existing and unsatisfied debt management plan.

**Satisfied:** Not Acceptable
* We do not accept applications from applicants who have had a satisfied debt management plan.

**Outside policy:**
* We could consider applicants who have had a satisfied debt management plan where this was satisfied at least three years ago. Please refer the application for further consideration.

# CRITERIA 37–41 – DEFAULTS

**Last 3 months:** Not Acceptable

**3–12 months:** Not Acceptable

**12–24 months:** Not Acceptable

**24–36 months:** Not Acceptable
